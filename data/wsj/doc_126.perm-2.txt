Dayton Power is a subsidiary of DPL Inc. and provides electric and gas service to portions of west-central Ohio.

Moody's Investor's Service Inc. upgraded this company's senior secured debt rating to single-A-1 from single-A-2 and its preferred stock rating to a1 from a2 based on the company's "continued improvement in financial performance."

The move affects $858.8 million of debt.
