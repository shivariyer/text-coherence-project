Viewlogic, which makes electronic design automation systems used by electrical engineers, reported net income of $12.4 million last year on revenue of $93.5 million.

Chronologic makes a digital simulator for the electronic design automation market.

It had net income last year of $700,000 on revenue of $4.4 million.

Viewlogic Systems Inc. said it agreed to acquire Chronologic Simulation, Los Altos, Calif., for $26.5 million of Viewlogic stock.
