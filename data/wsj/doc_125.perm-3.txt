TJ said owning closely held Pacific, which distributes computer equipment, would broaden TJ's customer base, while providing diversification.

TJ Systems Corp. said it agreed to acquire Pacific Mountain Computer Products Inc., Van Nuys, Calif.

The acquisition would increase TJ Systems' annual revenue to $20 million, or 40%, the company said.

TJ is engaged primarily in the leasing of computer and telecommunications equipment.

Terms weren't disclosed.
