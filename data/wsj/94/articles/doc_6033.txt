Gerard H. Mesnel, 56 years old, a consultant to this maker of sealing and trimming systems and other rubber and plastic products, was named to the newly created post of executive vice president of advanced technology, world-wide.
He will be responsible for product and process development as well as improving the company's sealing-systems technologies.
Also, Bruce G. Davis, former manager of materials sourcing and support operations with General Electric Corp.'s GE Transportation Systems, was named vice president of purchasing and supply, world-wide.
Mr. Davis, 45, succeeds Larry J. Enders, who was named president and chief executive officer of the company's Oliver Rubber Co. unit in July.
