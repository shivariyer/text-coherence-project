Regal Cinemas Inc. said it reached a definitive agreement to acquire 13 theaters from certain units of National Theatre Holdings Corp. for about $24.5 million.
The purchase price will be paid in cash and financed by Regal Cinemas' available cash and credit facility.
Most of the theaters are located in metropolitan Cleveland, Akron and Youngstown, Ohio.
Regal Cinemas said it expects to close the acquisition in late March.
National Theatre Holdings is based in Cleveland.
