John R. Wolf, president and chief executive officer, was named to the additional posts of chairman and chief operating officer for this maker of products used in ophthalmic surgery.
Mr. Wolf, 48 years old, succeeds LaMar Laster, 42, who resigned as chairman, executive vice president and chief operating officer for "personal reasons."
He will continue to work with Staar on a consulting basis.
No one was named to fill the position of executive vice president.
