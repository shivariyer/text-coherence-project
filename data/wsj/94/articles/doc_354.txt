Mercantile Bankshares Corp. agreed to buy Fredericksburg National Bancorp Inc. for about $41.6 million in stock.
Terms call for Mercantile to issue 2.84 common shares for each of Fredericksburg's 788,112 shares, or about 2.2 million Mercantile shares.
Mercantile Bankshares has about 49.5 million shares outstanding.
In Nasdaq Stock Market trading, Mercantile closed at $18.625, down 50 cents.
Mercantile Bankshares, a bank-holding company based in Baltimore, Md., has more than $5.5 billion in assets.
Fredericksburg National's principal banking unit, National Bank of Fredericksburg, has $236 million in assets.
