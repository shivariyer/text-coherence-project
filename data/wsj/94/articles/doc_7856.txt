Japan's household spending in November was 318,428 yen ($2,853.11) average, up 1.4% from a year earlier in real terms, the Statistics Bureau of the Prime Minister's Office said.
Real household spending takes into account inflation.
Household spending marked a year-on-year rise for the second month in a row.
In October, household spending rose 0.4%, the first year-on-year rise in six months.
