German automobile production fell an adjusted 17% in January from a year earlier, the VDA automobile association reported Friday.
The figure is adjusted to reflect 21 working days last month compared with 20 working days in January 1993, it said.
On an unadjusted basis, overall motor vehicle production skidded 12% to 274,300 units from 312,880 units a year earlier.
Car production slumped 14% to 257,800 units from 298,395 units a year earlier.
