Several executives were promoted at this provider of professional pharmacy and related consulting services for long-term care institutions.
Patrick E. Keefe, 48 years old, formerly vice president of operations, was promoted to senior vice president of operations.
Cheryl D. Hodges, 41, previously vice president and secretary, was promoted to senior vice president.
She continues as company secretary.
Thomas R. Marsh, 47, Omnicare's controller and acting treasurer, was named to the additional post of vice president.
