Freeport-McMoRan Inc. said it will buy any or all of its 10 7/8% senior subordinated debentures outstanding due May 15, 2001.
It will pay $1,090 per $1,000 principal amount, plus accrued interest from Nov. 15 through the date of purchase.
The principal amount of the notes is $125.1 million.
The purchases will be paid for by cash reserves and Freeport-McMoRan's bank credit.
Kidder, Peabody & Co. is the dealer manager for the offer.
It expires March 30.
