American Annuity Group Inc. said it extended to Feb. 8 the expiration date of its offer to purchase common shares for $11 each from holders who own 10 or fewer shares.
The previous expiration date for the tender offer was Jan. 28, the company said.
American Annuity said that as of Jan. 27, about 2,900 common shares had been tendered.
The company, through its Great American Life Insurance unit, is engaged in the sale of tax-deferred annuities to teachers and other employees of nonprofit institutions.
