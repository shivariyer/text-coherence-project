Terrence P. Brennan, 41 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of this provider of biomedical image processing and datamanagement services.
The duties Mr. Brennan assumes were previously shared by James J. Conklin, 51, who was recently named chief scientific officer and who will remain chairman, and William J. Fitzpatrick, 51, who resigns as president and chief operating officer but remains at the company through the transition.
The chief operating officer's position won't be filled.
Mr. Brennan is former president of Research Data Corp.
