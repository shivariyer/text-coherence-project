Amber's Stores Inc. said its sales rose 29% in January to $4.5 million from $3.5 million a year earlier.
The arts-and-crafts retailer's sales at stores opened for at least one year fell 12.4%.
For the year ended Jan. 31, Amber's sales rose 47% to $62 million from $42.2 million.
Same-store sales fell 7.6%.
