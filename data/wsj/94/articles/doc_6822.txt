Nichols Research Corp. said its president and chief operating officer, Lawrence R. Greenwood, asked to be "phased out" of his current positions.
Mr. Greenwood has served in the positions for two years.
He couldn't be reached for comment, and officials at Nichols didn't return phone calls.
A statement by the company said Mr. Greenwood's transition will occur during 1994, and will allow him to pursue other business interests.
The company added that Mr. Greenwood, during the transition, will continue to focus on certain areas of business development at Nichols.
Nichols provides technical services for state governments, the Department of Defense and the National Aeronautics and Space Administration.
In Nasdaq Stock Market trading yesterday, the company's stock rose 50 cents to $15.25 a share.
