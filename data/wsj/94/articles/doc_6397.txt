The American Stock Exchange fined Robert Burke, Springfield, Mass., a former branch office manager of Dean Witter Reynolds Inc., $10,000 for failing to properly supervise a broker.
Mr. Burke was also barred for six months as a branch office manager, registered options principal, or in any other supervisory position.
He didn't admit or deny charges that he failed to properly supervise a broker's improper options trading.
Mr. Burke couldn't be reached for comment.
Dean Witter is a unit of Dean Witter, Discover & Co.
Separately, the exchange barred for two years in any supervisory capacity Ninette Hereford, Riverside, Calif., a former branch office manager of Charles Schwab & Co.
She didn't admit or deny allegations of failing to supervise a registered employee's handling of options trades.
She couldn't be reached for comment.
