Twinpak Inc., a unit of Amcor Ltd. of Australia, said it agreed in principle to buy the assets of Westra Plastics Inc., a plastic-bottle maker in Plattsburgh, N.Y. Terms of the pact weren't disclosed.
A Twinpak spokesman said closely held Westra has about 50 employees and sales of between $2 million and $10 million.
Twinpak said the Plattsburgh plant will be its first in the U.S.
