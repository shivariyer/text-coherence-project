Gilles Godin was named acting chief financial officer of this maker of test equipment for digital telecommunications networks.
Mr. Godin, 34 years old, succeeds Philip J. Alford, and will remain vice president for finance.
Mr. Alford, 40, has been president of Tekelec since Jan. 15.
The company also named Philip Black to its board, increasing the board's size to eight.
Mr. Black, 39, is chief executive officer of Avalon Technologies, a closely held consulting firm in Calabasas.
