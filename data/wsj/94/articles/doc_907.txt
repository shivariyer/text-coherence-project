Morrison Knudsen Corp. said it won a $500 million, five-year contract to provide engineering, procurement and construction management services to Kennecott Utah Copper Corp.'s Bingham Canyon Copper Mine near Salt Lake City.
Work began in October 1993 and is scheduled to be complete in 1998.
The construction project is part of closely held Kennecott's mine and plant modernization program.
Morrison Knudsen is an engineering, contractor and manufacturing concern based in Boise, Idaho.
