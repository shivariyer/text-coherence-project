The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended February 11, 1994.
The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive.
Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer.
FEDERAL REALTY INVESTMENT TRUST will redeem on March 31, 1994, its 5 1/4 % convertible subordinated debentures, due 2002.
Citibank, New York, is paying agent.
FEDERAL NATIONAL MORTGAGE ASSOCIATION will redeem, on March 15, 1994, $450,000,000 principal amount of its 8.7% medium term notes, due March 15, 2001.
GREAT AMERICAN COMMUNICATIONS CO. will redeem, on Feb. 18, 1994, its 14% senior extendable PIK notes, series 1 and 2, due June 30, 2001.
Shawmut Bank Connecticut is trustee.
