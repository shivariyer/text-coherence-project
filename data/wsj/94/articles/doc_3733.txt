Chiquita Brands International Inc., Cincinnati, said it will redeem March 31 its 9 1/8% subordinated debentures due Feb. 1, 1998.
The marketer, processor and producer of food products has about $18 million of the debt outstanding.
Chiquita said the debentures will be redeemed at 100% of the par value plus accrued interest through the redemption date.
Bank of New York is the redemption agent.
