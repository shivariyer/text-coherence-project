Mirion P. Bowers and Andrea L. Van de Kamp were elected directors of the bank holding company and its principal subsidiary, City National Bank.
Dr. Bowers, 58 years old, is president and chief executive officer of the Hospital of the Good Samaritan.
Ms. Van de Kamp, 50, is senior vice president and managing director of the West Coast operations for Sotheby's Inc.
They fill vacancies created by the retirement of Stanley A. Wainer, 67, and A. Jerrold Perenchio, 63.
