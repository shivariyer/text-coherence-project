John H. Gray, 52 years old, was named chief operating officer of this closely held company that is the general partner of Leviathan Gas Partners L.P., a publicly traded natural-gas gathering concern.
He was formerly president.
Mr. Gray succeeds Donald V. Weir, 52, who resigned but who remains a director of Leviathan Gas Pipeline.
Grant E. Sims, 38, was named president of Leviathan Gas Pipeline, succeeding Mr. Gray in that job.
