Paul B. Smith, corporate senior vice president, operations, was named president of the company's Rocketdyne division.
He succeeds Robert D. Paster, 53 years old, who was appointed president of the Autonetics Electronic Systems division.
Mr. Smith, also 53, won't be succeeded immediately.
As head of Autonetics, Mr. Paster succeeds Thomas L. Gunckel II, 58, who was named senior vice president, research and engineering, for the parent.
Dr. Gunckel succeeds Robert L. Cattoi, 68, who continues as senior vice president and technical adviser to the office of the chairman until his retirement this year.
Rockwell is a diversified hightechnology concern with interests in the electronics, aerospace, automotive and graphics businesses.
