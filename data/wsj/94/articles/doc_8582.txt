Stephen Pettit was named to the newly created post of managing director, Europe, effective March 1.
Mr. Pettit joins the telecommunications company from British Petroleum Co., where he was chief executive officer, petrochemicals, with BP Chemicals.
Cable & Wireless also said Linus Cheung was named chief executive of its 57%-owned Hong Kong Telecommunications, succeeding Mike Gale, who died Tuesday.
Mr. Cheung will join Hong Kong Telecom from the Cathay Pacific Airways.
