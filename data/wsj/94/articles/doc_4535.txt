KERRIGAN DEAL: Walt Disney Co. signed a broad agreement with Olympic figure skater Nancy Kerrigan that includes a biographical TV movie for ABC, an ice-skating TV special and appearances at Disney parks, as well as a children's book.
Terms weren't disclosed.
WHO'S NEWS: Advance Publications Inc. named Thomas A. Florio, 38, president of the New Yorker magazine.
He retains the title of publisher of the New Yorker.
