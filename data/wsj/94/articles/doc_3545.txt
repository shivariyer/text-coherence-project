France's Cie. Generale des Eaux will operate telephone systems in two regions of Hungary, the company announced.
Generale des Eaux said it plans to invest 700 million francs ($120.9 million) in the telephone systems of Szeged and Szentes.
The regions now have some 64,000 telephone users out of a total population of 440,000.
Hungary currently is contracting out operation of 25 regional telephone systems, following privatization of Matav, the former Hungarian state telecommunications company.
