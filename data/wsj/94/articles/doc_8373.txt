Mining house Anglo American Corp. of South Africa said net income for its five gold mines grew 2.2% to 275.1 million rand ($80.6 million) in the fourth quarter of 1993 from 269.2 million rand in the third quarter.
Anglo American's largest mine, Free State Consolidated Gold Mines Ltd., known as Freegold, showed a 4.6% increase in net income to 127 million rand in the fourth quarter from 121.4 million rand.
