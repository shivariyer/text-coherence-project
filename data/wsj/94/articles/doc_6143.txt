Ryder System Inc., citing a strong performance in its vehicle leasing and services division, reported fourth-quarter net income rose 15%.
The Miami transportation services company said net increased to $34.2 million, or 44 cents a share, from $29.8 million, or 36 cents a share, a year earlier.
Excluding profit from discontinued operations of $3.7 million, earnings from continuing operations rose 29% to $30.5 million, or 39 cents a share, from $23.7 million, or 28 cents a share.
Revenue rose 6.3% to $1.09 billion from $1.03 billion.
Ryder noted that pretax profit from continuing operations in its vehicle leasing and services division jumped 50% on a 7% gain in revenue.
In New York Stock Exchange composite trading yesterday, Ryder was unchanged at $26.75.
