Carl J. Shields, 52 years old, was named president of Rawlings Sporting Goods, a unit of this diversified company serving the consumer, technical, industrial and service markets.
Mr. Shields succeeds James R. O'Brien, 49, who recently left the company to pursue other business opportunities.
Mr. Shields had most recently been vice president of sales and marketing at the unit.
As already reported, Figgie is considering selling all or a portion of Rawlings.
