CCP Insurance Inc. said its board authorized the repurchase of up to one million, or an indicated 3.4%, of its 29 million common shares outstanding.
The insurance concern said it considers its shares to be undervalued currently.
The timing and terms of any repurchases would be based on market conditions, the company said.
In composite trading yesterday on the New York Stock Exchange, CCP closed at $24.50 a share, up 87.5 cents.
