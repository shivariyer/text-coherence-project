Imperial Chemical Industries PLC said it sold its Indian fertilizer unit for #25 million ($37.3 million).
G.P. Goenka Group of Cos. agreed to buy the division, which is a unit of ICI India.
Additionally, ICI India will receive about #20 million in outstanding subsidy payments from the government of India.
The money will be paid over the next nine to 12 months, ICI said.
The sale is part of the British chemical company's strategy to restructure its regional operations.
