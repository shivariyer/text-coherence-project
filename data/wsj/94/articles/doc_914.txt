TransTexas Gas Corp. said it completed a natural-gas exploration well in Texas.
The natural-gas company said its Maria Cuellar No. 4 well flowed at a daily rate of 5.9 million cubic feet of natural gas from depths between 8,918 feet and 9,118 feet.
The well is in Zapata County near Laredo.
TransTexas said the well, which it owns and operates, was placed on production Feb. 14.
