D. Leonard Wise was named president and chief executive, pending confirmation of the company's reorganization plan.
Carolina Steel is operating under federal bankruptcy protection.
Mr. Wise, 59 years old, is an independent consultant and has held several senior-management and board positions in the steel industry.
A hearing on Carolina Steel's reorganization plan will be held in April.
The current chief executive, Richard J. Puricelli, 56, is serving on an interim basis and will remain with the management company of Grisanti, Galef & Goldress after Mr. Wise assumes his post.
