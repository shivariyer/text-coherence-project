French chemicals and drug group Rhone-Poulenc SA said it is setting up a joint venture with Groupe Limagrain SA in crop seeds that includes the creation of a joint holding company and a research unit.
The holding company will be divided 85.5% for Limagrain and 14.5% for Rhone-Poulenc Agro.
The unit will have sales of 1.2 billion francs ($205.3 million).
The research company will be divided equally between the two partners.
It also may be opened to third parties.
