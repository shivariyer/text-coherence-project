Navistar International Corp. said it will add a second shift of 300 workers this month at its Chatham, Ontario, plant, which makes heavy trucks.
Navistar said the move will increase truck production at the plant 30%.
The plant now has about 1,500 employees.
Navistar said it sold 35,100 heavy trucks in North America in the fiscal year ended Oct. 31, up 28.8% from 1992.
