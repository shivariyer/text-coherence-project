SUNCOR Inc. said it plans to issue 100 million Canadian dollars (US$75.6 million) of 10-year unsecured debentures.
The issue is expected to be completed in the first quarter and proceeds will be used to reduce bank debt.
Suncor said terms haven't been set.
Lead underwriter is ScotiaMcLeod Inc., a Toronto investment dealer.
Suncor is a Toronto-based oil and natural gas production, refining and marketing concern.
