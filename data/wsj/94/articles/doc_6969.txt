MC Shipping Inc., which owns and operates second-hand vessels, said it plans to acquire more vessels with proceeds from a rights offering for 3.1 million shares of common stock.
Shareholders will receive the right to buy a common share for each share held as of the record date, expected to be Feb. 9.
The subscription price is expected to be at a discount to a recent average for the shares, which have traded between $3.25 and $5.625 a share in the last month.
Shares of MC Shipping closed up 25 cents at $5.625 on more than five times average volume in composite trading on the American Stock Exchange.
