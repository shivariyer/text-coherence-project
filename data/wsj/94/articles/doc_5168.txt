Max M. Kampelman, 74 years old, was named a director of this designer and maker of simulators and military training programs, increasing the board to eight.
Mr. Kampelman, a partner at Fried, Frank, Harris, Shriver & Jacobson, a Washington, D.C., law firm, was formerly counselor of the State Department, and ambassador and head of the U.S. delegation to the negotiations on nuclear and space arms in Geneva.
