Standard & Poor's Corp. said it will remove Fedders Corp. from the S&P 500 Index after the close of trading April 5.
The ratings agency said the removal of Fedders should not be interpreted as a comment on the investment merits of Fedders securities.
The company's current market value is $172 million, ranking 499th in the 500 index.
Pacific Telesis Group is completing the spin off its cellular and paging operations.
The PacTel Corp. operations will be renamed AirTouch Communications and added to the S&P 500.
