ICF Kaiser International Inc., Fairfax, Va., said its subsidiary was awarded a $41.1 million share of three supplemental labor contracts at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory in Northern California.
The unit, Kaiser Engineers Livermore, will provide scientific, administrative and mechanical engineering expertise in a joint venture with TAD Technical Service Corp., Cambridge, Mass.
The contracts, totaling $54.8 million, will last a year, with additional one-year options.
ICF Kaiser International, formerly ICF International Inc., is an engineering, construction and consulting services concern.
