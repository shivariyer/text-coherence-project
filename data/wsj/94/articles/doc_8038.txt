France's Banque Nationale de Paris, Banque Paribas and Credit Lyonnais will direct the global placement of 66.5 million shares in the scheduled privatization of energy group Societe Nationale Elf Aquitaine, the three banks said in a joint statement.
Meanwhile, the placement of Elf shares among U.S. and Japanese investors will be lead-managed by Merrill Lynch & Co. and Daiwa Securities, respectively.
