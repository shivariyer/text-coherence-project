Norman E. Alexander was elected chairman of this coffee and food-service company.
Mr. Alexander, chairman and chief executive officer of Sequa Corp., won't assume any executive responsibilities at the company.
Mr. Alexander, 79 years old, succeeds former chairman and chief executive officer Joseph Breslin, who resigned in August to pursue other interests.
The duties of chairman and chief executive now have been divided.
Marvin I. Haas, 52, continues as chief executive officer.
