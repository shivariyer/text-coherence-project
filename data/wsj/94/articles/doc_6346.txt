United Airlines, a unit of UAL Corp., said January traffic rose 1.6% to 7.58 billion revenue passenger miles from 7.46 billion a year earlier.
A revenue passenger mile represents one paying passenger flown one mile.
Load factor, or the percentage of seats sold, rose to 63.1% from 62.8%.
The carrier's available seat miles rose 1.1% to 12.01 billion from 11.89 billion.
