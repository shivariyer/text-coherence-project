Mark Solutions Inc. said it signed a letter of intent to purchase Bilt-Rite Steel Buck Corp.
Financial terms weren't disclosed.
In a news release, Mark Solutions said Bilt-Rite in Westbury, N.Y., is a maker of hollow-metal products.
It operates four plants that produce metal-door and frame products.
The company said Bilt-Rite currently has annual sales of about $22 million.
