American International Group Inc.'s International Lease Finance Corp. and Swissair said they signed a $350 million lease contract covering 10 Airbus aircraft plus two optioned aircraft.
Swissair will lease five new technology aircraft from International Lease Finance for 10 years, and five used A-310-200 aircraft until mid-1995.
Swissair is a Zurich, Switzerland, based airline and a unit of Swiss Air Transport Co.
International Lease Finance leases and remarkets advanced technology commercial-jet aircraft to airlines, and American International is an insurance company.
