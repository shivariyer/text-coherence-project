Richard M. Rompala, 47 years old, formerly group vice president, coatings and resins, of PPG Industries Inc., was named president of this coatings manufacturer and a member of its board.
As president, Mr. Rompala succeeds Robert E. Pajor, 57, who was named to the new post of vice chairman.
The company also created an office of the chairman, which consists of Messrs. Rompala and Pajor as well as the chairman and chief executive officer, Angus Wurtele, 59.
There are now 11 board members.
