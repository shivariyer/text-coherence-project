Tech Data Corp., in a move to gain access to European markets, said it plans to acquire a French concern for about $22 million in stock.
The company, Paris-based Softmart International SA, is a major wholesale distributor of personal-computer products, according to Tech Data, with 1993 revenue of $135 million.
Tech Data didn't release earnings data for the closely held company, but said Softmart has been profitable since its inception in 1984.
Tech Data is a computer wholesaler with annual revenue of about $1.53 billion.
