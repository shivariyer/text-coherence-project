General Motors Corp. said it reached an agreement with Spartan Motors Inc. of Charlotte, Mich., to build and market motor home chassis beginning in the 1995 model year.
Under terms of the agreement, Spartan Motors will allow GM to use its technology to build a rear diesel engine motor home chassis.
In return, GM agreed to let Spartan Motors build a different rear diesel engine motor home chassis for GM.
Both will be sold as Chevrolet brands.
