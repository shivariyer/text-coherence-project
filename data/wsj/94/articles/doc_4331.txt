ACC Corp. shuffled its executive management as part of a new five-year business plan.
The telecommunications concern named 32-year-old Arunas A. Chesonis president, succeeding Richard E. Sayers, 57, who becomes ACC's vice chairman and president of its international division.
Mr. Chesonis had been president of ACC Long Distance Corp.
Michael R. Daley, 33, was named executive vice president and chief financial officer, a position that had been vacant for several years.
Mr. Daley had been vice president, finance.
Messrs. Sayers, Chesonis and Daley, along with Richard T. Aab, chairman and chief executive officer, will also make up the newly created Office of the CEO.
Mr. Aab, 44, will be chairman of the Office of the CEO.
