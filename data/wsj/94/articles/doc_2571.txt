Elected directors were Harry A. Hammerly, executive vice president, life sciences sector and international operations, of Minnesota Mining & Manufacturing Co.; D. Larry Moore, president and chief operating officer of Honeywell Inc., and R. Geoffrey P. Styles, chairman of Grosvenor International Holdings Ltd.
The men succeed John N. Lauer, D. Lee Tobler and Jon V. Heider of B.F. Goodrich Co., who left the board as a result of Goodrich's reduction of its Geon stock holdings.
Geon produces vinyl and vinyl resins.
