ARC International Corp., Toronto, expects to raise between 10 million Canadian dollars and C$12 million (US$7.4 million to US$8.9 million) from an initial public offering of a its cable-television equipment subsidiary, the company's underwriter said.
A spokesman for Research Capital Corp., the lead underwriter, said the diversified manufacturer plans to sell between 40% and 50% of the subsidiary, Cabletel Communications Corp., through the offering.
Cabletel had revenue last year of C$34 million.
ARC said proceeds will be used to reduce debt and for other corporate purposes.
