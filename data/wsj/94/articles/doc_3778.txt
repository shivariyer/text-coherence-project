Williams-Sonoma Inc. reported holiday sales of $162.7 million for the fourth quarter ended Jan. 30, up 26.2% from year-earlier sales of $129 million.
The retailer of kitchenware and other products for home and garden said comparable-store sales rose 22.5% for the 13-week period.
Mail-order sales surged 37.3% for the period, the company added.
