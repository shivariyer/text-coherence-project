Western Co. of North America said holders of about $97,784,000 aggregate principal amount of its 12 7/8% senior notes due 2002 accepted the company's tender offer.
The oil field services company said that amount met the requisite minimum for the purchase agreement.
Western said it offered to purchase the senior notes at $1,200, for each $1,000 of principal amount outstanding.
It said $100 million principal amount is outstanding.
The offer expired at midnight yesterday.
