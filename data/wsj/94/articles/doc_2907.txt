Arbor National Holdings Inc., a residential mortgage lender and servicer, said loan originations for the quarter ended Feb. 28 rose 53% to $884 million.
Loan applications rose 45% to $952 million.
Arbor said its servicing portfolio expanded 84% to $4.9 billion as of Feb. 28, from $2.7 billion in the 1993 period.
Loan originations for the fiscal year were up 68% at $4.24 billion.
Loan applications rose 80% to $5.89 billion.
