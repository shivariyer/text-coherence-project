Zurn Industries Inc. said it expects fiscal fourth-quarter operating profit for the period ending March 31 to be substantially below the operating profit of 47 cents a share reported in the year-ago period.
The Erie, Pa., company cited lower-than-expected sales of golf clubs and the combined effect of high warranty costs and low operating levels in the boiler equipment business.
Zurn makes Lynx golf equipment and systems for power generation and water quality control.
In composite New York Stock Exchange trading yesterday, Zurn shares fell 25 cents to $27.
