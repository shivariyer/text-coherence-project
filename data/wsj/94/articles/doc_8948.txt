Pittencrieff Communications Inc., Abilene, Texas, and the company's majority owner, Pittencrieff PLC of Scotland, reached an agreement under which it effectively would spin off to its shareholders its 54% stake in the publicly traded wireless communications concern.
As part of the spinoff, Pittencrieff PLC shareholders would have the option of receiving an unspecified amount of cash instead of shares in Pittencrieff Communications.
If the spinoff isn't carried out, Pittencrieff Communications has agreed to buy back the Scottish company's stake for cash.
Pittencrieff PLC's main business is oil and gas.
In Nasdaq Stock Market trading, Pittencrieff Communications fell $2.50, or 8.5%, to $26.75.
