Jean Philippe Fragrances Inc. said its Inter Parfums SA subsidiary completed the acquisition of the trademarks of Parfums Molyneux and Parfums Weil from Cosmetiques et Parfums de France I.D. SA for $3.15 million cash and 200,000 shares of Jean Philippe Fragrances common stock.
In addition, current inventory was purchased for about $2.2 million.
In Nasdaq Stock Market trading, Jean Philippe shares fell 75 cents to $11.
