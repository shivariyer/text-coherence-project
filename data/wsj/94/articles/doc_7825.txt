SHL SYSTEMHOUSE Inc., Ottawa, said it will offer between 50 million and 100 million Canadian dollars (US$38.1 million and US$76.2 million) of common shares in Canada and the U.S.
The number of shares to be offered and the price haven't been determined.
The computer services company said it will use the proceeds to finance growth and expansion.
The underwriting group will be led by Wood Gundy Inc. and Richardson Greenshields of Canada Ltd.
