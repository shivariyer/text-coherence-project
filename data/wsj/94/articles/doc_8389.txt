PacifiCare Health Systems Inc. said its PacifiCare of Washington subsidiary signed an agreement to acquire Preferred Health Resources Inc., a Seattle health-care company.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Preferred Health Resources operates Network Health Plan, a state-licensed health-care services contractor with more than 32,000 members, and Network Management Inc., a third-party administrator with more than 40,000 enrollees.
Completion of the acquisition, which is subject to regulatory approval, is expected within 45 days.
