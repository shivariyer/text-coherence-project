Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 11.9% last week to 1,667,000 tons from 1,892,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said.
Last week's output fell 5.9% from the 1,771,000 tons produced a year earlier.
The industry used 80.9% of its capability last week, compared with 91.9% the previous week and 83.8% a year ago.
The capability utilization rate is a calculation designed to indicate at what percent of its production capability the industry is operating in a given week.
