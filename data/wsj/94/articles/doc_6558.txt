Solo Serve Corp. said sales in the four-week period ended Saturday rose 1.7% to $8.7 million from $8.5 million a year earlier.
Sales at stores open more than one year decreased 8.3%.
The off-price retailer said that sales for the fiscal year ended Saturday increased 9.6% to $169.2 million from $154.4 million.
Comparable-store sales decreased 5.8%.
