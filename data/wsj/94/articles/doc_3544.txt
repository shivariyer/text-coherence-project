Fisher Scientific International Inc. said it signed a letter of intent with an affiliate of Baxter International Inc. to buy Baxter's Canlab division.
The Toronto division distributes over 35,000 laboratory products to clinical and industrial laboratories in Canada, Fisher said.
Financial terms for the cash transaction weren't disclosed.
A spokesman for Fisher, a maker of scientific instruments, said the transaction, subject to regulatory approval and a final agreement, is expected to be completed within two months.
Baxter is a Deerfield, Ill., health-care products and services concern.
