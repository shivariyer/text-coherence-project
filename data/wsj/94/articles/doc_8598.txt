PI PARENT Corp., to be renamed Plantronics Inc., said it would issue 3,588,000 common shares in an initial public offering at $12.50 a share.
Proceeds from the stock sale and a concurrent $85 million offering of senior notes will be used to repay the Santa Cruz, Calif., company's indebtedness.
Citicorp Venture Capital Ltd. will become the company's biggest stockholder, with a 36% stake.
The company makes telephone headsets.
Co-managers of the offering are Smith Barney Shearson Inc. and Prudential Securities Inc.
