National Gypsum Co. named Stephen M. Humphrey, 49 years old, as its president and chief executive officer.
He succeeds Peter C. Browning, 52, who resigned last fall to take a position with Sun Co.
Mr. Humphrey had been president-on-highway products, automotive operations, at Rockwell International Corp.
National Gypsum also recently elected C.D. Spangler, a current board member, as its nonexecutive chairman.
National Gypsum, a maker of wallboard, operates 25 manufacturing plants throughout the country.
The company emerged from Chapter 11 bankruptcycourt protection last July 1.
