SPARTA SURGICAL Corp., Pleasanton, Calif., said it filed a statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission to offer 750,000 units at $10 per unit.
Each unit would include one share of Series A convertible preferred stock and a currently undetermined number of common-stock purchase warrants.
The managing underwriter is Paulson Investment Co. of Portland, Ore.
Proceeds will be used for repayment of debt, purchase of equipment, research-and-development and marketing costs, and working capital.
Sparta develops, makes, distributes and markets medical, surgical and electrotherapy products.
