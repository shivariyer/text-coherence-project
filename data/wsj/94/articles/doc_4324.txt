National Record Mart Inc. said its board has approved the buyback of up to $1 million of its common stock.
The company said purchases will be made from time to time in the open market at its own discretion.
The company has five million common shares outstanding.
National Record Mart shares closed at $5.75, up $1, or 21%, in Nasdaq Stock Market trading yesterday.
National Record Mart is a specialty retailer of prerecorded music products including compact disks, audio and videocassettes and related accessories.
