Richard H. Sharpe, 38 years old, president and chief operating officer of this insurance concern's American Physicians Life Insurance Co. unit, was named chief operating officer of the parent company.
The appointment is effective immediately.
Mr. Sharpe fills a position vacated several years ago.
He will be responsible for the daily operations of the company, and all senior management will report to him.
Mr. Sharpe will continue as president and chief operating officer of the American Physicians Life unit.
