Tyson Foods Inc., Springdale, Ark., launched its previously announced hostile tender offer to acquire WLR Foods Inc. for $30 a share, valuing the chicken turkey processor at $330 million.
WLR, based in Hinton, Va., has rebuffed recent overtures from Tyson, the nation's largest poultry processor.
The tender offer expires April 8.
In Nasdaq Stock Market trading, WLR closed at $30.875, up 25 cents.
In New York Stock Exchange composite trading, Tyson was unchanged, at $20.50.
