Ernest D. Stein, 54 years old, was named executive vice president and general counsel, succeeding William Baronoff, who becomes executive vice president and special counsel to the president, a new position.
Mr. Stein was deputy general counsel at CIT, a $14 billion asset-based lender.
CIT is 60%-owned by Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank Ltd., Tokyo, and 40% owned by New York's Chemical Banking Corp.
