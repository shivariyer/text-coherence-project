Mexican truck and bus maker Consorcio G Grupo Dina SA and Motor Coach Industries International Inc. of Phoenix signed a purchase agreement.
Dina will acquire all of the shares outstanding of Motor Coach in exchange for a new class of Dina stock.
Dina will exchange American depositary shares worth $16.72 each in L series stock for each share of Motor Coach common stock.
At the exchange price, Dina is paying a premium of nearly 30% for the Motor Coach stock.
The deal is valued at $337 million, Dina said.
The merger is expected to be closed in the second quarter.
