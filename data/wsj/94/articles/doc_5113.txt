Price/Costco Inc. said sales for the four weeks ended Feb. 13 rose 8% to $1.13 billion from $1.05 billion for the year-earlier period.
Same-store sales declined 1%.
The company said same-store sales were hurt by the Jan. 17 earthquake in California and bad weather in the Northeast.
Year-to-date sales, for the 24 weeks ended Feb. 13, rose 6% to $7.61 billion from $7.16 billion.
Same-store sales slipped 3%.
