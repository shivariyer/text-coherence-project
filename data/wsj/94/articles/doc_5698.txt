Adac Laboratories said it acquired the nuclear-medicine patent portfolio of Philips Medical Systems, a unit of Philips Electronics N.V. Terms weren't disclosed.
The company said the portfolio consists of 13 U.S. patents and 56 foreign patents and patent applications.
Adac said Philips's divestment of its nuclear-medicine portfolio is consistent with its "long-term strategic plan to streamline its product offerings."
