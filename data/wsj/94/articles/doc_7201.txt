Clarence P. Cazalot Jr., president of Texaco's Latin America/West Africa Division, will become president of Texaco Exploration and Production Inc.
Mr. Cazalot, 43 years old, will succeed Earl L. Johnson, 63, who is retiring after 41 years with the company.
Patrick J. Lynch, 56, vice president and comptroller of Texaco, will become president of the company's Latin America/West Africa Division.
Robert Oelkers, 49, senior vice president of the company's refining and marketing unit, has been elected comptroller, succeeding Mr. Lynch.
Lester A. Amidei Jr., 49, general manager of corporate planning and economics, will assume Mr. Oelkers's post.
All the changes take effect March 1.
