William H. "Bill" Grigg, 61 years old, was named chairman, president and chief executive of Duke Power Co.
The appointment of Mr. Grigg, who has been vice chairman of the large electric utility since 1991, was expected.
The move is effective April 28.
Mr. Grigg succeeds William S. "Bill" Lee, who has led Duke Power for 12 years.
Mr. Lee, 64, will serve as a consultant to the company until his retirement on June 30.
A former Marine with undergraduate and law degrees from Duke University in Durham, N.C., Mr. Grigg joined Duke Power in 1963 as assistant general counsel and has served on the company's board since 1972.
Before being named vice chairman, Mr. Grigg was executive vice president, customer group.
