Roger Peirce was named president of the new electronic funds services unit of this information-processing company.
The unit combines the point-of-sale, oil and gas, debit and private label transaction card businesses.
Mr. Peirce, 52, was executive vice president at credit card association Visa International, responsible for world-wide delivery systems.
In San Francisco, Visa said William L. Chenevich, 50, succeeds Mr. Peirce.
Mr. Chenevich, who joined Visa in October, was supervising development of new credit, debit, and risk control services at the VisaNet strategic unit.
