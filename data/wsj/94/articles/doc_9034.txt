United Kingdom publisher Pearson PLC said the underwriters of its recent flotation of 55% of Camco International, its oil-services unit, have exercised their option to buy 3.6% of Camco's issued share capital.
Pearson said it will receive another #8.5 million ($12.7 million) through the underwriters' purchase.
In November, Pearson announced it would sell 55% of Camco as a first step in selling all of the company.
It received $15 a share.
Camco is traded on the New York Stock Exchange; Pearson owns 41.4% of Camco.
