Walter Hayes, 69, will retire Tuesday as chairman of Ford's Aston Martin Lagonda Group.
Ford acquired a majority stake in Aston Martin in 1987.
Mr. Hayes joined Aston Martin's board in 1990 and became chairman a year later.
Ford said Mr. Hayes will continue as a nonexecutive director.
John Oldfield, 57, vice president of product programs, vehicle engineering and design for Ford of Europe, will succeed Mr. Hayes.
Succeeding Mr. Oldfield is Richard Parry-Jones, 42, chief engineer of vehicle engineering for Ford of Europe.
