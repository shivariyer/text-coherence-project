DURIRON Co., Dayton, Ohio, raised its quarterly dividend to 15 3/4 cents from 15 cents, payable March 11 to shareholders of record Feb. 25.
The maker of pumps and valves also announced a 3-for-2 stock split payable March 25 to holders of record Feb. 25.
On a post-split basis, the cash dividend was increased to 10.5 cents a share from 10 cents a share.
Duriron currently has 12.6 million shares outstanding.
