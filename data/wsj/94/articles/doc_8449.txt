Electronic Arts Inc., San Mateo, Calif., said fiscal third-quarter net income leaped 47% as sales of its popular computer games soared.
The company earned $18.1 million, or 36 cents a share, in the period ended Dec. 31, compared with $12.3 million, or 26 cents a share, a year earlier.
Sales rose 40% to $149.5 million in the quarter from $107.1 million a year earlier.
Earnings were in line with expectations on Wall Street.
The company's shares fell 25 cents to $29 in Nasdaq Stock Market trading.
