This British pharmaceutical company confirmed John Robb as chairman and chief executive.
Mr. Robb was appointed temporarily to the jobs in August after Sir Alistair Frame retired as chairman.
Meanwhile, Sir Anthony Tennant joined the Wellcome board as deputy chairman.
Sir Anthony also is chairman of the United Kingdom auction house Christie's International as well as a nonexecutive director at leisure company Forte PLC and insurance concern Guardian Royal Exchange PLC.
