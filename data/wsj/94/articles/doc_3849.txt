Public Storage Properties VIII Inc., Glendale, Calif., said Storage Equities Inc. made an offer to acquire Public Storage Properties VIII for about $58 million in cash or stock.
Under the proposed offer, each outstanding share of Public Storage Properties VIII common stock would be converted into either Storage Equities common shares, or $21.94 in cash for up to 50% of Public Storage Properties VIII's 2.6 million common shares.
The arrangement is subject to approval by the Securities and Exchange Commission and shareholders of both companies.
Public Storage Properties VIII is a real-estate investment trust that owns miniwarehouse self-storage facilities, and Storage Equities is a real-estate investment partnership in Glendale.
In composite trading yesterday on the New York Stock Exchange, Storage Equities closed at $14.875, up 37.5 cents.
In composite trading on the American Stock Exchange, Public Storage closed at $20, up 37.5 cents.
