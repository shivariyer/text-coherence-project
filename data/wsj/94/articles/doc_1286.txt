Clifford A. Miller and C. Austin Fitts were named directors of this former bank holding company and its First American Bankshares Inc. unit, expanding the board to seven members.
In June 1993, both companies sold their remaining operations to First Union Corp.
They are continuing to liquidate remaining assets.
Mr. Miller is chairman of the Clifford Group Inc., a Los Angeles-based business consulting firm, and a senior consultant to Shamrock Holdings Inc., an investment holding company.
Ms. Fitts is managing director of the Hamilton Securities Group Inc., an investment bank based in Washington, D.C.
