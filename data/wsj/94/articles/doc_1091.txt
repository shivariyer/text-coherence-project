National Semiconductor Corp. said it will redeem its $4 depositary convertible exchangeable preferred shares through its depositary, First Bank of Boston.
The redemption price will be $51.37 a depositary share, including 57 cents of accrued dividends from March 1 to April 21, 1994.
The holder has the option of exchanging the shares for 3.3 shares of National Semiconductor common.
