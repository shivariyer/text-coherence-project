ARKLA Inc., Houston, said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a public offering of 13 million common shares.
Arkla shares rose 12.5 cents to $7.25 in New York Stock Exchange composite trading.
The natural-gas company said it would use proceeds to retire long-term debt.
Lead manager for the offering in the U.S. is Merrill Lynch & Co.
The offering is expected to occur in the last week of April or the first week of May.
The registration statement also covers an overallotment option of 1.95 million shares.
