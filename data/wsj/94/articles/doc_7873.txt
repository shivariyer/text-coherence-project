USA Networks International, a division of USA Networks, said it will launch a 24-hour, Spanish-language general entertainment cable-television network in Latin America in April.
USA, a 50-50 joint venture of Paramount Communications Inc., New York, and Matsushita Electric Industrial Co.'s MCA Inc., Los Angeles, said Paramount and MCA's Universal studios will supply programming, in addition to USA's own programming.
The company is working with operators and program distributors, including Multivision of Mexico, Produfe of Argentina and Cablevision of Venezuela, to carry and distribute the programming.
