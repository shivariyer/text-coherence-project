A. Stewart Hall Jr. was elected president and chief operating officer, as well as a director, of this distributor of electrical, plumbing and building materials.
Mr. Hall, 51 years old, who was executive vice president, succeeds David Hughes, 50, who remains chairman and chief executive officer.
Mr. Hughes said the moves were part of an "orderly transition."
The company also named John D. Baker II a director.
Mr. Baker, 45, is president of Florida Rock Industries Inc., Jacksonville, Fla.
Messrs. Baker and Hall increase the size of the board to 10 members.
