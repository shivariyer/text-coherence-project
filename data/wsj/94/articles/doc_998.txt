Sherritt Inc. closed an agreement to purchase most of the fertilizer assets of Imperial Oil Ltd. for about 385 million Canadian dollars (US$282.2 million).
The Fort Saskatchewan, Alberta, mining, chemicals and advanced materials company said the principal assets it agreed to buy are Imperial's fertilizer production plant at Redwater, Alberta, and distribution terminals in Manitoba and Saskatchewan.
Imperial, based in Toronto, is Canada's largest petroleum company.
It is 69.6%-owned by Exxon Corp.
