Daewoo Heavy Industries Ltd. said it secured a controlling stake in Namyang Metals Co., a maker of castings for auto engines, by purchasing 800,000 shares, or a 21.04% equity.
Daewoo Heavy purchased the stake for 4.6 billion won ($5.7 million) to secure a smooth supply of castings used for diesel and car engines for its sister company, Daewoo Motor Co.
