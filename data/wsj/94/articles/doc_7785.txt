Bio-Vascular Inc. said it entered into a definitive agreement to acquire Vital Images Inc., a closely held Fairfield, Iowa, concern.
The agreement calls for Bio-Vascular, a maker of medical devices, to exchange one share for each share of Vital Images common and Series A convertible preferred shares outstanding.
Completion is subject to certain conditions, including the availability of pooling of interests accounting, and approval by Vital Images holders.
Vital Images had sales of $1.3 million for the 10 months ended Oct. 31, and reported a loss of $776,000, of which $678,000 was attributed to research and development expenses.
