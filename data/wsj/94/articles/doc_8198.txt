Mark C. Gray, 45 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of this digital electronic graphics equipment and systems maker.
Mr. Gray, formerly executive vice president and general manager at Sony Corp.'s business and professional group, succeeds Alfred O.P. Leubert, 71, who resigned last year.
Chyron Vice Chairman Peter J. Lance, 57, had been acting president and chief operating officer following Mr. Leubert's resignation in March.
Chyron is 69%-owned by Pesa Inc., a unit of Madrid-based Pesa Electronica SA, a broadcast electronic company.
