Alpine Lace Brands Inc. said it sold 65% of its Mountain Farms subsidiary in Logan, Utah, to J.R. Simplot Co., a private conglomerate based in Boise, Idaho.
Alpine Lace, a maker and distributor of cheese, said terms of the sale will be disclosed within 15 days in a Form 8-K filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission.
The company said proceeds from the sale will be used to reduce debt.
