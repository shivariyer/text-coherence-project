Comair Holdings Inc., the parent company of airline Comair, said passenger boardings for February were up 21.7% to 220,490, from the year-earlier level of 181,196.
Revenue passenger miles, a measure of one passenger flying one mile, increased 41.2% to 59.3 million from the year-earlier 42 million.
The load factor -- the percentage of available seat miles sold to paying passengers -- was 46.3%, up from 45.5%.
