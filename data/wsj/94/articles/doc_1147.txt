GaSonics International Corp. said it sold 2.2 million common shares at $13 each during its initial public offering.
Of the total, 1,350,000 shares were sold by the company and 850,000 by selling holders.
In addition, underwriters have an option to purchase as many as 330,000 additional shares to cover any overallotments.
After the offering, the company has 8,065,000 shares outstanding.
The offering was co-managed by Montgomery Securities and Hambrecht & Quist Inc.
Proceeds will be used to repay debt and for working capital and general corporate purposes.
GaSonics develops and supplies equipment used to fabricate advanced integrated circuits.
