TELEGLOBE Inc., Montreal, said it will issue C$125 million (US$92.5 million) of seven-year, 5.4% cumulative, redeemable preferred shares.
The public offering consists of five million shares priced at C$25 each.
RBC Dominion Securities Inc. and Gordon Capital Corp. are lead underwriters.
Teleglobe, a Montreal-based telecommunications company, said it will use net proceeds for capital expenditures and general corporate purposes.
Pending such use, the proceeds may be used to reduce outstanding borrowings, it said.
