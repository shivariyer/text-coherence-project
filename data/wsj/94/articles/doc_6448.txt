J. Clayburn La Force Jr., 65 years old, emeritus dean and professor at the John E. Anderson Graduate School of Management at the University of California, Los Angeles, was elected a director of this maker of highly engineered bearings and alloy steels.
He succeeds Richard A. Gulling, a former vice president of finance and a director since 1976, who retired from the board in compliance with its retirement policy.
Timken's board remains at 12 members.
