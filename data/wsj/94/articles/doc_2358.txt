Portugal's consumer price index rose 0.6% in February, allowing the year-to-year rate to drop to 6.1% from 6.3% in January, the national statistics institute announced.
The largest drops were seen in transport and communications prices, which rose 1.2% in February compared with a 2% increase in January, and in the household goods sector, which saw growth rates ease to a 0.2% pace from a 0.9% rate.
