America West Airlines affirmed its previous selection of a $220 million bid led by David Bonderman and James Coulter, Fort Worth, Texas, investors who are major shareholders in Continental Airlines.
The decision, which was expected, effectively rejects a recently sweetened offer of equal value from an investment group led by New York money manager Michael Steinhardt.
America West, of Phoenix, has been operating under Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law protection for two and a half years.
