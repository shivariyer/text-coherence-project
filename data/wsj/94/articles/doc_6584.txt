John C. Appel, 45 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of the Dain Bosworth Inc. unit of this brokerage and investment banking concern.
As president, Mr. Appel, formerly executive vice president and chief financial officer of both the parent and Dain Bosworth, succeeds Irving Weiser, 46, who continues as chairman and chief executive officer of the unit as well as president and chief executive officer of the parent company.
Mr. Appel continues as an executive vice president of Inter-Regional Financial.
