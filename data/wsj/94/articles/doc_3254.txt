Bed Bath & Beyond Inc. said sales for the five-week February sales period totaled $28 million, up 35% from a year earlier, while sales at stores open at least a year rose 9.2%.
The retailer of domestic merchandise and home furnishings said sales for the fourth fiscal quarter ended Feb. 27 rose 36% to $87.8 million from $64.5 million, and comparable store sales for the quarter rose 10%.
And for the fiscal year, sales rose 41% to $305.8 million from $216.7 million, while sales at stores open at least a year more than doubled.
