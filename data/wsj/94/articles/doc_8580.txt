Don H. Davis was named an executive vice president and chief operating officer.
Mr. Davis will share that title with Kent M. Black, who has served in the post since 1989.
Mr. Davis, 54 years old, was president of Rockwell's Allen-Bradley industrial automation unit in Milwaukee.
He will be succeeded as president by Jodie K. Glore, 46, the unit's senior vice president.
Rockwell also has interests in the space industry, electronics and printing.
