The oil-field services concern named Terry Doyle, 49 years old, president and chief operating officer, effective April 1.
Beta said Mr. Doyle has been a consultant in Houston since November 1993.
As president, he will succeed William Gordica, 61, who remains chairman and chief executive officer.
As chief operating officer, he will succeed Robert Dobson, 49, who was named executive vice president and chief financial officer.
