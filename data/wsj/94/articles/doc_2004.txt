Shareholders of Columbia Real Estate Investments Inc. approved a plan to dissolve and liquidate the company.
David J. Gallitano, chairman, said the first distribution is expected in the next 60 to 90 days, with a final distribution of the remaining proceeds, after allowances for expenses, within 12 months.
A spokeswoman put the estimated value of the dissolution at $9 to $9.25 a share.
In American Stock Exchange trading yesterday, Columbia shares closed at $8.50, down 12.5 cents a share.
The Columbia, Md., company invests in residential mortgages and other mortgage derivative products.
