Western Co. of North America said it increased the price it will pay for its 12 7/8% senior notes due 2002 to $1,200 from $1,190 for each $1,000 of principal outstanding.
The oil field services company also said the amended offer will expire March 1.
Western said $28,245,000 of the $100 million principal amount outstanding had been tendered thus far.
J.P. Morgan Securities Inc. is managing the offer.
