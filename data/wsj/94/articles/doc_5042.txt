Paper and packaging maker CSS Industries Inc. said it's in talks to sell its Ellisco Inc. unit to U.S.
Can Corp. of Oak Brook, Ill.
The Ellisco unit makes and sells specialty metal containers, among other products.
CSS said the sale would generate about $33 million, including proceeds from the sale of stock and a dividend by Ellisco before the sale.
The transaction is subject to the availability of financing and approval by the companies' boards, among other conditions.
