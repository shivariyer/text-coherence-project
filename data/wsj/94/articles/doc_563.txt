The nation's index of producer and import prices fell 0.4% in February from a year earlier but rose 0.1% from January, the federal statistics office said.
In January, the index had declined 0.5% year-to-year and decreased 0.2% month-to-month.
In February of last year, the index edged up 0.2% year-to-year and was unchanged month-to-month.
