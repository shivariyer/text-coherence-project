Joseph J. Whiteside, 52 years old, managing director of Cornerstone Capital Advisors Ltd., Pittsburgh, was named executive vice president and chief financial officer of this financial services concern.
He succeeds Eric Booth, 49, who held the dual titles of Michigan National's chief financial officer and chairman of its mortgage subsidiary, Independence One Mortgage Corp.
Mr. Booth recently assumed the additional title of president of the mortgage subsidiary and will continue as its chairman.
