Paul M. Anderson and Charlotte Temple were elected to the board of this wood-products and financial-services concern.
One of the directors took the seat of Arthur Temple, 73 years old, who had retained a position on the board after stepping down as chairman.
The other director was named to a new seat, expanding the board to 10 members.
Mr. Anderson, 49, is president of Panhandle Eastern Corp.
Ms. Temple, 53, is a private investor.
