John Rallis, a senior vice president, was named president and chief operating officer of this foam-products manufacturer.
Mr. Rallis, 56 years old, formerly served as chairman and chief executive of Great Western Foam Products Corp. prior to its acquisition by Foamex last year.
He will succeed William H. Bundy, 53, who will continue as a senior manager and board member.
In addition, Foamex Chairman Marshall S. Cogan, 56, will assume the newly created title of chief executive.
