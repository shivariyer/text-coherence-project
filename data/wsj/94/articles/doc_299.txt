Norwood Promotional Products Inc. agreed to acquire ArtMold Products Corp., Cranston, R.I. Terms weren't disclosed.
Manufactured Home Communities Inc. paid $8 million to acquire Northlake, a 435-site, 57-acre manufactured housing community in Thornton, Colo., from Carlsberg Management Co.
Bertelsmann AG's BMG International unit agreed to act as international distributor of the multimedia software products published by Crystal Dynamics Inc.
Using its home video and music distribution networks outside the U.S., BMG will launch a BMG Multimedia unit, its first foray into interactive software.
Coors Brewing Co., a unit of Adolph Coors Co., completed the purchase of a brewery in Zaragoza, Spain, from El Aguila SA, Madrid.
Terms of the pact weren't disclosed.
