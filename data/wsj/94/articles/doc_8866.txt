CMS Energy Corp.'s independent power unit, CMS Generation Co., is building a $420 million lignite-fired energy plant in India to provide 250 megawatts of electrical power to the state of Tamil Nadu.
Construction is expected to begin later this year, with commercial operation to start in 1997.
CMS Generation Co. will be the project manager and plant operator and will hold a 40% ownership interest.
Other partners in the project include: S.T. Power Systems, Rockville, Md., and Neyveli Lignite Corp., Neyveli, India.
The International Finance Corp., Washington, D.C., is also expected to participate in the project.
