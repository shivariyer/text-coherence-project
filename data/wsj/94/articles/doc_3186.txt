YORK INTERNATIONAL Corp. doubled its quarterly dividend to four cents a share, payable March 25 to shares of record March 15.
York said the new rate boosts the indicated annual dividend rate to 16 cents a share from eight cents.
The increase is "a sign of continued confidence about our prospects," the York, Pa., company said.
York, which makes heating, ventilating and air-conditioning products, has annual revenue of about $2 billion.
