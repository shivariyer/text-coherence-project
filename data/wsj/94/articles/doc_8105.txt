Tiger Management Corp. and General Electric Co.'s GE Investments agreed to make a combined $225 million investment in Tiger Management's Tiger International Fund.
Neither company was more specific about their respective investments in the $1 billion fund that targets Asia and other emerging markets.
GE Investments also will join the fund's investment committee and make personnel available in Hong Kong and elsewhere to help in evaluating potential investments.
Tiger Management, a global investment firm, manages about $7.5 billion in assets.
GE Investments manages about $47 billion in assets, including more than $26 billion from the GE pension trust.
