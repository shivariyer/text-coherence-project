Applied Extrusion Technologies Inc. said it entered into a letter of intent to acquire the Packaging Film Group of Hercules Inc. for more than $160 million.
Applied Extrusion, a Salem, Mass.-based plastics products maker, said it plans to finance the transaction with a combination of debt and equity securities.
It wasn't more specific.
Hercules, Wilmington, Del., is a chemical concern.
The packaging film group has annual sales of more than $160 million, Applied Extrusion said.
