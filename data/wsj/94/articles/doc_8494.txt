Sybase Inc., Emeryville, Calif., said net income climbed 64% in the fourth quarter on a 66% sales gain amid robust demand for its network software and services.
The company earned $18 million, or 34 cents a share, compared with $11 million, or 21 cents a share, a year ago.
Sales rose to $138 million from $83 million.
The results were slightly higher than analysts' expectations of 31 cents a share, according to Zacks Investment Research Inc.
The company said revenue growth was expecially strong from its on-line services and from licensing of its products.
For 1993, Sybase earnings rose 86% to $44 million, or 86 cents a share, from $24 million, or 48 cents a share.
Sales rose 61% to $427 million from $265 million.
In Nasdaq Stock Market trading yesterday, Sybase stock closed at $45, unchanged.
