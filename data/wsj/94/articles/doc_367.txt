Tyco International Ltd. said that Tyco Investments Australia Ltd., Sydney, plans to distribute its 10% stake in Tyco to Tyco Investments shareholders on a proportional basis.
Tyco Investments, formerly Wormald International Ltd., received the 4.7 million shares of Tyco common and a warrant in connection with the sale of its world-wide fire protection business to Tyco in 1990.
Tyco International, of Exeter, N.H., said distribution of Tyco Investments' shares is subject to shareholder votes and Australian court approvals.
A spokesman for Tyco International said Tyco Investments is primarily held by institutional investors with the Tyco stake representing most of its assets.
In late trading on the New York Stock Exchange, Tyco International was quoted at $50, down $1.875.
