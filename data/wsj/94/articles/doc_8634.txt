Great Bay Bankshares Inc. bought back 5,000 common shares under the stock buyback program authorized by its board in October.
In a news release, the company said a total of 75,000 shares has been bought under the current program at a weighted average price of $12.11 a share.
A spokesman said the company may buy back an additional 196,255 common shares according to the stock buyback program.
Great Bay's stock closed yesterday at $12.625 a share, up 37.5 cents, on the Nasdaq Stock Market.
