STATESMAN GROUP Inc., Des Moines, Iowa, declared a dividend of 10 cents on common stock, pay Feb. 14 to record Jan. 31.
The financial services holding company, which doesn't have a regular dividend, said the special payout recognizes the company's accomplishments and "reflects the confidence of our board of directors in the company's financial condition and future earnings prospects."
The company's last dividend was 5% in stock paid in December.
Statesman also said its board authorized the repurchase of up to one million common shares, or about 7.5%, of its approximately 13.4 million shares outstanding.
The company said the purchase program will be in effect for the remainder of 1994.
Shares acquired under the program will be used for general corporate purposes, the company said.
