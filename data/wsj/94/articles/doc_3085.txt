H.J. Heinz Co. said Michael R. McGrath, 39 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of the company's Weight Watchers Food Co. unit.
Mr. McGrath had been vice president of marketing at All American Gourmet Co., a unit of Philip Morris Cos.'
Kraft/General Foods, and had responsibility for marketing Budget Gourmet and the former Birds Eye brand.
He succeeds Brian J. Ruder, 39, who, as expected, was named to the new position of corporate vice president and area director in charge of implementing Heinz's worldwide baby-food strategy.
