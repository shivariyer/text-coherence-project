Aris Melissaratos, 50 years old, was named vice president of science, technology and quality at this electrical equipment, power generation and broadcasting company.
He succeeds Francis J. Harvey, 50, formerly vice president of science and technology, who in late January became president of the recently created Westinghouse Government & Environmental Services Co. unit.
Mr. Melissaratos previously had the title of vice president of corporate productivity and quality, and he retains those duties in his new position.
Mr. Melissaratos will have overall responsibility for the company's science and technology operations as well as its quality and performance improvement programs.
He will direct the company's Westinghouse Science & Technology Center and its Productivity and Quality Center, both located near Pittsburgh.
