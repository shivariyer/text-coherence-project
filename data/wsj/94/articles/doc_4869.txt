Health Care & Retirement Corp. and Cincinnati-based Omnicare Inc., a provider of pharmacy services for long-term care facilities, formed a strategic partnership aimed at developing an integrated service network.
Under terms of the agreement, each company will make equal capital contributions to the 50-50 partnership, which will be called Heartland Healthcare Services.
The partnership agreement has a 15-year minimum term.
Health Care & Retirement, a provider of long-term care, operates 130 nursing centers with more than 16,500 beds in 16 states.
Omnicare serves more than 100,000 residents in 1,200 nursing facilities in nine states.
