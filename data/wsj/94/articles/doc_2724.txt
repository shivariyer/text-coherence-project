The Treasury plans to raise $725 million in new cash with the sale Monday of about $25.20 billion in short-term bills.
Maturing bills outstanding total $24.47 billion.
The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills, which will be dated March 17 and will mature June 16 and Sept. 15, respectively.
The Cusip number for the three-month bills is 912794L28.
The Cusip number for the six-month bills is 912794N34.
Noncompetitive tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by noon EST Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders for the bills must be received by 1 p.m. EST.
