Andreas M. Georghiou, 44 years old, was named vice president, commercial business services, at GE American Communications Inc., a new position.
Mr. Georghiou was previously director, broadcast services, at the unit.
Also, Charles Castine, 44, was named vice president, consumer service, at the GE Appliances unit.
Mr. Castine succeeds David Cote, recently named vice president and general manager of GE Silicones.
GE is an electrical and industrial products, financial services and broadcasting company.
