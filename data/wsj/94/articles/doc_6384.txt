Peter B. McKee was named senior vice president and chief financial officer of this wholesale drug distributor.
Mr. McKee, 55 years old, was a partner at InterSolve Group Inc., a Dallas management consulting firm.
At FoxMeyer, he succeeds Dennis Letham, who resigned in July.
FoxMeyer also named William L. Estes senior vice president, operations.
Mr. Estes, 46, was chief operating officer for the U.S. unit of Body Shop International PLC.
FoxMeyer didn't disclose whom Mr. Estes succeeded.
