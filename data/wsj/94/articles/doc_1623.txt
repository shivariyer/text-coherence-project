Money-market mutual fund assets rose $4.41 billion to $598.19 billion from a revised $593.78 billion for the week ended Wednesday, the Investment Company Institute said.
Assets of 658 retail money-market mutual funds rose by $1.84 billion to $413.22 billion, the trade group said.
Among retail funds, assets of 408 taxable money-market funds rose $1.42 billion to $319.13 billion.
Assets of 250 retail tax-exempt funds increased by $421.1 million to $94.09 billion.
Assets of the 284 institutional money-market funds rose $2.57 billion to $184.97 billion.
Among institutional funds, the assets of 230 taxable money-market funds rose by $2.65 billion to $161.06 billion.
Assets of 54 tax-exempt funds decreased by $71.8 million to $23.91 billion.
