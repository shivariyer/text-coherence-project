Pete Rozelle and Kenneth Hamlet were elected directors of this programmer and broadcaster of interactive entertainment programming.
They succeed Ronald E. Hogan and Michael J. Downs, who resigned from the board but remain officers of the company.
Mr. Hogan, 54 years old, is senior vice president of finance, chief financial officer and secretary; Mr. Downs, 60, is vice president and director of education.
Mr. Rozelle, 68, a commissioner of the National Football League from 1960 to 1989, acts on a consulting basis for the NFL.
Mr. Hamlet, 49, was president and chief executive officer of Holiday Inns Inc. from 1980 to 1990 and is owner and partner of NKI Hospitality Hotel/Investment Management.
