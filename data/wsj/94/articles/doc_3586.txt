John A. Saxton, 45 years old, vice president, cosmetic and fragrance products, was named vice president, national government relations, for the company as of April 4.
Mr. Saxton, who has been with P&G since 1971, will succeed R. Marvin Womack, 58, who will retire July 1 after 35 years with P&G. Beth J. Kaplan, 36, currently general manager of olestra, shortening and oils, will succeed Mr. Saxton.
