ITT Corp. said the previously announced spinoff of its ITT Rayonier timber and forest products unit would be distributed Feb. 28 to stock of record Feb. 24.
ITT shareholders will receive one Rayonier Inc. share for each four shares held.
Holders of Series N preferred stock will receive one Rayonier share for each 3.159 shares held.
Rayonier Inc. will trade on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol RYN.
ITT also has interests in insurance, financial services, automotive parts, hotels and defense.
