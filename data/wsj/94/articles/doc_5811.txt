This weekend commences the Winter Olympics in Lillehammer.
Even life forms on the moon, of course, will be tuned in for the Skate-off of the Century, but one has to wonder about how the rest of this snow-covered sport will play in ice-weary America, where even the Great Lakes are almost frozen over.
All those homebound with legs cracked on slick ice may spend hours with white Norway, but some of the rest of us may prefer daydreaming about summers, sun and dry pavement long forgotten.
