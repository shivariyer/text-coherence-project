William M. McDonough, 37 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of this consumer software company.
He was previously senior vice president and chief financial officer.
He succeeds Ed Auer, 61, who set his retirement plans last October.
After the completion of the previously announced acquisition of Broderbund by Electronic Arts Inc., Mr. McDonough will report to Electronic Arts' president and chief executive officer, Doug Carlston.
