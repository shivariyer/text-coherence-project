Craig S. Phillips, 38 years old, was named a managing director and head of the mortgage-backed securities department at the investment banking company.
He also will manage a joint venture with the real-estate department concentrating on securitization of commercial mortgages.
Mr. Phillips had been a managing director and co-head of the mortgage department at First Boston Inc.
He succeeds Charles Johnston, who continues to head mortgage-backed securities trading and risk management.
