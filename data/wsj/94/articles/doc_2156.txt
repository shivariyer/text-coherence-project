Sir Colin M. Marshall, British Airways PLC chairman and chief executive officer, and Clifton Wharton Jr., a director of Ford Motor Co. and former deputy secretary of state, were nominated to become directors of this stock exchange representing the public.
Deryck C. Maughan, chairman and CEO of Salomon Inc.'s Salomon Brothers unit, was nominated to represent the securities industries.
Directors are elected for two-year terms on the board, composed of 12 public members, 12 industry members and two officers from the exchange.
