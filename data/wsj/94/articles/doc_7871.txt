EAB, a banking company, reported fourth-quarter earnings of $17.4 million, up 1.2% from the year earlier.
Profit for all of 1993 climbed 22% to $67.1 million.
EAB, which has assets of $6 billion, is a unit of ABN Amro Bank N.V., the Dutch banking concern.
Earnings were helped by a decline in the provision for loan losses and a pickup in other revenue, such as service charges.
Net interest income declined slightly in the quarter and rose a bit for the full year.
