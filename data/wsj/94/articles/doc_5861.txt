Harold's Stores Inc. said sales for its fiscal year ended Jan. 29 increased 23.7%, to $60.9 million from $49.3 million a year earlier.
The men's and ladies' apparel retailer said sales in stores open a year or more rose 7.4%, to $49.5 million from $46.1 million the previous year.
The company said catalog sales leapt 68.6%, to $5.4 million from $3.2 million.
