French municipal services group Cie. Generale des Eaux SA said its holding in media group Havas SA has climbed to 2.5% following the purchase of a second large block of shares.
A spokeswoman for Generale des Eaux said it bought the shares from oil company Elf Aquitaine SA.
Although Generale des Eaux declined to specify the price paid for the shares, market sources said the group paid around 480 francs ($82.72) a share.
The spokeswoman said Generale des Eaux bought around 380,000 shares in Havas Wednesday.
