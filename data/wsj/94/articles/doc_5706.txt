General Motors Corp., Ford Motor Co., Chrysler Corp. and Honda Motor Corp. all scheduled overtime this week at U.S. assembly plants.
Altogether, the 10 domestic auto makers said they plan to build 258,719 cars and trucks this week, an increase of 26.7% from the 204,206 vehicles built in the same week last year.
In January, the auto makers built 934,955 cars and trucks, up 16.7% from the year-earlier total of 801,192.
