Alexa Willson left Kidder, Peabody & Co. to form her own investment banking firm specializing in placing private equity and providing merger and acquisition advisory services.
Ms. Willson said her firm, Mille Capital Corp., has already secured its first advisory assignment with a multimedia company that she declined to identify.
The 36-year-old Ms. Willson most recently worked as senior vice president at Kidder Peabody, a unit of General Electric Co., Fairfield, Conn.
Ms. Willson, who will serve as president and chief executive officer of her firm, said she sees the best investment banking prospects in specialty retail, health care and multimedia.
