Sharper Image Corp., a retailer, said it expects net income for its fiscal fourth quarter ending Jan. 31 to grow by 16% to $5 million, compared with $4.3 million a year earlier.
For the fiscal year, the San Francisco-based company said it expects earnings of $1.8 million, or 20 cents a share, compared with $359,000, or four cents a share, a year earlier.
Sharper Image attributed the higher earnings to increased sales in its fourth quarter and reduced expenses.
Shares of the retailer rose 62.5 cents, or 11%, to $6.25 in Nasdaq Stock Market trading.
