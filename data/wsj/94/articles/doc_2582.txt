Harleysville Group Inc. said it expects losses from recent winter storms to reduce first-quarter results by about $7.5 million, or 58 cents a share, after taxes.
The regional insurance-holding company had net income of $4.5 million, or 35 cents a share, in the year-ago first quarter.
Most of the losses relate to property damage covered by homeowners' policies, and occurred in New England and the midAtlantic states.
Shares of Harleysville rose 37.5 cents to close at $24.125 a share, in trading on the Nasdaq Stock Market.
