Autotote Corp. said it agreed to acquire Marvin H. Sugarman Productions Inc. and its affiliate, Racing Technology Inc.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Sugarman Productions, based in New York City, is the largest simulcaster of live horse and greyhound racing events to off-track betting patrons in North America, Autotote said.
Sugarman Productions also simulcasts special entertainment and news and special live sports events world-wide.
