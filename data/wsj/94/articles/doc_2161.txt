Eugene M. Bullis, 48 years old, was named to the new post of vice president of interactive services at Nynex Information Resources Co., a unit of this Bell telephone company that publishes more than 300 yellow and white pages directories.
Mr. Bullis, formerly senior vice president for finance and strategy at Nynex's AGS Computers Inc. subsidiary, will oversee development of electronic shopping and on-line yellow pages at the publishing unit.
