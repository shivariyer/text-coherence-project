French telecommunications and engineering company Alcatel-Alsthom SA said it signed agreements with the Post, Telegraph & Telephone agency of China to create two joint-venture companies in telecommunications in Beijing and Chengdu.
The first venture, Alcatel of Beijing Transmission Systems, will make and sell microwave equipment for China's market.
The second venture, Alcatel of Chengdu Communication Systems, will make and sell line-transmission equipment.
The two will be jointly owned by Alcatel -- through its Alcatel China unit -- and by the Chinese government.
Alcatel Italia will ensure the technology transfer.
Alcatel has a 50% market share in digital-switching equipment in China.
