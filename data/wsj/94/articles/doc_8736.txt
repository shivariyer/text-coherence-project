Savings Bank of Rockland said its shareholders approved a proposed merger with First Fidelity Bank, New York, a unit of First Fidelity Bancorp., Lawrenceville, N.J.
Savings Bank of Rockland shareholders will receive $9 a share for a total of $5.9 million.
Pending certain regulatory approvals, the merger should be completed within a couple of months, the company said.
Savings Bank of Rockland has assets of $185 million.
First Fidelity has assets of $33.8 billion.
