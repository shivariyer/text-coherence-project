Marathon Oil Co. announced a natural gas discovery in Egypt's northern Nile Delta area, about 105 miles north of Cairo.
Marathon Oil is part of USX-Marathon Group.
The El Qar'a Northwest No. 1 well tested at combined daily flow rates of 36.2 million cubic feet of natural gas and 215 barrels of condensate from three zones.
The tests were conducted at measured depths of 11,254 to 11,275 feet, 11,812 to 11,835 feet, and 11,926 to 11,943 feet on choke sizes varying from 3/4 to 1 1/2 inches.
