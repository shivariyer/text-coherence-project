Bombardier Inc., Montreal, said Comair exercised options for five of Bombardier's Canadair Regional Jet aircraft, with a total value of $80 million.
Comair had ordered 20 of the 50-passenger jets in 1991, with options for an additional 20 aircraft.
Deliveries of the original 20 jets are to be completed by the end of 1994, with the five new orders scheduled for delivery by August 1995.
Comair, a unit of Comair Holdings Inc., Cincinnati, is a regional airline that coordinates its scheduling with that of Delta Air Lines.
Bombardier is a maker of transportation equipment.
