Parker & Parsley Petroleum Co. said a unit raised $183 million by privately selling 3,776,400 shares of 6 1/4% cumulative guaranteed convertible monthly income preferred stock with a liquidation preference of $50 a share.
Parker & Parsley said the funds will be used for acquisitions as they arise.
Meanwhile, the oil and natural gas company said it would use the proceeds to reduce its bank debt to $282 million.
Parker & Parsley said it had bank debt of $509 million at the end of 1993.
