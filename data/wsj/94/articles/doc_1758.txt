Price/Costco Inc. said sales for the four weeks ended March 13 rose 8% to $1.15 billion from $1.06 billion a year ago.
The club-warehouse operator said sales at stores open at least a year fell 1% for the four-week period.
For the 28 weeks ended March 13, total sales rose 7% to $8.76 billion from $8.22 billion a year ago.
Sales at stores open a year or more fell 3%.
