Charles R. Eitel and Royce Renfroe were elected to the board of this carpet manufacturer.
Mr. Eitel, 44 years old and chief executive officer of Interface, and Mr. Renfroe, 47, chief executive of Bentley Mills Inc., which was acquired by Interface last year, will succeed Don Lee, who retired, and Grant Todd.
Mr. Todd resigned from the board this month.
He has been appointed to the new position of vice president of corporate marketing.
