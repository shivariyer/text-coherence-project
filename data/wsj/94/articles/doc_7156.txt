John A. Porter, 50 years old, was elected to the board of this maker of plastic, specialty chemical and polymer products.
The election increases the number of directors to eight.
Mr. Porter was president and chief executive officer of Telephone Management Corp. before it was acquired by LDDS Communications Inc. in 1988.
Currently, he is vice chairman of the successor company of the three-way merger of LDDS Communications, Resurgens Communications Group Inc. and Metromedia Communications Corp. to form LDDS Metromedia Corp.
