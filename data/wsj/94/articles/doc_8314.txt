CORRPRO Cos.
(Medina, Ohio)-Michael R. Tighe, 43 years old, was named to the newly created post of senior vice president of this provider of corrosion-control engineering services, systems and equipment.
Mr. Tighe was president of Elgard Corp., Chardon, Ohio, a "major competitor" in the corrosion-control market for bridges and concrete structures, Corrpro said.
