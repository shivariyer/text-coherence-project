OF LOUISIANA Inc., Lafayette, La., said it is offering as many as 50 million common shares at 50 cents each.
The oil and natural gas company said it expects the offering to close by Friday.
A spokeswoman said half of the proceeds would be used to develop the Berry R. Cox oil field in South Texas, and the other half would be used for a drilling program in China and for general corporate purposes.
The company has 132.7 million shares outstanding.
