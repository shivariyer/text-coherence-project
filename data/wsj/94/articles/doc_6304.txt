Geotek Industries, Montvale, N.J., and financier George Soros said they agreed to acquire a 30% interest in a Mexican wireless communications company for about $24 million.
Mr. Soros and Geotek, also a wireless communications company, will pay $20 million in cash for Radio Cel SA de C.V.
In addition, Geotek will issue the Mexican company about 270,000 shares of common stock and offer it some of Geotek's technology.
Radio Cel has a license to provide wireless communications service in Mexico City, Monterrey, Guadalajara and other metropolitan areas.
