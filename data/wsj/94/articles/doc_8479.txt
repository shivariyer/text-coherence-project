Bernard V. Vonderschmitt and Curtis S. Wozniak have been elected directors of this maker of multimedia computer products.
Mr. Vonderschmitt, 70 years old, is president and cofounder of Xilinx Inc.
Mr. Wozniak, 38, is vice president of marketing for Sun Microsystems Computer Corp., a unit of Sun Microsystems Inc.
The appointments expand board membership to six, filling one vacant seat and adding a new one.
