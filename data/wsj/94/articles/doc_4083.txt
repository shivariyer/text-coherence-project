J. Michael Kelly was elected senior vice president, finance, of this telecommunications company, succeeding Nicholas L. Trivisonno, who earlier had been named executive vice president, strategic planning, and group president.
Mr. Kelley, 37 years old, who joined GTE in March 1991, has been vice president-controller of GTE.
