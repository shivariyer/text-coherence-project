Tracor Inc. said its Applied Sciences Inc. unit was awarded a five-year contract with a total potential value of $13.2 million by the Naval Undersea Warfare Center.
The contract is to provide engineering development support for antisubmarine warfare systems for surface ships and analysis and life-cycle support of sonar transmitter and receiver subsystems.
The company said the contract continues more than 15 years of providing similar work to the U.S. Navy.
