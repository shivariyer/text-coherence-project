Michael Burns, 55, was named president of this holding company's Inland Pacific Enterprises Ltd. unit, a company BC Gas created to hold its nonutilities investments.
BC Gas said Patrick Lloyd, 42, will succeed Mr. Burns as executive vice president, finance and administration, of its BC Gas Utility Ltd. unit.
Mr. Lloyd is the gas utility's corporate secretary, as well as senior vice president, corporate development and gas supply.
BC Gas also announced a reorganization into three business units: BC Gas Utility; Inland Pacific; and a 67%-owned oil pipeline subsidiary Trans Mountain Pipe Line Co.
