Amoco Corp. said a unit's natural gas well in a newly discovered North Sea field tested at a daily rate of 54.8 million cubic feet of natural gas.
A spokesman for the oil company said its Amoco (U.K.) Exploration Co.'s Bell well was tested at depths between 9,052 feet and 9,132 feet.
Amoco (U.K.), the operator, owns a 30.77% interest in the well, as does a unit of British Gas PLC.
A unit of Amerada Hess Corp. owns a 23.08% interest, and Enterprise Oil PLC owns the remaining 15.38%.
