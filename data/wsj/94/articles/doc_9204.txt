Franklin Resources Inc. agreed to pay about $9 million in stock and cash to a departing executive who joined the mutual fund company when it acquired Templeton, Galbraith & Hansberger Ltd. in 1992.
Franklin Resources agreed to the severance package for Thomas Hansberger, who resigned in October as a Franklin Resources director and as president and chief executive officer of its Templeton Worldwide Inc. unit., according to a Franklin Resources proxy statement dated Dec. 30.
The package includes $8.29 million in cash and shares valued at $581,000.
Mr. Hansberger was president and chief executive officer of Templeton before its acquisition by Franklin Resources.
