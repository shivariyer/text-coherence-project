Robert P. Bozzone, 60 years old, announced plans to retire in June as president and chief executive officer of this specialty steelmaker.
A company spokesman said Mr. Bozzone has long said he wanted to retire from the chief executive's post when he reached 60.
Mr. Bozzone is expected to be named vice chairman and assume a consulting role with the company.
He also holds about 9% of the company's shares outstanding.
Mr. Bozzone and Chairman Richard P. Simmons will recommend that the board name Arthur H. Aronson president and chief executive.
Mr. Aronson, 58, is executive vice president and chief operating officer.
