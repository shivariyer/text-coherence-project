Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 1.3% last week to 1,879,000 tons from 1,854,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said.
Last week's output rose 4% from the 1,806,000 tons produced a year earlier.
The industry used 91.2% of its capability last week, compared with 90% the previous week and 85.4% a year earlier.
The capability utilization rate is a calculation designed to indicate at what percent of its production capability the industry is operating in a given week.
