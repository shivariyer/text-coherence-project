Anadarko Petroleum Corp., Houston, said it will boost capital spending about 40% in 1994 to $370 million, from 1993 capital expenditures of $264.5 million.
Exploration and development spending of $247 million is planned, with exploration spending increasing to $105 million from $97.3 million, and development spending increasing to $142 million from $77.7 million.
The oil and natural gas company said 85% of exploration and development funds will be spent on domestic drilling projects, with the remainder going to projects in Algeria, Canada and Indonesia.
In addition, the company has budgeted $36 million for construction of gas gathering facilities in southwestern Kansas.
