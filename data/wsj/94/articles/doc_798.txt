Jacobs Engineering Group Inc. said it entered an agreement with Grupo Sacmag, Mexico City, to jointly provide engineering, procurement and construction-management services for projects in Mexico.
Terms weren't disclosed.
In a news release, Jacobs said the companies will jointly serve the food, pharmaceutical, biotechnological and medical-products industries.
Grupo Sacmag, an engineering, architectural and environmental-services unit of Sacmag de Mexico, has six companies in Mexico, one in Puerto Rico and one in Costa Rica.
