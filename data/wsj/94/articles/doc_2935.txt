American Airlines said its February traffic slipped 2.8% from February 1993.
The AMR Corp. unit reported 6.7 billion revenue passenger miles for the month, down from 6.9 billion miles a year earlier.
A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.
Load factor, or percentage of available seats filled, rose to 59.5% from 56.3% a year earlier.
For the first two months, traffic fell 3.1% to 14.1 billion revenue passenger miles from 14.5 billion in 1993.
Load factor rose to 59.2% from 56.7%.
