The Treasury plans to raise $1.6 billion in new cash with the sale Thursday of about $16.5 billion of 52-week bills to redeem $14.91 billion in maturing bills.
The Cusip number for the bills is 912794Q49.
The bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, will be dated Feb. 10 and will mature Feb. 9, 1995.
Noncompetitive tenders must be received by noon Eastern time on Thursday at the Treasury Department or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders must be received by 1 p.m.
