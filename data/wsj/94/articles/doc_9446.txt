Tandy Corp., Fort Worth, Texas, said it expects to add more than 3,600 jobs this year as it continues to expand its retail operations.
The operator of the Radio Shack chain of electronics stores said most of its job growth will come from its two newest chains, Computer City and Incredible Universe.
Tandy said the two units are expected to add 3,400 workers this year.
Tandy has said it plans to open 24 new Computer City stores this year and six new Incredible Universe superstores.
Tandy said Radio Shack's work force will grow this year by about 200 people.
