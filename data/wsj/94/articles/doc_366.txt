The Texas Economic Index, a weekly leading indicator, rose slightly to 111.7 in the latest week from a revised 111.6 in the previous week.
The annual growth rate in the index, meanwhile, rose to 2.5% from a revised 2.44% in the prior week.
Among the factors boosting the annual growth rate: Initial unemployment claims fell slightly from the year-earlier level and Texas refinery output rose.
