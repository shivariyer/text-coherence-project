Roger F. Davis, 50 years old, was named vice president, international finance at this telecommunications company.
Mr. Davis succeeds Vince Salvati, 44, who becomes vice president, international strategic analysis.
Maureen B. Tart, 38, succeeds Mr. Davis as vice president and controller, a position he had held since 1988.
Ms. Tart had been chief financial officer at AT&T Capital Corp., the publicly held company that is 86% owned by AT&T.
The appointments are effective March 15.
