American Telephone & Telegraph Co. said it reached telephone and telecommunications service contracts with three major customers.
Chevron Corp., San Francisco, reached a $42 million agreement for long-distance services, extending its contract through mid-1997.
Owens-Corning Fiberglas Corp., Toledo, Ohio, extended for four years, or $12 million, a contract for voice and data communications services.
And Banc One Corp., Columbus, Ohio, extended various service agreements, without disclosing the value.
