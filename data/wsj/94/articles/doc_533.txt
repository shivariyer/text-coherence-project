American Financial Corp. said it extended its exchange offer for its debentures outstanding to April 15 from last Friday.
American Financial, which has interests in property and casualty insurance, annuity programs and portfolio investing, said investors have tendered $192 million principal amount of the $548.7 million principal amount outstanding.
American Financial is offering 9.75% debentures due April 20, 2004, plus cash for all its debentures outstanding.
