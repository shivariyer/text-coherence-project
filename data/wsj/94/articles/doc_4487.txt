James W. Kennedy and Leo M. Hart were named to share the posts of chairman and chief executive officer of this marketer of Swiss Army knives.
Mr. Kennedy, 43 years old, had been president and chief executive officer, while Mr. Hart, 44, was executive vice president.
They replace as chairwoman Karen Brenner.
Ms. Brenner is managing director of Noel Group, a holding company with ties to Louis Marx Jr., a major Forschner investor and chairman of Forschner's executive committee.
Ms. Brenner, who presided over Forschner's restructuring, will continue as a consultant to the company.
