Siemens AG announced it has orders for telecommunications projects in the Philippines and Vietnam.
The contract in the Philippines is valued at about 230 million marks ($130.3 million) and involves installing about 1.2 million digital telephones for Philippine Long Distance Telephone Co.
The second contract is with Vietnam Posts and Telecommunications and is valued at 20 million marks.
The project would modernize Ho Chi Minh City's network and involves about 60,000 connections, the company said.
