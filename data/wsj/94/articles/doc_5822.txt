Corning Inc. said it agreed to buy one-third of the stock in Ceramem Separations Inc., a closely held company that plans to make patented ceramic membrane products for environmental and process applications
Terms weren't disclosed.
Corning said Ceramem, based in Waltham, Mass., is one-third owned by Enjay Inc., a unit of Exxon Corp.
The remaining shares are held by Ceramem.
