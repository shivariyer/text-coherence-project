RYAN, BECK & Co., West Orange, N.J., announced a special cash dividend of 18 cents a share and a quarterly cash dividend of one cent a share, payable Feb. 18 to holders of record Feb. 10.
The investment-banking firm also said fourth-quarter net income was $626,000, or 18 cents a share, down 43% from a year-earlier $1.1 million, or 31 cents a share.
Revenue slid 3.4% to $6.6 million from $6.9 million.
For the year, earnings were $4.1 million, or $1.15 a share, up from a year-earlier $3.5 million, or $1.02 a share.
Revenue rose 17% to $28.6 million.
