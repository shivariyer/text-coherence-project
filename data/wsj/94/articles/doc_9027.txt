Bombardier Inc. said it will issue through a public offering 10 million Class B common shares at 21.875 Canadian dollars (US$16.55) each.
Bombardier currently has 155 million common shares outstanding, including 110 million Class B shares.
In trading on the Montreal Exchange Friday, the Class B shares closed at C$21.75, down 37.5 cents.
ScotiaMcLeod Inc. is lead underwriter for the offering.
Bombardier, a transportation equipment maker, said it will use the net proceeds of about C$210 million to increase working capital.
