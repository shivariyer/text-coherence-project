Crystal Oil Co. said shareholders approved an amendment to the terms of its Series A convertible voting preferred stock.
The company said the amendment increases the conversion rate on the preferred stock to 0.0031 common share for each preferred from 0.002 common share for each preferred.
The amendment also requires Crystal to redeem for six cents a share all the Series A preferred shares outstanding as of the close of business on the first business day following the 90th day after the effective date of amendment.
