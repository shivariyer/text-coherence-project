Public Service Co. of Colorado said it will redeem about $190 million of bonds and $22.6 million of promissory notes.
The redemptions include all outstanding 7.25% first-mortgage bonds due 2001, at a price of 101.71% of the principal amount, plus accrued interest; the 7.50% series due 2002, at a price of 102.37% of the principal amount, plus accrued interest; the 7.625% series due 2003, at a price of 102.37%, plus accrued interest; and the 8.25% series due 2007, at a price of 103.25%, plus accrued interest.
The natural gas and electricity company also said it intends to sell $212 million of its First Collateral Trust Bonds to provide funds for the redemptions.
