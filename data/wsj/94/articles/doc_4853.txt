Onex Corp. said it recorded a gain of about 39 million Canadian dollars (US$29.1 million) on the sale of much of its remaining stake in Johnstown American Industries Inc.
Onex sold 1,677,101 shares of Johnstown through the manufacturing concern's recent secondary stock issue, which was priced at US$25.75 a share, for total proceeds of C$54 million.
It continues to hold about 375,000 shares of Johnstown, for a 4% stake in the company.
Onex is a holding company with investments in airline catering, automotive products and other manufacturing ventures.
