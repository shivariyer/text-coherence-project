International Paper Co. said it bought 140 million shares in Carter Holt Harvey Ltd. for about $289 million, increasing its stake in the New Zealand forest-products company to 24%.
International Paper said it purchased the shares from Brierley Investments Ltd. of Wellington, New Zealand, its partner in a joint venture that will continue to hold International Paper's shares and Brierly's remaining 8% stake.
International Paper, which held one of nine director positions at Carter Holt in 1993, declined to say if it planned any further increase in its stake.
International Paper, a Purchase, N.Y., paper and paper products company, had $13.69 billion in sales in 1993; Carter Holt had about $1.3 billion in sales for the year ending March 31, 1993.
