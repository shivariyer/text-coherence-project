A Black & Decker Corp. unit said it won two contracts from the Environmental Protection Agency with a potential value of as much as $200 million over the next five years.
PRC Environmental Management Inc., a provider of scientific and technology-based systems and services to government and commercial clients, will work with the EPA to enforce the proper management of solid waste.
PRC employs 6,800 people and had revenue of $761 million in 1993.
