International Gaming Management Inc. said it agreed to acquire the remaining 50% stake in Splash Casino from Casino River Vessels Inc., Midamerica Investments Inc. and Casino Consultants Inc., in a transaction valued at $58 million.
The Minneapolis company, which sells and leases gaming machines, previously entered into a definitive agreement to acquire an initial 50% stake in Splash Casino.
International Gaming said the transaction is subject to a number of conditions, including obtaining necessary financing and the approval of the Mississippi Gaming Commission.
The Splash Casino is at the entrance to Mhoon Landing in Tunica County, Miss., 35 miles south of Memphis, Tenn.
It has 635 slot machines and 36 gambling tables.
For its first year of operation, ended Oct. 19, 1993, the casino had $150 million in revenue.
