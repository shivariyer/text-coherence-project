Supercuts Inc. said sales at stores open a year or longer grew more than 5% in 1993's fourth quarter.
Full results will be announced in early March.
For the current year the hair-care chain said it expects sales overall to increase 12% to 15%, while it expects sales in stores open a year or more to increase by 4.5% to 5.5%.
Supercuts also said it will open 150 stores this year, including 50 in metropolitan New York.
