The Treasury plans to pay down $125 million on the public debt with the sale Monday of about $25.2 billion in short-term bills.
Maturing bills outstanding total $25.32 billion.
The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week bills (Cusip 912794K37) maturing on April 28, 1994, and 26-week bills (Cusip 912794L51) maturing July 28, 1994.
Noncompetitive tenders must be received by noon EST Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders must be received by 1 p.m. EST.
The minimum denomination is $10,000.
