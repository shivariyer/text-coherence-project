Norwest Bank Minnesota, a unit of Norwest Corp., said it has agreed to acquire a stake in Crestone Capital Management Inc., a Denver-based investment adviser.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The bank said Crestone focuses on securities of small- and medium-sized companies with small market capitalization.
Norwest said the acquisition will allow Norwest Investment Management, a unit of the bank, to expand its investment product.
The transaction is subject to approval by the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency.
