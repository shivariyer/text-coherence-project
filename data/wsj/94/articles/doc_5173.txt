Medical Care America Inc. said it extended the expiration date of its tender offer for seven million shares of its common stock to midnight EST, March 9.
The offer, priced at $26 to $29 a share, was to expire at midnight EST, Feb. 24.
Completion of the tender offer is contingent on the sale of its unit, Critical Care America Inc., to Caremark International Inc.
That transaction awaits government approval.
Medical Care's business is surgical centers.
