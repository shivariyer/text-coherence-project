Infinite Machines Corp. signed a letter of intent to acquire HGG Laser Fare Corp. for an undisclosed amount of stock.
Laser Fare is a privately held laser applications company in Smithfield, R.I.
Infinite Machines, based in Incline Village, Nev., said Laser Fare had 1993 revenue of about $3.3 million.
Infinite Machines said it expects the acquisition to close this month.
The company also said that it plans to open a rotary engine development facility in Wisconsin in the second quarter, and that it expects to begin marketing gasoline-powered rotary engines.
