Midland Resources Inc., Midland, Texas, said it acquired all of the common stock outstanding of Miresco Inc., a privately held affiliate.
Under the terms of the agreement, Midland will issue a still-to-be-determined number of shares to Deas H. Warley III and Sal J. Pagano, Miresco's former shareholders.
No purchase price has been set.
The exchange is to be based on the relative fair values of Midland and Miresco, which the two boards will determine after receiving an outside fairness opinion.
The issuance of Midland shares will occur by March 31.
Midland explores, develops and produces natural gas and oil.
