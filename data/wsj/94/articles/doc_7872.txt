Unilab Corp. completed its acquisition of Premier Laboratory Services Inc., the parent company of PathLab.
Unilab, a medical-testing concern, said the purchase price is $31.5 million in cash and $7.5 million in Unilab common stock.
Unilab said it will operate under the PathLab name in the San Jose and San Francisco areas.
PathLab, based in San Jose, Calif., has current annual sales of about $22 million.
On the Nasdaq Stock Market, Unilab shares closed at $6, unchanged.
