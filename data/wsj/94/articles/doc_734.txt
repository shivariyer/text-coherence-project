OHM Corp. said it was awarded a hazardous-waste-clean-up contract by the Navy Department with a potential value of $250 million over five years.
The contract specifies that the environmental remediation company will clean up contaminated sites at Navy, Marine Corps and other government installations in Southern California, Arizona, New Mexico and Nevada under the administration of the Southwest Division Naval Facilities Engineering Command.
OHM had revenue of $242.4 million in 1993.
