PRATT HOTEL Corp., Dallas, said its subsidiaries GB Property Funding Corp. and PRT Funding Corp. sold $270 million of debt securities.
GB Property sold $185 million of 10 7/8 first mortgage notes guaranteed by Greate Bay Hotel & Casino Inc. and GB Holdings Inc., and secured by a first lien on the Sands Hotel & Casino in Atlantic City, N.J. PRT Funding sold $85 million of 11 5/8% senior notes guaranteed by its parent company, Pratt Casino Corp.
Pratt Hotel said proceeds of the offerings will be used to refinance much of the existing debt of its subsidiaries.
Salomon Brothers led the underwriting group.
The offerings are expected to close Feb. 17.
