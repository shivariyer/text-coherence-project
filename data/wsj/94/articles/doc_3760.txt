Robert Bagby was named president and chief operating officer, a new position.
Mr. Bagby, 61 years old, had been vice president of operations of this maker and designer of computer systems; he will retain those duties.
He succeeds as president Leonard Mackenzie, 56, who will remain chief executive officer and vice chairman.
Also, Don Rutherford, the company's chief financial officer, is leaving "to pursue other interests," General Automation said.
No successor was named for Mr. Rutherford, 54, who couldn't be reached for comment.
A spokeswoman for General Automation said the company expected to name a successor to Mr. Rutherford within six weeks.
