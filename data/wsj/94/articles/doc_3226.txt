Canadian steel production totaled 259,188 metric tons in the week ended Saturday, up 0.3% from 258,299 tons in the preceding week and down 7.9% from 281,443 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a government agency, said.
A metric ton is equal to 2,204.6 pounds.
The cumulative total for 1994 was 2,033,542 tons, down 7.4% from 2,195,892 tons a year earlier.
