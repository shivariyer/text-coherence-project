DURACELL INTERNATIONAL Inc. increased its quarterly dividend 38%, to 22 cents a share from 16 cents, payable March 17 to shares of record March 3.
Duracell's initial cash dividend was declared in August 1992 at eight cents a share and doubled to 16 cents in February 1993.
Duracell, of Bethel, Conn., makes and markets alkaline batteries.
