George B. Beitzel, 65 years old, a retired senior vice president of International Business Machines Corp., was elected a director of this information technology services concern.
Mr. Beitzel succeeds Robert Stephenson, 55, an IBM vice president and president of IBM's World Trade Asia Corp. unit, who resigned from CTG's board due to the demands of his new Asia post.
Mr. Stephenson had served as a director for five years.
The board remains at seven members.
