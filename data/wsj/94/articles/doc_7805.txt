George Fugelsang was named president of Dresdner Securities (USA) Inc., a unit of the German bank, effective Feb. 1.
Dresdner Securities previously was known as ABD Securities Corp.
Mr. Fugelsang currently is a managing director with Morgan Stanley & Co. in London.
In addition, Gerhard Eberstadt, the bank's management board member responsible for securities operations, will succeed Wolfgang Roeller as chairman of Dresdner Securities (USA).
