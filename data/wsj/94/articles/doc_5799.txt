National Golf Properties Inc. acquired the El Dorado Lakes Golf Course in Gilbert, Ariz., from a Phoenix investor group.
Terms weren't disclosed.
National Golf Properties said American Golf, Santa Monica, Calif., will lease the golf course and add it to its Phoenix Area Central Reservation System.
El Dorado Lakes is a newly constructed 6,716-yard, daily-fee golf course near Phoenix, the company said, and began operating Dec. 15, 1993.
