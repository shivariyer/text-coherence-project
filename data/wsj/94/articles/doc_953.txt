BROWN-FORMAN Corp., Louisville, Ky., said it filed a medium-term note program under its existing $250 million shelf registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission.
Under a medium-term note program, the company may sell periodically up to $250 million of debt securities with maturities of nine months or greater or through agents or directly to purchasers.
Brown-Forman, whiskey and distilled-spirits marketer, said it may also sell debt securities on an underwritten basis.
