WESTERN GAS RESOURCES Inc., Denver, said it is offering 2.4 million shares of $2.625 cumulative convertible preferred stock.
The natural gas company said it will use the proceeds from the $120 million offering to pay part of the debt incurred in acquiring Mountain Gas Resources Inc. and the Black Lake Processing Facility and related reserves in 1993.
Morgan Stanley & Co. and Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp. are underwriting the offering and have an option to purchase as many as 360,000 additional shares to cover overallotments.
