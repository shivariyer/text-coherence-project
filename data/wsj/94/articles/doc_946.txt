Admiral S. Robert Foley Jr. and Armen Der Marderosian were elected to this construction company's board, expanding the number of members to nine from seven.
Mr. Foley, 65 years old, retired last June from Raytheon Co. as vice president of commercial marketing and planning.
Mr. Der Marderosian, 56, is executive vice president and general manager of GTE Corp.'s Government Systems Corp. unit, Needham Heights, Mass.
