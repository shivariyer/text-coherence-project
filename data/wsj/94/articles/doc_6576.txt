Charming Shoppes Inc. said sales for the four weeks ended Jan. 29 were $60.5 million, about flat with a year ago, while sales at stores open at least a year fell 9%.
The apparel retailer, which operates 1,333 stores in 45 states under the names Fashion Bug and Fashion Bug Plus, said severe winter storms had an adverse effect on sales.
For the 12 months ended Jan. 29, sales rose 6.4% to $1.25 billion from $1.18 billion, while sales at stores open at least a year fell 2%.
