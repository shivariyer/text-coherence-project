This mortgage banking concern established the office of the president and named Robert K. Cole and Brad Morrice to serve jointly in that new position.
Mr. Cole, 47 years old, also will hold the title of chief operating officer, finance and Mr. Morrice, 37, will serve as chief operating officer, administration.
Jack French, 62, who continues as chairman and chief executive officer, previously held the title of president.
Mr. Cole most recently was president of Triple Five Inc.
Mr. Morrice previously was executive vice president of Plaza Home Mortgage.
