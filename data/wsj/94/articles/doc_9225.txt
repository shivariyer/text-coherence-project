Money market mutual fund assets jumped $10.59 billion to $598.40 billion from a revised $587.81 billion for the week ended Wednesday, the Investment Company Institute said.
Assets of 651 retail money market mutual funds increased by $2.64 billion to $400.64 billion, the trade group said.
Among retail funds, assets of 404 taxable money market funds rose by $1.09 billion to $309.11 billion.
Assets of 247 retail tax-exempt funds increased by $1.55 billion to $91.53 billion.
Assets of the 279 institutional money market funds rose by $7.94 billion to $197.76 billion.
Among institutional funds, the assets of 225 taxable money market funds increased by $7.94 billion to $174.55 billion.
Assets of 54 tax-exempt funds rose $1.7 million to $23.21 billion.
