The United Steelworkers of America said that members employed by USX Corp. approved a long-term contract by a vote of 8,405 to 2,471.
The contract is effective Feb. 1, and expires July 31, 1999.
It covers 15,128 employees and puts a union representative on the USX board, along with raising wages and pensions, the union said.
The current agreement expires today.
