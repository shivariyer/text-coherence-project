Apparel maker Liz Claiborne Inc. said it entered into an agreement to market apparel products in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.
The first free-standing licensed Liz Claiborne store in the Middle East region will open Feb. 8 in the Bur Juman Centre.
The company will design the stores, with the licensee responsible for daily operations.
The company has 19 stores in the U.S. and other retail license agreements in Hong Kong, Singapore, St. Maarten and Japan.
