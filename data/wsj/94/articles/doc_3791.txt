USBancorp Inc. said its board adopted a stock repurchase program through which the company may repurchase 237,000 shares, or 5%, of its common stock over the next 12 months.
The board authorized a cash outlay of $6.5 million for the repurchase.
At the end of 1993, USBancorp had about 4.7 million shares of common stock outstanding.
With this program, USBancorp can repurchase its common stock in open market transactions at prevailing prices, or in privately negotiated transactions.
