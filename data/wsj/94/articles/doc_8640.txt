Franklin Resources Inc. said it expects to report earnings of between $56 million and $57.5 million for the quarter ended Dec. 31, outstripping Wall Street's consensus estimate.
The money-management firm in San Mateo, Calif., released the fiscal first-quarter earnings range, which is equal to between 68 cents a share and 70 cents a share, at its annual shareholders' meeting yesterday .
In the year-ago period, it earned $34.8 million, or 43 cents a share, on revenue of $136.7 million.
Analysts had expected the company to earn 61 cents a share, according to Zacks Investment Research Inc.
In New York Stock Exchange late trading, the company's stock closed at $42.875 a share, up $2.625, or 6.5%.
