John E. Sircy, 38 years old, was named to the new post of executive vice president and chief operating officer of the bank-holding company and of its lead bank, Citizens Bank & Trust Co., effective April 4.
Mr. Sircy, formerly senior vice president and chief financial officer of Norwest Bank Iowa N.A., a unit of Norwest Corp., will oversee the credit-administration, audit, human-resources, technology, financialreporting, investment-portfolio and asset-liability management functions.
