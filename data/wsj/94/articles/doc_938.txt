CALIFORNIA FEDERAL BANK, Los Angeles, said it raised $172.5 million through its public offering of 10 5/8% noncumulative perpetual preferred stock, Series B. The bank sold a total of 1,725,000 shares, including 225,000 issued under an overallotment option.
Combined with $195 million raised earlier this year through a rights offering, the bank has raised about $367.5 million in new equity.
