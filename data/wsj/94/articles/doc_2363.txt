Chempower Inc., Akron, Ohio, said it received a contract valued at about $14 million to provide asbestos abatement and insulation services for McDermott International Inc.'s Babcock & Wilcox Co. unit at the Armstrong Generating Station in Reesedale, Pa.
The facility is owned and operated by West Penn Power, a subsidiary of Allegheny Power System Inc.
Babcock & Wilcox is serving as the prime contractor of the project.
The contract will begin in April 1994 and be completed in November 1995.
