Tenneco Inc. said it will make a $19.9 million mandatory annual principal payment to its sinking fund March 1.
The diversified industrial corporation said the funds will be used to redeem 195,744 shares of its $7.40 cumulative preferred stock.
Holders of record Jan. 20 who were selected by lot to participate in the redemption will be paid $100 a share plus $1.85 in dividends.
