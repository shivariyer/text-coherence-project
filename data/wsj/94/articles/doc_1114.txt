ABITIBI-PRICE Inc., Toronto, said it will issue 10 million common shares at 17.35 Canadian dollars (US$12.71) each.
The underwriting syndicate is led by Nesbitt Thomson Inc.
The forest-products company said it will use the proceeds for capital projects, to repay long-term debt and for general corporate purposes.
The announcement came after the close of trading in New York and Toronto.
In composite trading yesterday on the New York Stock Exchange, Abitibi-Price closed at $13.375, unchanged.
