J.E. "Buck" Cannon was named a director and chairman, filling a vacancy left since the energy products and services company's December acquisition of a unit of Santa Fe Energy Resources Inc.
Mr. Cannon, 60 years old, was a consultant to American Oil & Gas Corp.
The company also named Gregory G. Jenkins, president, chief operating officer and a director, to the additional post of chief executive officer.
Mr. Jenkins, 36, also fills a position vacant since the acquisition.
