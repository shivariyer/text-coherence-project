Eskimo Pie Corp. said it acquired substantially all of the assets of frozen yogurt maker Sugar Creek Foods of Russellville Inc. for $13 million.
Eskimo Pie, maker of the Eskimo Pie ice cream bar and other frozen novelties, said it would pay $9.2 million in cash and $3.8 million in 4.5% convertible subordinated debentures, convertible into Eskimo Pie common stock at $23.375 a share.
In Nasdaq trading, Eskimo Pie closed at $19.75, up 25 cents.
Sugar Creek, based in Russellville, Ark., makes a variety of yogurt for the institutional, food-service and away-from-home markets.
Preliminary results indicate that Sugar Creek had 1993 revenue of $9.3 million and pretax profit of $2.1 million.
