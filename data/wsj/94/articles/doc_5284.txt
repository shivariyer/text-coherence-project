Telefon AB L.M. Ericsson, a Swedish telecommunications company, signed a number of contracts with Liaoning Post and Telecommunications Administration in China covering the delivery of AXE switching equipment.
The total value of the contracts is 220 million kronor ($27.6 million) and all equipment will be delivered in 1994.
Ericsson also signed a contract with the Posts & Telecommunication Corp. in Zimbabwe for deliveries of equipment to the Midlands Digitalization Project.
Ericsson valued the order at 240 million kronor.
It covers turn-key supply and installation of an AXE switching system as well as a transmission system for microwave-radio and optical-fiber equipment.
