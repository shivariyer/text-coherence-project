MAGAZINES: Caroline Miller, editor-in-chief of Lear's magazine, was named editor of Seventeen magazine by K-III Communications Corp.
Ms. Miller, 46, succeeds Midge Richardson, 63, who left the post in October to become editor-at-large at Kohlberg Kravis Roberts & Co.'s K-III.
Meanwhile, YM editor-in-chief Bonnie Hurowitz-Fuller, 37, said she is leaving Bertelsmann AG's YM.
She will become editor-in-chief of Marie Claire, an American version of a French fashion magazine to be tested in the fall by Hearst Corp.
