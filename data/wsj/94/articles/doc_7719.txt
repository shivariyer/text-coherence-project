Domino's Pizza International Inc. signed an agreement giving franchise rights to Domino's Pizza Group Ltd. of the United Kingdom to oversee the 75 U.K. stores and open at least 200 stores in four years in the U.K.
Domino's said the U.K. stores had sales of more than $26 million in 1993.
Domino's Pizza Group will oversee training, marketing and development support to the existing 32 franchise owners.
Domino's Pizza International has more than 5,200 stores throughout the U.S. and in 35 international markets.
