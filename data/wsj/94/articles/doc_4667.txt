Procter & Gamble Co. said it signed an agreement to establish a joint venture in China with Guangzhou Lonkey Industrial Co. and Hutchison Whampoa Ltd. to make and market laundry detergent.
The joint venture, Procter & Gamble Lonkey Detergent Ltd., will make detergent at the Guangzhou Lonkey Industrial facility in southern China and market it under Guangzhou Lonkey's brand names.
Hutchison Whampoa, a Hong Kong-based conglomerate, is a partner in P&G's other Chinese joint ventures.
