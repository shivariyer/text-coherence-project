John J. Lee, chairman of this manufacturer of high-tech materials, was named sole chief executive officer, and Robert D. Krumme was named vice chairman.
They succeed John L. Doyle, who resigned from both positions but remains a director.
Hexcel's board also elected three directors, expanding the board to 10.
They are Mr. Krumme; Charles A. Lynch, chairman of Greyhound Lines Inc. and chairman of Market Value Partners Co.; and Donald J. O'Mara, Hexcel's president and chief operating officer.
