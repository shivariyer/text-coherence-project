Discount-apparel retailer 50-Off Stores Inc. said sales at stores open for at least a year increased 2.5% in the five-week period ended last Friday and that total sales for the period rose 11% from a year earlier.
For the fourth quarter, ended Friday, comparable-store sales were essentially flat, and for fiscal year 1994, 50-Off said such sales were down more than 10%.
Total sales for the quarter increased 11% to $68.6 million from $61.6 million and for the year grew 10% to $199.7 million from $181 million.
