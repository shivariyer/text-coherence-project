Hicks, Muse, Tate & Furst Inc.'s Hat Brands Inc. said it signed an agreement to acquire privately held Texace Corp., San Antonio, a maker of golf headwear.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The private investment concern said Texace posted sales of $15 million in 1993.
Hicks Muse said Hat Brands, which it acquired in 1992, projects earnings of about $40 million on sales of about $200 million this year, including Texace's results.
