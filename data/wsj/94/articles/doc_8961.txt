Raw-steel production by the nation's mills decreased 0.1% last week to 1,892,000 tons from 1,894,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said.
Last week's output rose 7.2% from the 1,765,000 tons produced a year earlier.
The industry used 91.9% of its capability last week, compared with 92% the previous week and 83.5% a year earlier.
