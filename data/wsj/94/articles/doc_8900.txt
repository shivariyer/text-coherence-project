WPI Group Inc. said it will acquire Termiflex Corp. in a stock swap.
WPI said it expects a definitive agreement to be signed by week's end under which Termiflex shareholders would receive 1.2 WPI common shares for each Termiflex share.
The value of the proposed transaction wasn't given.
Termiflex, a Merrimack, N.H., maker of computer terminals, currently has about 1.35 million shares outstanding.
WPI, a Warner, N.H., power-conversion components concern, has about 3.7 million common shares outstanding.
