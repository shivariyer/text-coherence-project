Fujitsu Ltd. and a unit of France's Alcatel-Alsthom SA have jointly won a contract valued at 13 billion yen ($122.7 million) to lay 3,400 kilometers of submarine fiber-optic cable linking Hong Kong, Vietnam and Thailand by November 1995, the Japanese computer and electrical equipment maker announced.
Fujitsu said its share in the contract, awarded by an international consortium, is valued at six billion yen.
Plans call for completion of the system by December 1995.
