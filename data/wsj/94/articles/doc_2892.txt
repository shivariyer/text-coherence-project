Washington Homes Inc. said it agreed in principle to take over substantially all the assets of Westminster Homes Inc.'s home-building division for a price to be based on assets and liabilities at closing.
The home-building division of Westminster, a unit of Weyerhaeuser Real Estate Co., Tacoma, Wash., had revenue of $36 million last year.
It operates in Raleigh and Greensboro, N.C.
