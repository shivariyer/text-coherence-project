Spain's gross domestic product contracted by 0.3% in the fourth quarter of 1993 from the fourth quarter of 1992, the national statistics institute said.
The institute said real GDP shrank 1% in 1993 from 1992.
GDP grew 0.8% in 1992.
Fourth-quarter GDP rose 0.1% from the third quarter, the institute said.
The institute also revised its third-quarter GDP figure to a 1% decline from the third quarter of 1992 from the original figure of 0.9% released last December.
