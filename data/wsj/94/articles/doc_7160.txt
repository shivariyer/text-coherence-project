Ladd Furniture Inc. said it completed its acquisition of Pilliod Cabinet Co.
The transaction, valued at $54 million, involved a $23.9 million cash payment for all stock outstanding of Pilliod's parent, Pilliod Holding Co., Ladd said in a news release.
The deal also involved the repayment of about $29.9 million of Pilliod debt and the assumption of certain Pilliod liabilities.
Pilliod is a furniture maker with more than $80 million in annual sales.
The purchase agreement was announced in December.
