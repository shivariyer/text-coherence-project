Airgas Inc., a unit of BOC Group Inc. of England, completed the acquisition of the California and Nevada retail industrial gas and welding supply operations of Birmingham, Ala. based Airco Gas & Gear Co.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The acquired business has annual sales of about $48 million.
Airgas has annual sales in excess of $550 million.
AIRGAS Inc., based in Radnor, Pa., completed its acquisition of certain businesses of Airco Gas & Gear Co., a unit of BOC Group Inc.
An article yesterday incorrectly said Airgas was a unit of BOC Group.
(WSJ Feb. 10, 1994)
