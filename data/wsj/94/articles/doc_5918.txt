MORRISON KNUDSEN Corp. (Boise, Idaho)-Zbigniew Brzezinski, 65 years old, former national security adviser to President Jimmy Carter, was elected a director of this engineering and construction concern, succeeding Harold Anderson, director and contributing editor of the Omaha World-Herald Co., who retired.
In addition, Stephen Hanks, 43, formerly senior vice president, secretary and general counsel, was named to the new post of executive vice president, finance and administration.
