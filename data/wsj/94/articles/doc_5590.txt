Denmark's November trade surplus fell to 4.87 billion kroner ($712.8 million) from 4.98 billion kroner in October, the Danish statistical agency said.
Imports rose to 17.28 billion kroner from 16.88 billion kroner the month before, while exports rose to 22.15 billion kroner from 21.86 billion kroner.
The agency said the figures indicated that the pickup in exports that started in the middle of 1993 is continuing.
