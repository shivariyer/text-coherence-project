Foster Wheeler Energy Corp., a unit of Foster Wheeler Corp., said it is negotiating a $185 million contract to provide two steam generators for a Chinese power project.
The company said the two 300-megawatt anthracite coal-fired generators would be purchased by the International Tendering Co. of the China National Technical Import & Export Corp. and the Hubei Electric Power Industry Bureau.
Foster Wheeler Corp., a provider of design, engineering, construction and plant operations services, said the power project is being financed by the Overseas Economic Cooperation Fund of Japan.
