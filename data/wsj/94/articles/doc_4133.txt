Japan's exports of cars, trucks and buses plummeted 27% in January from a year earlier to 390,418 units, the Japan Automobile Manufacturers' Association said.
It was the 10th straight month of year-on-year declines in auto exports.
Vehicle exports to the U.S. fell 18%, the sixth straight month of declines, while those to the European Union dropped 38% after a year-on-year recovery in November and December.
