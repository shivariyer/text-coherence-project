McLEAN, Va. -- LCI International Inc. said it reached a tentative agreement to acquire a California long-distance company.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The telecommunications company also announced an extension of its bank-credit facility to $200 million from $150 million.
The company said additional credit will go toward further expansion of its business.
LCI said it signed a nonbinding letter of intent to acquire closely held T.M. Sepulveda Inc. and its affiliate, Premium Access Inc., both of Los Angeles.
The two companies have about $10 million in combined revenue.
