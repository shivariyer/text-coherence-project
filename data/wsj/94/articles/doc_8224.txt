Players International Inc. said it received approval from the City of Maryland Heights, Mo., to develop a riverboat casino complex.
The company also said it has applied to the Missouri Gaming Commission for a license to own and operate a riverboat casino, and it is seeking other approvals for the development site.
Upon completion, the proposed facility will be the third riverboat casino developed and operated by the company.
Players International is a developer and operator of riverboat casinos that owns and operates a facility at Merv Griffin's Landing on the Ohio River in Metropolis, Ill., and in Lake Charles.
