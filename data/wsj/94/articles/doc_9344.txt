William J. Scott Sr., 50 years old, formerly president of ABB Environmental Systems, a unit of Asea Brown Boveri Inc., was named senior vice president of this maker of mining equipment and air-pollution and ash-handling equipment, as well as president of Joy's Environmental Group, one of the company's two main businesses.
He succeeds Brian J. Watt, who resigned from the two posts at Joy in August 1993 to pursue other interests.
