Consumer prices in Austria rose 0.2% in December from November and were up 3.5% from the year-earlier period, the central office of statistics said.
In November, consumer prices were also up 3.5% from a year earlier, the statistics office said.
In December, the consumer price index stood at 122.8.
Prices in 1986 are equal to 100.
For all of 1993, inflation averaged 3.6%, down from 4.2% in 1992.
The consumer price index in 1993 averaged 121.9.
