Armco Steel Co. and the United Steelworkers of America agreed to a June 1 expiration date for their existing labor contract.
Armco is a 50-50 joint venture between Armco Inc. and Kawasaki Steel Corp.
The agreement covers about 1,200 employees at Armco Steel's Ashland, Ky., steelmaking operations.
The parties also agreed to a third-party dispute resolution procedure if needed to reach a new contract.
Under terms of the extension, no strikes, slowdowns, work stoppages or lockouts will occur while a contract is negotiated.
