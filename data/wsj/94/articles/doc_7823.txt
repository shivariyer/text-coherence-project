Tadd C. Seitz, 51 years old, chairman and chief executive officer of Scotts Co., Marysville, Ohio, and Robert L. Purdum, 58, chairman of Armco Inc., Pittsburgh, were named directors of this maker and marketer of highly engineered lighting fixtures and systems for industrial, commercial and outdoor applications.
The appointments increase the size of Holophane's board to seven members from six.
Mr. Seitz, who had been elected a director immediately after the completion of the company's initial public offering in November 1993, succeeded Fred Ulrich, who left the board at that time.
Mr. Purdum is a new addition to the board.
