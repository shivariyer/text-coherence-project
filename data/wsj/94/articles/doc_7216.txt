No one could mistake Planned Parenthood's status as the nation's Abortion Central, but nevertheless a classified ad that the group placed in the Arizona Republic recently caught our eye.
The listing for a "family planning assistant" included, among other qualifications, "must be pro-choice."
These days the threat of antidiscrimination litigation means you can't try to specify spirituality or exclude sexual practices in advertising for a renter, but when it comes to advising prospective mothers at Planned Parenthood, only the politically correct need apply.
