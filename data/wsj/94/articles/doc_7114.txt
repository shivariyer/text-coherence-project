DIEBOLD Inc. declared a 3-for-2 stock split and boosted its quarterly cash dividend 10%.
The split is payable Feb. 22 to stock of record Feb. 10.
Diebold said that after the split, it will have about 30 million common shares outstanding.
The Canton, Ohio, company also declared a quarterly dividend of 22 cents a share, which represents a 10% increase in its dividend on a post-split basis.
The new, first-quarter cash dividend is payable March 31 to stock of record March 10.
Diebold develops, makes and services automated self-service and security systems.
