Michaels Stores Inc. said January sales at stores open more than a year increased 6% from January 1993 and total sales for the month were up 32% to $57.4 million from $43.6 million.
Same-store sales for both the fourth quarter and the fiscal year ended Jan. 30 increased 3%, the arts-and-craft retailer said.
Total sales for the fourth quarter were up 29% to $234.7 million from $182.1 million and total sales for the year increased 26% to $619.7 million from $493.2 million.
