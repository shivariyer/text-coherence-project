Edward M. Esber Jr., 41 years old, was named chairman, chief executive officer and president of this multimedia concern's Creative Insight unit, a new company that will produce three-dimensional, full-motion games for personal computers, game players and arcades.
Mr. Esber is currently president and chief operating officer of Creative Labs, the U.S. unit of Creative Technology.
He is the former chairman and CEO of Ashton-Tate Inc., which was acquired by Borland International Inc. in 1990.
