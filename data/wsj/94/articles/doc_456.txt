In regard to your March 16 editorial "Language Wars!"
Prime Minister Edouard Balladur is perfectly right to promote the elegance of the French language, and it is amusing to see you get hysterical about it.
(Incidentally, you might add "Journal" to your list of words to boycott in the U.S.)
Most French citizens are proficient in English anyway, and even the purists will always use English to assist helpless Americans who confuse Cannes and Caen.
