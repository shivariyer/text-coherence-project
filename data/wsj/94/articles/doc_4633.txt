RolfGunther Thumann, 44 years old, was named a vice president in the economic and market analysis group of Salomon Brothers International Ltd. in Frankfurt, the European subsidiary of this securities firm and investment bank.
Mr. Thumann will be responsible for coverage of economic and financial trends in Germany, a new position.
Mr. Thumann has been head of the fiscal and macroeconomic policy division of the Federal Ministry of Economics in Bonn since 1992.
