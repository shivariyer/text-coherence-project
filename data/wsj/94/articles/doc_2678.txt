New World Communications Group Inc. and SCI Television Inc. said they completed a previously reported transaction under which New World became a holding company for SCI Television.
The transaction was reported Feb. 16, when New World Communications was known as SCI Parent Corp.
SCI Television is controlled by investor Ronald Perelman.
The companies also said Andrews Group Inc., the Perelman-controlled majority owner of New World common shares, sold to New World two of its own units: New World Entertainment Ltd. and Four Star International Inc.
In exchange, Andrews Group received about 25.4 million additional shares of New World Class B common shares.
