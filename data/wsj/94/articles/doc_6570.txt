Today's Man Inc. said sales for the four weeks ended Jan. 29 rose 11% to $12.6 million from $11.3 million.
Sales at stores open at least a year declined 6%.
Sales for the 12 months ended Jan. 29 rose 26%, to $167.1 million from $132.6 million, while same-store sales rose 3.8%.
Today's Man operates 21 menswear superstores in three northeastern regional markets.
