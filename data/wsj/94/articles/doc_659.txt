Royce S. Caldwell, 55 years old, has been named president and chief executive officer of Southwestern Bell Telephone Co., the telephone unit of this telecommunications company.
Mr. Caldwell will be responsible for local operations, including long-term business strategies and financial operations.
Currently president, customer services, for the unit, he will succeed Robert G. Pope, 58, who is retiring Thursday.
Edward Mueller, 46, a senior vice president, will succeed Mr. Caldwell, who assumes his new position Friday.
