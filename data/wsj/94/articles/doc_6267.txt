Michael Baker Corp. said its Intelcom Support Services Inc. unit won a $13 million contract to operate and maintain the Navy's radio transmitting facilities in Lualualei, Hawaii, and Barrigada, Guam.
The company said the contract is for nine months, with four option years.
Intelcom will be responsible for operation and maintenance of high-powered, high-frequency transmitters at both facilities, Michael Baker said.
