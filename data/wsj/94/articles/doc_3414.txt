Swedish telecommunications group AB L.M. Ericsson said its subsidiary in Thailand, Ericsson Thai Networks Co., will form a joint venture company with two local partners to manufacture and sell network materials for the Thai telecommunications market.
Ericsson's partners are Loxley Public Co. and Jasmine International Public Co.
According to the Swedish group, each of the partners will have an equal stake in the new company, called Ericsson Thai Network Products Co.
Production will start immediately at a factory in Rayong, southwest of Bangkok.
