Five new directors were elected at this real-estate investment trust, increasing the board's size to nine members.
They are Tom Bradley, 76 years old, who was mayor of Los Angeles from 1973 to 1993; R. Bruce Andrews, 53, president and chief executive officer of Nationwide Health Properties Inc.; Warren Fix, 55, a partner in the Contrarian Group, an investment and management firm; Warner Heineman, 71, a senior adviser at First Business Bank; Fred L. Riedman, 63, a partner in the law firm of Riedman, Dalessi & Dybens.
