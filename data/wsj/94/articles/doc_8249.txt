Transco Energy Co. said it plans $231 million in capital expenditures in 1994, up from $170 million in 1993.
The company said $221 million of the 1994 expenditures will go to its natural gas businesses.
Of that amount, its pipeline businesses, Transcontinental Gas Pipe Line Corp. and Texas Gas Transmission Corp., will receive $217 million and its Transco Gas Marketing Co. and Transco Gas Gathering Co. units will get $4 million.
The remaining $10 million will go to Transco Coal Co.
