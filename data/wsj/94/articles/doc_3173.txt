Novagas Clearinghouse Ltd. said it purchased all the outstanding shares of Great West Energy Ltd. of Mississauga, Ont., from Bow Valley Energy Inc.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Novagas is a natural gas services enterprise jointly owned by Nova Corp. of Alberta and Natural Gas Clearinghouse of Houston.
Great West is an established natural gas marketing company whose customers include commercial and industrial accounts and local distribution companies.
Novagas said it will offer employment to all Great West employees.
The purchase is effective immediately, Novagas added.
