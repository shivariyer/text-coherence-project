Lehman Brothers International named Frederick C. Appel managing director in charge of its equity derivatives business for Europe and the U.K., a newly created post.
The company said Mr. Appel's appointment, effective at the end of the month, underscores Lehman Brothers commitment to derivatives, especially in the international markets.
Mr. Appel most recently worked for Salomon Brothers International, a unit of Salomon Inc., as head of U.K. equity and derivatives business.
Lehman Brothers International, London, is a unit of American Express Co.'s Lehman Brothers unit.
