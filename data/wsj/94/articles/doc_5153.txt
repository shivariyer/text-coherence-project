William E. MacDonald III, 47 years old, executive vice president of this bank and financial-services holding company and president of its National City Bank (Cleveland) unit, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer of the unit, which is the company's lead bank.
He succeeds David A. Daberko, 48, who continues as chairman of the unit.
Mr. Daberko also is president and chief operating officer of the holding company.
