Ohsl Financial Corp. said it plans to buy back about 5% of its common shares outstanding in the open market over the next six months, beginning on or after Wednesday.
The company said it currently has 1,356,600 common shares outstanding.
The thrift holding company said the shares will be purchased at prevailing market prices from time to time.
Ohsl said the repurchased shares will be held as treasury shares and will be available for general corporate purposes.
As of Dec. 31, the company had assets of $169.3 million.
