Hicks, Muse & Co. said that HMW Communications, Inc., its Atlanta-based affiliate, has acquired, or signed definitive agreements to acquire, 10 radio stations in North Carolina and South Carolina.
The private investment concern, based in Dallas, said that HMW acquired six stations from closely held Voyagers Communications, Raleigh-Durham, N.C., for $26.5 million.
Those stations are: WRDU-FM in Raleigh-Durham, N.C.; WLWZ-FM in Greenville/Spartanburg, S.C.; WMAGFM and WMFR-AM in Greensboro/Winston-Salem, N.C.; and WNOK-FM and WOIC-AM in Columbia, S.C.
In addition, Hicks Muse said that HMW has signed definitive agreements with other parties to acquire WTRG-FM in Raleigh-Durham, N.C.; WJMZ-FM in Greenville/Spartanburg, S.C.; and WWWB-FM and WGLD-AM in Greensboro/Winston-Salem, N.C. Terms of those acquisitions weren't disclosed.
