Peoples First Corp. said it entered into a definitive agreement to acquire Libsab Bancorp for about $29.6 million in stock.
Shareholders of Libsab, the parent of Liberty Bank & Trust, will receive 13.101 shares in Peoples First for each share held.
The acquisition, which requires regulatory and shareholder approval, is expected to take place early in the third quarter, the companies said.
As of Dec. 31, Liberty Bank & Trust, Mayfield, Ky., had assets of $141.7 million and Peoples First had assets of $837 million.
