Hawker Siddeley Canada Inc. said Britain's BTR PLC agreed with underwriters to sell its 59% stake in the Canadian company for 25.25 Canadian dollars (US$18.83) a share, or a total of about C$121 million.
Hawker Siddeley said the shares haven't been registered for sale in the U.S.
Based in Mississauga, Ontario, Hawker Siddeley Canada repairs aircraft and makes industrial equipment.
Its shares closed yesterday at C$25.50, down 50 Canadian cents, in trading on the Toronto Stock Exchange.
BTR, an industrial conglomerate, said last month that it planned to make a public offering of its stake in the Canadian subsidiary.
