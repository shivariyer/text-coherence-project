Crocker Realty Investors Inc. said it agreed to acquire the 280,000-square-foot One Boca Place.
The company said the purchase price is about $30 million, and the seller is Glades Pond Co.
The real-estate investment trust also said it has secured a commitment from General Electric Co.'s General Electric Credit Corp. to finance the property.
Crocker Realty said One Boca Place is 95% leased.
