Money market mutual fund assets rose $1.19 billion to $599.66 billion from a revised $598.47 billion in the week ended Wednesday, the Investment Company Institute said.
Assets of 651 retail money market mutual funds increased by $989.3 million to $401.71 billion, the trade group said.
Among retail funds, assets of 404 taxable money market funds rose $1.42 billion to $310.61 billion.
Assets of 247 retail tax-exempt funds fell $433 million to $91.09 billion.
Assets of the 280 institutional money market funds rose $193.4 million to $197.95 billion.
Among institutional funds, the assets of 226 taxable money market funds rose $728.1 million to $175.28 billion.
Assets of 54 tax-exempt funds fell by $534.7 million to $22.67 billion.
