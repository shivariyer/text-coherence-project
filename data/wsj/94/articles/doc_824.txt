Fruit of the Loom Inc. said it reached a licensing agreement with Americal Corp. to produce ladies sheer hosiery under the Fruit of the Loom label.
Fruit of the Loom said it adds sheer hosiery to its existing ladies offerings of panties, bras, slips and socks.
Americal, the third largest producer of ladies' sheer hosiery in the U.S., is based in Henderson, N.C.
