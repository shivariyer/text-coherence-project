Robert L. Costello, 42 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of this engineering concern, succeeding Jimmy D. Allison, 53, who resigned those positions.
The company said Mr. Allison asked for a reassignment to concentrate on "public/private partnership activities, particularly in the area of alternative financing methodologies for transportation infrastructure projects."
He was named a vice president.
Mr. Costello was executive vice president and chief financial and administrative officer of the company.
