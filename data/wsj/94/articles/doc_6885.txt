OHM Corp. said it was awarded a $250 million environmental remediation contract by the U.S. Navy for an initial base period, which may be extended for four additional option periods.
The $250 million potential value extends over the five years.
The contract provides that OHM will clean up environmentally contaminated sites at Navy, Marine Corps and other government installations located in Hawaii, Guam and other Pacific and Indian Ocean areas.
OHM, of Findlay, Ohio, is an on-site hazardous-waste remediation firm with annual revenue of $209 million in 1992.
