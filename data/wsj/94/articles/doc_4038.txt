Quick & Reilly Group Inc. announced the formation of an office of the president, which will comprise the presidents of the company's three subsidiaries as well as the Group president.
Effective tomorrow , the office will include Leslie C. Quick III, 40 years old, new president of U.S. Clearing Corp.; Thomas C. Quick, 39, president of Quick & Reilly Inc.; Peter Quick, 38, new president and chief operating officer of Quick & Reilly Group; and Christopher C. Quick, 37, president of JJC Specialist Corp.
The financial services holding company said the office is being formed to ensure that each executive gains greater knowledge of the three businesses.
Each executive will eventually serve as Group president, with the goal of selecting a chairman to eventually succeed their father, Leslie C. Quick Jr., 68.
