Rubbermaid Inc., Wooster, Ohio, said it intends to form a joint venture with Japan's Richell Corp. to sell, market and develop housewares, leisure and specialty products for the Japanese market.
The venture is scheduled to begin operations in April.
Rubbermaid will hold a 40% equity interest and will have the opportunity to increase its holding to a majority position by the end of the year.
Rubbermaid makes and markets plastic and rubber products, including kitchenware, laundry and bath accessories, microwave ovenware, office products and children's toys.
Richell makes home and leisure products.
