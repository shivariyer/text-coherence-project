Sweden's Foersaekrings AB Skandia said it signed a cooperation agreement with Royal Insurance Global, a unit of Royal Insurance PLC of the United Kingdom.
Under the terms of the pact, Royal Global will provide insurance service for Skandia's Nordic industrial clients in Japan, Singapore, Hong Kong, the Philippines, Spain, Portugal and Ireland, while Skandia will give reciprocal service for Royal Global clients in Sweden, Norway and Iceland, the Swedish group said.
