Steven Perskie, chairman of the New Jersey Casino Control Commission, will join this riverboat casino operator as vice president, general counsel and a director, effective May 2.
Mr. Perskie, 49 years old, is filling a new corporate position.
He also fills a board vacancy created by the resignation of Stanley Fishman.
Mr. Perskie, who was instrumental in drafting the New Jersey Casino Control Act, will help oversee Players's entry into emerging gambling jurisdictions and will advise the company on various political and strategic issues, Players said.
