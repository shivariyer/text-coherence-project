Kaneb Pipe Line Partners L.P. said its ST Services partnership acquired a terminal from Total Petroleum Inc. in Salina, Kan.
Total Petroleum Inc. is a unit of Total Petroleum (North America) Ltd.
Kaneb said the terminal has 65,000 barrels of storage and is supplied by Kaneb Pipe Line.
Kaneb also said ST Services had previously acquired a 70,000-barrel terminal from Star Enterprises in Montgomery, Ala.
The two transactions cost a combined $2.5 million, Kaneb said.
The company said it expects the terminals to jointly contribute $500,000 of cash flow to the partnership annually.
