Ralston Purina Co.'s board authorized the buyback of as many as three million common shares of its Ralston Purina Group stock.
The authorization allows the company to make purchases from time to time at prevailing market prices as determined by management.
The company also said that its previous three million share buyback program, authorized in August 1993, has been substantially completed.
As of Dec. 31, Ralston Purina Group had 100.3 million shares outstanding.
