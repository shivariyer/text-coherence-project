Bearings Inc., Cleveland, completed acquisition of Mainline Industrial Distributors Inc., Appleton, Wis., in exchange for 196,000 shares of Bearings stock.
Bearings, which distributes power-transmission products, industrial rubber and electrical products, expects the transaction to be accounted for as a pooling of interests and to result in approximately $700,000 in nonrecurring costs in the quarter ending March 31.
Unisys Corp., Blue Bell, Pa., won a $127 million, three-year contract to improve customer service for the Savings Bank of the Russian Federation.
The bank is one of the largest in the world, handling 212 million accounts in 43,000 agency locations.
Alamo Group Inc., Seguin, Texas, bought all the shares of SMA, a French manufacturer of heavy-duty mowing equipment, for $4.3 million.
Essex Corp., Huntsville, Ala., got a $3.6 million, 32-month U.S. Navy contract to engineer and produce 172 aviation trainers of eight types for delivery to the Naval Air Station, Pensacola, Fla.
