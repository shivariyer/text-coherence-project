Investment banking company NatWest Markets, a unit, named C. William Schroth to head its North American Capital Markets division as the company moves to expand its activities there.
Mr. Schroth, 45 years old, was formerly managing director of CS First Boston for eight years.
The capital markets division was formerly headed by Richard P. Kurth, 41, who expands his role in that area to include client development.
Mr. Schroth and Mr. Kurth, executive vice presidents, will both serve on the company's management committee, which is responsible for the firm's North American business.
