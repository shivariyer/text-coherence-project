REORGANIZATION: Sony Corp.'s Sony Pictures Entertainment Television Group reorganized its TV-production operations, creating a new unit, Columbia Tristar Television.
It will be headed by Jon Feltheimer, 42, who was named president, and Andrew J. Kaplan, 33, senior executive vice president, who was formerly executive vice president of the SPE Television Group.
Five previously autonomous operations will be consolidated within the new unit: Merv Griffin Enterprises; international productions; interactive television operations; TriStar Television, which Mr. Feltheimer previously headed; and Columbia Pictures Television, whose president, Scott Siegler, is leaving.
