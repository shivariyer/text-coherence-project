SHERRITT Inc., Fort Saskatchewan, Alberta, plans to offer $100 million (U.S.) of debentures due in 2014, and 135 million Canadian dollars (US$99 million) of notes due in 2004, according to a Securities and Exchange Commission filing.
Sherritt said proceeds will be used to finance the purchase of Imperial Oil Ltd.'s fertilizer assets.
Sherritt, a fertilizer, oil and gas and metals concern, said Goldman, Sachs & Co. will underwrite the offerings.
