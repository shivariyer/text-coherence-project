Robert W. Bradshaw Jr. and Grant Hamrick were named directors of this women's apparel retailer, increasing board membership to 12.
Mr. Bradshaw, 60 years old, is a partner in Robinson, Bradshaw & Hinson, a Charlotte law firm.
Mr. Hamrick, 55, is senior vice president and chief financial officer of Charlotte-based American City Business Journals Inc., a publisher of business newspapers.
