UNITED COS.
FINANCIAL Corp., Baton Rouge, La., said it sold $178.3 million of mortgage-backed securities in a public offering through underwriters Prudential Securities Inc.
The financial services and insurance company said the securities consisted of six parts -- one adjustable-rate and five fixed-rate -- with an average yield to investors ranging from 4.825% to 7.2% on the fixed-rate bonds.
The securities have an effective life of 3.5 years and were rated triple-A by Standard & Poor's Corp., Moody's Investors Service Inc. and Fitch Investors Service Inc., the company said.
