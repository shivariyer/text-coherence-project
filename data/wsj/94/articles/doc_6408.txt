EMERGING MEXICO FUND Inc., New York, said it plans to sell about 2.2 million common shares through a rights offering to shareholders.
Holders will receive one right for each share held, and may purchase at a discount one share for each three rights held.
The closed-end fund, which invests primarily in Mexican equity securities, has about 6.7 million shares outstanding and about $156 million in assets.
In New York Stock Exchange trading Friday, the fund's shares closed at $23.875, down 75 cents.
