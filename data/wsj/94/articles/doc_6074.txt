MTV Networks said it reached an agreement for Fingerhut Cos., Minneapolis, to take and fill orders and warehouse merchandise for its new home-shopping programming.
In composite New York Stock Exchange trading, Fingerhut shares rose 9%, or $2.375, to $28.875 a share.
MTV Networks, a unit of Viacom Inc., recently announced plans for a six-month test to sell music and related products over home-shopping programming on its three cable-TV networks.
