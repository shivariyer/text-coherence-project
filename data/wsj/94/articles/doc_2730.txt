A high-ranking Lehman Brothers Inc. managing director quit the firm to head the investment banking division of Furman Selz Inc., a small investment firm.
William A. Shutzer, 46 years old, joins Furman Selz as an executive vice president.
He succeeds Brian P. Friedman, 38 years old, who has been named president of a new merchant-banking division, Furman Selz Investments Inc.
Mr. Shutzer was a member of Lehman Brothers' Management Committee, handled top industrial clients and previously was deputy chairman of Shearson Lehman's mergers and acquisitions department.
Lehman Brothers is a unit of American Express Co.
