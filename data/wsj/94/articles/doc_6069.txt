Singapore Telecom and three other parties Tuesday signed a joint-venture agreement to establish a company that will develop, operate and manage a research and development park.
The partners of TeleTech Park Pte. Ltd. are Singapore Telecom, with a 40% equity; a subsidiary of the government's Telecommunication Authority of Singapore, 10%; Technology Parks Pte. Ltd., 25%; and Singapore Technologies Ventures Pte. Ltd., 25%.
