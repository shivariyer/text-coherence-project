Atlantic Gulf Communities Corp., Miami, acquired 245 acres of residential land in Kissimmee, Fla., for the development of a 940-unit single-family residential community.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The company said most approvals have been obtained, and it expects delivery of finished lots to local builders in the second quarter.
The company said it already signed an agreement with American Heritage Homes for more than 250 lots, with house construction expected to start in June.
Those terms also weren't disclosed.
American Heritage Homes, located Kissimmee, is a developer of single-family residential homes.
