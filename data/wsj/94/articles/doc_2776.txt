F&M National Corp. said its agreement to acquire Hallmark Bank & Trust Co., Springfield, Va., will cost about $19.3 million in stock.
The two banks announced a preliminary merger agreement in January, but didn't announce terms.
Under the newly proposed definitive agreement, F&M, a bank holding company, will exchange shares of its common stock having an aggregate market value of $11.13 for each share of Hallmark Bank.
As of Dec. 31, Hallmark had more than 1.7 million shares outstanding.
The agreement must be approved by regulatory authorities and the stockholders of Hallmark Bank.
F&M reported total assets of $1.4 billion as of Dec. 31, while Hallmark's total assets were $118 million.
