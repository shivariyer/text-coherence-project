Earth Technology Corp. agreed to acquire Summit Environmental Group Inc. in a stock swap.
Both companies are environmental engineering and consulting concerns.
Summit is closely held and is based in Canton, Ohio.
Earth Technology, which has three million common shares outstanding, will issue about 3.262 million new shares in the transaction, though the number of shares may be reduced to less than 2.8 million, based on Earth Technology's stock performance.
On the Nasdaq Stock Market, Earth Technology shares closed at $13.25, up 37.5 cents.
On a pro forma basis, the two companies had combined revenue of $150 million in fiscal 1993; Earth Technology alone had revenue of $62.9 million for the fiscal year ended last Aug. 27.
