MAGAZINES: SmartMoney magazine, a joint publication of Dow Jones & Co. and Hearst Corp., was named magazine of the year by Advertising Age.
The trade publication said two-year-old SmartMoney has become "a major presence in the personal finance category."
Dow Jones also publishes The Wall Street Journal.
WHO'S NEWS: Jacqueline McCord Leo, 47-year-old editor in chief of Family Circle magazine, was named to the new post of editorial director of New York Times Co.
Women's Magazines.
