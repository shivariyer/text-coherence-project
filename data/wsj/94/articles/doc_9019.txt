The number of French corporate bankruptcies, liquidations and legal settlements rose to 6,021 in November 1993 from 4,597 in November 1992, according to seasonally adjusted statistics published by the National Statistics Institute.
The latest figure was also up from 5,629 failures in October 1993.
The institute said that for the 12-month period from December 1992 through November 1993, business failures were up 12.5% from the prior period, representing a failure rate of 3.3% among all French businesses.
