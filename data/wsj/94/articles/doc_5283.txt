U.S. Treasury securities pricing service GovPX said Tullett & Tokyo Securities Inc. will be the fifth interdealer U.S. government securities broker to provide it with U.S. Treasury securities price and volume data.
Tullett & Tokyo, a subsidiary of London-based Tullett & Tokyo Forex International Ltd., joins Garban Ltd., Hilliard Farber & Co., Liberty Brokerage Inc. and RMJ Securities Corp. in providing information to GovPX.
