Circuit City Stores Inc. said February sales rose 39% to $334.9 million from $240.5 million a year earlier.
Sales at stores open at least one year increased 18%.
Sales for the year ended Feb. 28 were $4.13 billion, up 26% from $3.27 billion in fiscal 1993.
Same-store sales increased 8% for the year.
Separately, Circuit City said it will enter the Cleveland market this fall.
Circuit City operates 251 Circuit City Superstores, and 43 other smaller stores.
