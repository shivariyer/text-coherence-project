Thorn EMI PLC said that its EMI Music unit has agreed in principle to acquire Intercord Tongesellschaft GmbH, a unit of Germany publisher Verlagsgruppe Georg von Holtzbrinck GmbH.
Intercord is an independent recorded-music concern.
Thorn, a leisure group, said it will announce the price of the acquisition when it is completed.
In a statement, Thorn said Intercord has annual sales of #58.5 million ($87.2 million), while Thorn has sales of #1.5 billion.
