Two changes are being made in the Dow Jones Equity Markets Index, effective today.
KeyCorp is being deleted from the Eastern regional banks industry group.
It is merging with Society Corp., which will remain in the Central regional banks industry group but is changing its name to KeyCorp.
Rayonier Timberlands L.P. is being added to the forest-products industry group.
The company was spun off from ITT Corp.
RAYONIER Inc. is being added to the forest-products industry group of the Dow Jones Equity Market Index, not its subsidiary, Rayonier Timberlands L.P., as erroneously reported in an article yesterday.
