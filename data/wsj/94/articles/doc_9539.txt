Edwin T. Burton was named managing director of this regional brokerage firm's investment-banking operations, a new position overseeing both its public-finance and corporate-finance operations.
Mr. Burton, 51 years old, on Monday is to assume managerial roles now held by the separate heads of the two operations.
George Woodall, 51, head of public finance, and Allen Rogers, 47, head of corporate finance, will remain with the firm as senior bankers reporting to Mr. Burton, who has been president of Rothschild Financial Services Inc., a financial-services subsidiary of Rothschild North America Inc.
