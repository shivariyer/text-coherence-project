CONTINENTAL MORTGAGE & EQUITY TRUST, INCOME OPPORTUNITY REALTY TRUST and TRANSCONTINENTAL REALTY INVESTORS Inc. (Dallas) -- Oscar Cashwell, 63 years old, was named president of all three companies.
He succeeds William S. Friedman, 50, who resigned to focus his attention on National Income Realty Trust and Vinland Property Trust, where he remains president.
Mr. Cashwell remains president of Basic Capital Management Inc., the adviser to the three real estate investment trusts.
