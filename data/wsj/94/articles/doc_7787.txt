Several executives were named to new positions.
Richard Perkins, formerly executive vice president of the pipeline company's Texas Eastern Transmission Co. unit, was named president of 1 Source Corp., Panhandle's gas transportation and management services unit.
Mr. Perkins, 52 years old, succeeds Fred Fowler, 47, who was named president of Texas Eastern Transmission.
Mr. Fowler succeeds George Mazanec, 57, who was named vice chairman of the parent company in December.
Steve Roverud, 54, formerly senior vice president of Panhandle's Panhandle Eastern Pipe Line Co., was named president of that unit.
He succeeds Paul Anderson, 48, who was named president of the parent company in December.
