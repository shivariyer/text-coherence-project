French municipal services group Lyonnaise des Eaux-Dumez SA said it has entered into a partnership with Thailand's Italthai.
Lyonnaise-Dumez said the alliance will allow the two companies to bid jointly for projects in Thailand to distribute and treat water.
Terms of the partnership weren't disclosed.
Italthai, with annual revenue of about five billion francs ($846 million), is involved mainly in construction activities, but also has hotel and property development business, Lyonnaise-Dumez said.
It said Italthai employs 30,000 people.
