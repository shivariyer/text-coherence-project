Raritan Bancorp said it agreed to acquire Manville Savings Bank.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The transaction, pending the approval of Manville's depositors and regulatory authorities, is expected to close in the fourth quarter.
Raritan Bancorp is based in Raritan, N.J.
Manville, also based in New Jersey, has assets of about $14.4 million.
Raritan's main subsidiary bank, Raritan Savings Bank, has total assets of about $307.3 million.
