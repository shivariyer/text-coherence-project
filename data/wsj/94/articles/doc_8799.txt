Charles W. Grigg resigned as president and chief operating officer of this valve maker to become chairman and chief executive of SPS Technologies in New Town, Penn.
Watts Industries said Timothy P. Horne, its chairman and chief executive, will assume Mr. Grigg's responsibilities.
Watts also named as directors Kenneth J. McAvoy, its chief financial officer; David A. Bloss Sr., an executive vice president; and Wendy E. Lane, chairman of Lane Holdings Inc., a private equity investor.
The appointments expand board membership to eight.
