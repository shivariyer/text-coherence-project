Texas Industries Inc. said it will redeem the remaining $47.5 million of the company's 9% convertible subordinated debentures outstanding due Jan. 15, 2008.
The redemption date is April 11.
The company said bondholders may either exchange their bonds for cash at par value plus accrued interest or may convert them into Texas Industries common at a conversion price of $32.74 a share.
If bondholders choose to convert all bonds into common stock, about 1.45 million common shares will be issued, according to Texas Industries, a producer of steel, cement and concrete products.
