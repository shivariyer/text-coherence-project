Stanley R. Fallis, 53 years old, was named senior vice president of planning for this financial-services holding company.
He succeeds Stephen McConahey, who recently was named president of the Kemper Securities unit.
Mr. Fallis previously was senior executive vice president and chief financial officer at Kemper Securities.
Joseph R. Sitar, 48, was named to the new position of senior vice president and chief accounting officer of Kemper Corp.
He formerly was a vice president and comptroller at the American Life Insurance unit of American International Group.
