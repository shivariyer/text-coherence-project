ProxyMed Pharmacy Inc., of Fort Lauderdale, Fla., acquired Service Drugs, a Tampa, Fla.-based pharmacy-management operation.
ProxyMed said it issued 120,000 common shares for all assets and liabilities of Service Drugs, and offered additional stock incentives of as much as $2.5 million based on future performance.
Through its two units, C&B Medical Consultants Inc. and F&H Medical Consultants Inc., Service Drugs operates six pharmacies that exclusively serve members of health-maintenance organizations.
Service Drugs reported revenue of $4.25 million in 1993.
The company's president, H. Ray Hodges, will be an executive vice president and member of the board at ProxyMed.
