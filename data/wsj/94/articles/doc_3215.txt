Thomas E. Miller was named vice president and controller, succeeding Gary A. McCue, 57 years old, who recently became vice president, corporate value planning and development.
Mr. Miller, 46, was a vice president and assistant controller of Avery, which makes pressure-sensitive adhesives and materials, identification and retail systems, office products and specialty chemicals.
James E. Shaw, 62, was named to the newly created post of group vice president, automotive and graphic systems.
He had been vice president of automotive and graphics systems.
