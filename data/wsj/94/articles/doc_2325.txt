GE Capital, the financial arm of General Electric Co., said it acquired Logeroute, a long-term vehicle-rental company based in Nanterre, France.
GE Capital's European fleet-service business, Avis Fleet Services, completed the acquisition for an undisclosed sum.
GE Capital said Logeroute has assets of $25 million.
Avis Fleet Services operates in France and western Europe with more than 170,000 corporate vehicles under lease and service management.
Logeroute, a unit of Suez Group's Locafrance Equipment, manages 2,300 vehicles.
