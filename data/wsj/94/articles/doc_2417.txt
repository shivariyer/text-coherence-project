EATON Corp. said it entered an underwriting agreement linked to its previously announced public offering of 3.5 million shares.
Price per share was set at $58.50.
Eaton, a maker of vehicle power train parts and controls for industries including transportation, said the lead underwriter for the U.S. tranche of 2.8 million shares is CS First Boston Group Inc.
Lead underwriter for the international portion of 700,000 shares is also CS First Boston.
The Cleveland company granted underwriters an additional 525,000 shares to cover overallotments.
The company said proceeds will be used to repay a portion of the debt issued to finance its acquisition of Westinghouse Electric Corp.'s distribution and controls unit.
On Dec. 31, Eaton had 71.3 million common shares outstanding.
