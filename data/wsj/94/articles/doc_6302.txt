Bank holding company F&M National Corp. said it agreed to acquire PNB Financial Corp., Warrenton, Va., for $41.15 a share in F&M stock in a transaction valued at about $21 million.
The transaction is subject to approval of regulatory officials and PNB shareholders.
PNB is the holding company of Peoples National Bank, which had year-end total assets of $98.1 million.
F&M National had assets of $1.4 billion.
