GOOD TIMES RESTAURANTS Inc., Westminster, Colo., said it completed the sale of 1.4 million units, each consisting of two shares of stock and one series B redeemable common stock purchase warrant, for $3.50 a unit.
The warrants expire Feb. 10, 1997.
With the completion of the sale, the company has 5.8 million common shares outstanding.
Proceeds of the sale will be used to build Good Times Drive Thru restaurants.
The company currently has 10 Good Times Drive Thru locations and 17 Round the Corner Restaurants.
It plans to build seven to nine company-owned Good Times Restaurants in 1994.
