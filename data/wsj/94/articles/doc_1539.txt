MAY DEPARTMENT STORES Co., bolstered by record sales and earnings last year, raised its annual dividend 13%, to $1.04 a share from 92 cents.
The St. Louis department store retailer said the increase reflects the company's "continued strong financial performance and our positive outlook for long-term growth."
It marks the company's 19th consecutive dividend increase.
For the year ended Jan. 29, May posted net income of $711 million, or $2.77 a share, up 18% from year-ago earnings of $603 million, or $2.35 a share.
Revenue rose 3.4% to $11.53 billion from $11.15 billion.
