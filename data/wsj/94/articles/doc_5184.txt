Advanced Polymer Systems Inc. said it has nearly completed final Phase III studies on its ProZone sun protectant product and expects to file a new drug application in the second half of this year.
Advanced Polymer said it hopes to position the product, in both over-the-counter and prescription formats, as one that offers greater protection against melanoma than other products.
ADVANCED POLYMER SYSTEMS Inc., Redwood City, Calif., said it expects to file a new drug application for its ProZone sun protectant product in the second quarter.
An article Wednesday incorrectly said Advanced Polymer would file an application in the second half of this year.
(WSJ Feb. 22, 1994)
