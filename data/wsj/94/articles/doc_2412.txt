This energy and manufacturing company named presidents of two new world-wide business groups created during a restructuring of its operations.
James R. Burke, 56 years old, was named president and general manager of Weatherford Products, a manufacturing and engineering group.
Mr. Burke remains a senior vice president of the parent company.
M.E. Eagles, 54, was named president and general manager of Weatherford Services, an oil field services group.
Mr. Eagles also remains a senior vice president of the parent.
