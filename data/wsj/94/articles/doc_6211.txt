This energy and natural-resources concern named John W. Kennedy president of its new London-based unit.
Mr. Kennedy, 43 years old, will head the Dresser Engineered Drilling Services Division, which will coordinate the company's efforts to provide customers with efficient drilling programs.
Mr. Kennedy was formerly president of Atlas Wireline, a unit of Western Atlas International Inc., until Dresser sold its interest in the oil-services venture to the majority owner, Litton Industries Inc.
Dresser also promoted James L. Bryan, 57, to the newly created position of senior vice president, operations, from vice president, operations, of the company's oilfield-services segment.
