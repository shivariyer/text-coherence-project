Italian information systems and communication company Ing.
C. Olivetti & Co. SpA said it formed a media and communications joint venture with U.S. company Redgate Communications.
The joint venture, to be called Redgate Olivetti Communications, will be 51%-controlled by Olivetti and 49%-controlled by Redgate.
Olivetti said the venture will operate in new-media services and applications development.
The new-media markets -- in which computing, telecommunications, television and information services converge -- are expected to be worth an estimated $10 billion in Europe in 1996.
