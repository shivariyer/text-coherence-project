Coherent Inc. said it filed a lawsuit charging Trimedyne Inc. with violating three patents covering some of Coherent's laser surgery systems.
The suit was filed in U.S. District Court in San Francisco.
Trimedyne, based in Irvine, Calif., also makes laser systems.
A Trimedyne spokesman said the company hadn't yet seen the Coherent complaint but didn't expect it to have any material impact on its operations.
In Nasdaq Stock Market trading Friday, Trimedyne shares closed at $13.875, down 87.5 cents.
