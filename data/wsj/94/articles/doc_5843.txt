Data Broadcasting Corp., a New York provider of real-time financial market data and news, said it signed a definitive agreement to acquire privately held Capital Management Sciences for $24.3 million in cash, stock and notes.
Capital Management Sciences, of Los Angeles, provides fixed-income analytics data to institutions, investment managers and professional investors.
For the fiscal year ended Sept. 30, it had revenue of $8.8 million and operating profit of $2.3 million.
Data Broadcasting had revenue of $14.8 million and profit of $927,000, or five cents a share, for its fiscal first quarter ended Sept. 30.
In Nasdaq Stock Market trading, Data Broadcasting closed at $5.50, up 12.5 cents.
