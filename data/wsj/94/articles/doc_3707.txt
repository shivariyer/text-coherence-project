Adolf Mueller, 62 years old, executive vice president and general manager of this company's Occupant Restraints & Controls Group, will retire effective Aug. 1.
He has been with TRW for 30 years.
Mr. Mueller will be succeeded by John A. Janitz, 51, vice president and general manager, occupant restraints, and Philippe Lemaitre, 44, vice president and general manager, automotive electronics, who together will assume group executive responsibilities.
The two executives will report to TRW's president and chief operating officer, Edsel D. Dunford, and will become members of the corporation's senior management committee, as Mr. Mueller has been.
TRW provides high-technology products and services for the automotive, space and defense and information markets.
