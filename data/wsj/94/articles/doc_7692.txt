Raytheon Co. said the Navy Space and Naval Warfare Systems Command awarded it a $79.7 million contract to produce 62 satellite terminals for the Navy's extremely high frequency satellite communications program.
The maker of missiles, aircraft and appliances said the contract covers terminals for ship, submarine, and shore stations, as well as training, documentation, and related support items.
The terminals, which provide secure communications, will be produced at Raytheon's manufacturing plant in Waltham, Mass., with engineering work to be performed at Marlboro, Mass.
