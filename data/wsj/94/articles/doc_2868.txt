CAMDEN PROPERTY TRUST (Houston) registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission to sell as many as 3,450,000 common shares and as much as $86,250,000 in convertible subordinated debentures due 2001.
The self-administered real estate investment trust said it will use the proceeds, which could approach $175 million, to repay debt and to acquire three multifamily properties.
The lead underwriter for the offerings is Kidder, Peabody & Co.
