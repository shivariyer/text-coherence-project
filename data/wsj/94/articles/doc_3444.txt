Crescent Airways Corp. (Lawrenceville, Ga.) -- Thomas M. Ramirez, 55 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of this helicopter operating and leasing company.
Mr. Ramirez was vice president of corporate development for Pittsburgh-based Corporate Jets Inc.
He succeeds Dean Sheely, who will remain on the company's board.
Crescent also named Ronald L. LaFleur, 52, vice president of Miami-based Mill Brook, vice president-marketing.
