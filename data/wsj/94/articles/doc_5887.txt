The Treasury said it sold $1.22 billion of Series EE savings bonds in January, down 52.1% from a year earlier.
January sales were up 24.2% from December.
The value of Series EE savings bonds held by Americans at the end of January reached $161.98 billion, up 8.6% from $149.22 billion a year earlier.
Savings bonds held five years or more currently pay a market-based rate of 4.25%.
Bonds redeemed during the first five years earn the guaranteed minimum rate of 4%.
