American Financial Corp. said it increased the interest rate of the debentures being offered in the exchange offer for its debentures outstanding.
The rate is being increased to 9.75% from 9.5%.
The cash premium, accrued interest and other terms of the exchange offer remain unchanged.
Additionally, the company called for redemption of all $73.5 million principal amount of its 13.5% debentures and 13.5% Series A debentures due 2004.
The redemption price is 100% of principal amount.
