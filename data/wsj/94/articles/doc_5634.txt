Lomak Petroleum Inc. said its proven reserves increased 245%, to 17 million barrels of oil equivalent on Dec. 31, 1993, compared with a year-earlier five million barrels.
The company said it had reserves of 74.56 billion cubic feet of natural gas, compared with year-earlier reserves of 17.61 billion cubic feet.
Lomak Petroleum also reported reserves of 4.5 million barrels of oil, an increase of 129% from a year-earlier two million barrels.
