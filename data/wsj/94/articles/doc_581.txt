Terrence L. Stecz, 39 years old, was named to the new position of president of the Whitehall-Robins healthcare-products division of the drug, medical-supply and food concern.
Mr. Stecz was most recently president of the company's A.H. Robins Consumer Products unit.
Whitehall-Robins had U.S. sales of about $1.2 billion in 1993.
