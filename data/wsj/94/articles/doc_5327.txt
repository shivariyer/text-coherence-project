This railcar maintenance and retrofitter named Philip C. Kantz president and chief executive officer.
Mr. Kantz, 50 years old, was previously president and chief executive officer of Itel Containers International Corp.
He succeeds Steven L. Pease, who returns to Deucalion Venture Partners in Sonoma, Calif.
Mr. Pease, 50, oversaw Transcisco's recovery from reorganization proceedings under Chapter 11 of the federal Bankruptcy Code last year.
