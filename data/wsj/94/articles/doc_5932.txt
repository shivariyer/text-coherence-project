Edsel D. Dunford, 58 years old, president and chief operating officer of this provider of high-technology products and services for the automotive, aerospace, defense and information markets, said he plans to retire at the end of 1994.
Peter S. Hellman, 44, is expected to succeed Mr. Dunford at the end of the year.
Mr. Hellman was named to the "transitional" position of executive vice president and assistant president, effective March 1.
He is currently executive vice president and chief financial officer.
Also, Ronald D. Sugar, 45, currently vice president of group development of TRW Space & Electronics Group, one of the company's seven main business groups, was named executive vice president and chief financial officer, effective April 1.
He will succeed Mr. Hellman.
