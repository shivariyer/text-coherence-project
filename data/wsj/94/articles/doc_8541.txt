British food and beverage concern Cadbury Schweppes PLC said it's buying Bouquet D'Or of France, a specialty-chocolates company.
Cadbury said it will pay #18 million ($26.9 million), including acquired debt, subject to adjustment based on the completion balance sheet.
Cadbury will pay in cash.
Cadbury said that Bouquet D'Or's sales for the year to April 30, 1993, were #29 million.
Cadbury said that the management of Chocolat Poulain, its established confectionary business in France, will manage and develop the Bouquet D'Or business.
