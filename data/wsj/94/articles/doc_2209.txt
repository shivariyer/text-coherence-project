First Security Corp. announced a definitive agreement to buy CrossLand Mortgage Acquisition Corp., the parent company of CrossLand Mortgage Corp., for about $112 million.
First Security, a Salt Lake City bank holding company with operations in six states, said it will operate CrossLand Mortgage as a separate subsidiary of First Security Bank of Utah.
CrossLand Mortgage, a privately held company based in Murray, Utah, issues mortgages through 60 offices in 18 states.
First Security, which generated $2.1 billion in residential mortgage loans last year, said the acquisition was designed to generate more income from service fees and to expand the reach of its mortgage lending to other states.
First Security's stock closed unchanged at $28.75 yesterday in late Nasdaq Stock Market trading.
