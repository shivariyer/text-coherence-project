Columbus Southern Power Co., a unit of American Electric Power Co., said on Feb. 12 it will redeem the entire principal amount outstanding of its 8 5/8% first mortgage bonds due 1996 and the entire principal amount of its 9% first mortgage bonds due 1999.
The redemption price for the 8 5/8% bonds will be 100% of the principal amount plus accrued interest from Feb. 1.
Redemption price for the 9% bonds will be 101.74% of the principal amount plus accrued interest from Dec. 1.
Also, the company said that on March 1, it will redeem $1 million principal amount of its 9% first mortgage bonds due 2017.
The company will redeem those bonds at the special redemption price of 100% of the principal amount.
Citibank, New York, is the redemption agent, Columbus Southern Power said.
