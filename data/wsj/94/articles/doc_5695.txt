John M. Tetro, 47 years old, was named executive vice president and chief financial officer at this insurance holding company.
This is an additional position for Mr. Tetro, who is also executive vice president and chief financial officer at the Home Insurance Co. unit.
Mr. Tetro succeeds Jan Eveland, who will be relocating to Europe to take a job at Sweden's TryggHansa SPP, which owns about 64% of Home Holdings.
Separately, Mary A. Taylor, 45, was named senior vice president, corporate communications at Home Holdings, a new position.
She will continue to hold that same title at the unit.
Also, Larry Walters was named to the new position of vice president, investor relations.
Mr. Walters, 45, was previously a vice president at American International Group Inc. in New York.
