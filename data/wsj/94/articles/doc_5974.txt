BLAIR Corp., Warren, Pa., a direct-mail-order retailer of men's and women's apparel and home furnishings, declared a quarterly dividend of $1 a share payable March 15 to shareholders of record Feb 23.
The company had paid a dividend of 35 cents a share in the first three quarters.
A Blair spokesman said that a year ago, the company had paid dividends of 25 cents a share for each of the first three quarters, and a $1.55 a share dividend in the fourth quarter.
The lower fourth-quarter dividend in the latest period reflects lower earnings reported by the company, he added.
