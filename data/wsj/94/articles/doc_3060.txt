Nels R. Carlson, 45 years old, was named chief executive of this oil and gas producer, succeeding Thomas J. Jacobsen, 61.
The company said Mr. Jacobsen and Mr. Carlson will remain, respectively, chairman and president, positions they currently hold.
It said Mr. Jacobsen is stepping aside as chief executive in order to devote more time to other business interests.
The company also said John P. McGrain, 47, stepped down as a director in order to pursue other business interests.
