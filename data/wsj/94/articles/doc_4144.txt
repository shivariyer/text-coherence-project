Money-market mutual-fund assets jumped $2.75 billion to $587.95 billion from a revised $585.2 billion for the week ended Wednesday, Feb. 23, the Investment Company Institute said.
Assets of 654 retail money-market mutual funds rose by $2.04 billion to $404.37 billion, the trade group said.
Among retail funds, assets of 406 taxable money-market funds rose by $1.16 billion to $313 billion.
Assets of 248 retail tax-exempt funds rose by $876 million to $91.37 billion.
Assets of the 284 institutional money-market funds rose by $721.6 million to $183.59 billion.
Among institutional funds, the assets of 230 taxable money-market funds rose by $471.5 million to $160.48 billion.
Assets of 54 tax-exempt funds rose by $250.1 million to $23.11 billion.
