IFIL-Finanziaria di Partecipazioni SpA, the industrial holding company of the Fiat Group, increased its voting rights in French food and drinks group BSN.
The Italian company now controls 8.99% of BSN's voting rights, compared with 4.72% previously.
Under BSN's bylaws, investors who hold nominative shares in the company and who keep their shares for at least two years automatically receive increased voting rights.
