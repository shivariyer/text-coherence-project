Alamo Group Inc. said it has reached an agreement to acquire all the shares of SMA, a French maker of heavy-duty tractor-mounted mowing equipment, for $4.3 million.
Alamo, which also makes tractor-mounted equipment, said the acquisition is considered a foreign direct investment and requires French government approval.
SMA had 1993 sales of $7.2 million.
On the Nasdaq Stock Market, Alamo shares closed at $14.75, down 37.5 cents.
