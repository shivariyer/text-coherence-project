Richard W. Blossom was named president of the Asia/Pacific region and executive vice president of Revlon International, a division of Revlon Inc., the cosmetics marketing unit of Revlon Group.
In the newly created position, Mr. Blossom, 45 years old, will be responsible for Revlon International's operations throughout the region other than Japan.
For the last three years, he was president, Asia Pacific for PepsiCo Foods International, a unit of PepsiCo Inc.
