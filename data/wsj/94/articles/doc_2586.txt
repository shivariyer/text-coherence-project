Ryan, Beck & Co. said it will offer to exchange as many as 1,250,000 shares of newly created voting cumulative convertible preferred stock, Series A, for the same amount of common shares outstanding.
The company said it will also offer to purchase as many as 150,000 of its common shares for between $6.25 a share and $7.25 a share.
The offers are expected to begin tomorrow.
Shares of Ryan Beck, a brokerage and underwriting firm, rose 50 cents, or 7.5%, to close at $7.125 in Nasdaq Stock Market trading.
