Phelps Dodge Magnet Wire Co., a unit of Phelps Dodge Corp., said it acquired the assets of Texas Magnet Wire Co. in El Paso, Texas, from Rea Magnet Wire Co. and Fujikura International.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Phelps Dodge Magnet Wire also purchased the Laurinburg, N.C., fine-wire business of Rea Magnet.
