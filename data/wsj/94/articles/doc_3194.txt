Lesley Daniels Webster was named managing director and head of the market-risk management group at the Chemical Bank unit of the banking concern.
Ms. Webster succeeds Michael Zarrilli, 44 years old, who held that position on an interim basis.
Mr. Zarrilli continues as managing director for the unit's Asia, Europe and Capital Markets group.
Ms. Webster, 41, was managing director and head of the proprietary and arbitrage trading group at UBS Securities Inc.
