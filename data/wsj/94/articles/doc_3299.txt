Money-market mutual-fund assets slid $1.12 billion to $586.78 billion from a revised $587.90 billion for the week ended Wednesday March 2, the Investment Company Institute said.
Assets of 654 retail money-market mutual-funds rose by $2.89 billion to $407.2 billion, the trade group said.
Among retail funds, assets of 406 taxable money-market funds increased by $2.10 billion to $315.05 billion.
Assets of 248 retail tax-exempt funds rose by $791.3 million to $92.16 billion.
Assets of the 284 institutional money-market funds fell by $4.01 billion to $179.57 billion.
Among institutional funds, the assets of 230 taxable money market funds dropped by $4.49 billion to $155.99 billion.
Assets of 54 tax-exempt funds increased by $476.1 million to $23.58 billion.
