The New York Stock Exchange announced six new listings, including four initial public offerings.
The Big Board began trading four initial offerings: Gables Residential Trust, Atlanta, a real estate investment trust, under GBP; Glimcher Realty Trust, Columbus, Ohio., a real estate trust, under GRT; Plantronics Inc., Santa Cruz, Calif., a maker of headsets, accessories and telephone products, under PLT; and the Americas Income Trust Fund, Minneapolis, a closed-end fund, under XUS.
The Big Board also began trading Quantum Restaurant Group Inc., Roslyn Heights, N.Y., a restaurant operator previously traded over the counter; and Rowe Furniture Corp., Salem, Va., a furniture maker, under ROW, which previously traded on the American Stock Exchange.
