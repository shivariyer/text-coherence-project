LITTON INDUSTRIES Inc., Beverly Hills, Calif., declared a dividend in the form of a distribution of 100% of Western Atlas Inc. common stock, payable March 17 to Litton stock of record March 14.
Shareholders will receive one share of Western Atlas common stock for each share of Litton common stock held.
As previously reported, Litton's spinoff of Western Atlas was to have taken place in 1993 but was delayed by two purchases.
Western Atlas provides seismic surveys and wireline logging services for exploration, development and production of oil and gas.
