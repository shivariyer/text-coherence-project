British confectioner and beverage company Cadbury Schweppes PLC said its pretax profit jumped 25% to #416.3 million ($619 million) in 1993 as profit margins improved.
Cadbury's pretax profit was at the top end of market forecasts, which ranged from #395 million to #410 million.
Its 9.1% dividend increase to 14.4 pence (21.4 cents) a share from 13.2 pence was in line with expectations.
Cadbury cited strong sales and profits in its U.K. beverages, which includes Coca-Cola and Schweppes Beverages brands.
