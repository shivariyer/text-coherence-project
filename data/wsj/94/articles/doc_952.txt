CAPITAL HOLDING Corp., Louisville, Ky., said Capital Holding LLC filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a public offering of four million shares of cumulative monthly income preferred stock.
Capital Holding LLC is a limited life company and is a newly formed, wholly owned finance subsidiary of Capital Holding Corp.
Lead manager of the offering will be Goldman, Sachs & Co.
The company said the projected initial purchase price of the shares, which will pay dividends monthly, will be $25 each.
Net proceeds of the offering will be lent to Capital Holding Corp. and will be used to provide funding for the redemption of Capital Holding's adjustable-rate preferred stock, Series F. Capital Holding said the new unit was formed primarily to issue the preferred stock.
Capital Holding Corp., with about $22.9 billion in assets, is an insurance and financial-services company.
