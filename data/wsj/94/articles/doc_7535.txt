STEWART ENTERPRISES Inc. said it completed an offering of 2,705,000 common shares on Monday.
An additional 400,000 shares were sold by a major shareholder, the operator of funeral homes and cemeteries said in New Orleans.
After the offering, the company has 21.8 million common shares outstanding.
Stewart said the offering generated about $65 million in proceeds and that it would use the funds for acquisitions.
Bear, Stearns & Co. was the managing underwriter.
