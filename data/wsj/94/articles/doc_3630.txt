Questar Corp. said its subsidiary Universal Resources Corp. agreed to acquire oil and natural gas properties from Union Pacific Resources Corp. for $94.5 million.
The acquisition includes properties in Oklahoma, Texas, New Mexico and Colorado.
R.D. Cash, Questar's chairman, said the transaction is "the largest and most significant reserve acquisition in the company's history, and will accelerate our development as one of the nation's leading independent oil and gas producers."
