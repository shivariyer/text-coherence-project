Ivaco Inc. said it will sell 3,640,000 Class A subordinate voting shares to a group of underwriters for 30.9 million Canadian dollars (US$22.6 million), or C$8.50 a share.
The offering is expected to close about April 6, subject to regulatory approval.
Ivaco said it will use the proceeds to repay bank debt and for investment in its business.
The steelmaker currently has 18.1 million Class A shares and 6.8 million Class B shares outstanding.
