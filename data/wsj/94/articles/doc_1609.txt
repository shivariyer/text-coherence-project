Calvin Klein Inc. said it teamed up with several Japanese companies to establish a joint venture to open 12 Calvin Klein stores in Japan over the next five years.
The closely held apparel company's new partners have expertise in various aspects of retailing and apparel manufacturing.
The partners in the new company, called Calvin Klein Japan Co., include Mitsui & Co., with a 40% equity stake; Onward-Kashiyama Co., with 30%; Isetan Co., with 10%; and Okano Associates Co., with 5%.
Calvin Klein will have a 15% share.
