FEDERAL-MOGUL Corp., a Southfield, Mich., supplier of auto parts, said it plans to offer four million shares of its common stock.
Proceeds from the sale will be used to repay debt.
The underwriters may issue an additional 600,000 shares if they exercise their over-allotment option.
About 3.4 million shares will be sold in the U.S., with the remainder to be sold outside the U.S. Managers of the proposed offering in the U.S. are CS First Boston and Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp.
