Mr. Coffee Inc. said its board of directors hired Equitable Cos.'
Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp. as its financial adviser.
The company last month said it was entering discussions with an unidentified potential buyer.
Mr. Coffee is the leading seller of automatic drip coffee makers in the U.S., with about 34% of the market.
The Cleveland company also sells coffee filters, water-filter machines, iced-tea makers, and a portable potato baker.
Last spring, Mr. Coffee was the subject of takeover rumors, though nothing came of them.
