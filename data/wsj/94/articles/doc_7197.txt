Raw-steel production by the nation's mills increased 8% last week to 1,800,000 tons from 1,667,000 tons the previous week, the American Iron and Steel Institute said.
Last week's output rose 2.1% from the 1,763,000 tons produced a year earlier.
The industry used 87.4% of its capability last week, compared with 80.9% the previous week and 83.9% a year earlier.
The capability utilization rate is a calculation designed to indicate at what percent of its production capability the industry is operating in a given week.
