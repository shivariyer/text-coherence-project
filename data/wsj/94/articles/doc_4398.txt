William A. Schreyer, chairman emeritus of Merrill Lynch & Co., was named to the board of the construction and agricultural-equipment concern.
Mr. Schreyer, 66 years old, served as Merrill Lynch's chairman for eight years until last year, and as the company's chief executive officer for eight years until 1992.
The addition of Mr. Schreyer brings the total number of directors to 10.
