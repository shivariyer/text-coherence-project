Bernard Luskin, 55 years old, was named to the new post of chief executive officer of Jones Interactive Systems Inc., a digital publishing and interactive media unit of this telecommunications company.
Mr. Luskin will also serve as vice chairman of Mind Extension University, a cable education channel controlled by Jones International.
Mr. Luskin was formerly president and chief executive of Philips Interactive Media, and president of Philips Media Education & Reference Publishing Group, both of which are units of Philips Electronics NV.
