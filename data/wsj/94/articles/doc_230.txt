Union Bank of Switzerland reached an agreement in principle to buy the assets and business of Resource Investments Inc.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Closely held Resource Investments manages about $700 million direct timber and mill investments on behalf of large institutional clients.
The firm declined to specify its revenue figures, but industry estimates range between $4 million and $7 million a year.
Union Bank said Resource Investments, of West Lebanon, N.H., will become one of the investment specialty shops of its UBS Asset Management Inc. unit.
UBS Asset Management has more than $30 billion in assets under management; Union Bank of Switzerland has total assets of $208 billion.
