ONEX Corp., Toronto, said it expects to raise 97.5 million Canadian dollars (US$73.6 million) from the sale to a group of underwriters of 5.5 million subordinate voting shares.
Onex said it will have about 41 million subordinate voting shares outstanding after the issue.
The underwriting group is being led by Wood Gundy Inc. and RBC Dominion Securities Inc.
Onex, an industrial holding company, said proceeds will be used to finance future acquisitions.
