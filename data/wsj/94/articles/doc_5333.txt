ProNet Inc. said it signed a letter of intent to acquire the assets of Chicago Communications Service Inc.'s paging operation based in Chicago, which serves 30,000 subscribers.
Terms weren't disclosed.
ProNet, a provider of paging and message services, said in a news release that the transaction is subject to various conditions, including due diligence, a definitive purchase agreement and approval by ProNet's board.
It also is subject to Federal Communications Commission, regulatory and other third-party approvals.
