Exploration Co. of Louisiana Inc. said it successfully completed a natural gas well in South Texas.
The oil and natural gas company said the Rufino & Josefa Lopez No. 3 well tested at a rate of 4.5 million cubic feet of gas a day from depths of between 15,150 and 15,320 feet.
A spokesman for the company said the well in the Berry R. Cox Field in Jim Hogg County will begin producing in about 30 days.
Exploration Co., which operates the well, holds a 53.75% working interest in it.
Columbus Energy Corp. has a 21% working interest; Sheffield Exploration Co. holds a 12.5% interest, and the remaining interest is held by private investors.
