Moody's Investors Service placed the financial-strength ratings of Equitable Life Assurance Society of the U.S. and its Equitable Variable Life Insurance Co. unit under review for a possible upgrade.
The ratings agency said it will focus on improving financial fundamentals and a favorable outlook at the company's individual life-insurance operations.
Equitable Life Assurance Society of the U.S., a unit of insurance concern Equitable Cos., reported statutory assets of about $47 billion as of Sept. 30.
Equitable Life Assurance Society of the U.S. and Equitable Variable Life Insurance Co. have a single-A-3 financial-strength rating.
