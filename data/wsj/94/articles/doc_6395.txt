Consolidated Stores Corp. said sales for the five weeks ended Jan. 29 totaled $63.4 million, down 2% from $64.7 million in the comparable period of 1992.
The company said comparable-store sales for stores open two full fiscal years fell 14% for the five-week period and rose 1.8% for the fiscal year ended Jan. 30.
Consolidated Stores operates a chain of specialty retail close-out stores.
