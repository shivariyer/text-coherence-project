Philips Electronics Korea Ltd., a venture of Philips Electronics NV, said the Dutch company sold a 100% stake in the company to South Korean investors as part of efforts to streamline global operations.
Philips Electronics Korea, which produced resistors and capacitors for supply to its parent, has had losses since its inception in 1975, officials said.
Philips, however, will maintain here its incorporated company, Philips Industries Korea Ltd., which imports Philips products into Korea.
