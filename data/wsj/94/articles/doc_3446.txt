James J. Gallogly, 45 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of the spinal-device company.
Mr. Gallogly succeeds E.R. Pickard, 45, who was named chairman.
Mr. Pickard will retain his post as chief executive officer.
The current chairman, L.D. Beard, 61, is retiring, but will remain on the board.
Previously, Mr. Gallogly was president and chief executive of Resound Inc., a hearing-aid company.
Mr. Pickard had been Sofamor Danek's president and chief operating officer since 1990, and chief executive since 1991.
