PLY GEM INDUSTRIES Inc., New York, called for the redemption of $50 million in convertible debt in a move to strengthen its balance sheet and save $5 million in annual interest expense.
The debt, 10% convertible subordinated debentures due 2000, can be converted to Ply Gem stock at $18.16 a share (55.07 shares for each $1,000 face amount of debentures) or redeemed for $1,048.33.
The redemption date is March 23.
Ply Gem makes and distributes products for the home-improvement industry.
In American Stock Exchange trading, Ply Gem's stock ended unchanged at $25.
