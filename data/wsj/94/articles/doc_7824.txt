PAY-TV: Sony Corp.'s Sony Pictures Entertainment unit agreed to acquire a substantial equity interest in HBO Ole, a partnership of Time Warner Inc.'s Home Box Office pay-television unit and Omnivision Latinamerican Entertainment Inc., Caracas, Venezuela, a Latin American pay-television service company.
HBO Ole is a 24-hour Spanish-language pay-TV network in Latin America.
WARNER BROS.: Time Warner Inc.'s Warner Bros.
Worldwide Retail will open 50 more stores in 1994, including flagship stores in London; Glasgow, Scotland; and Berlin, following the success of its first 60 stores selling merchandise based on Warner Bros. cartoons and films.
