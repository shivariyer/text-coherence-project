Mirror Group Newspapers PLC said it formed a consortium to bid for Newspaper Publishing PLC, the company that owns the Independent and Independent On Sunday titles.
In a statement, Mirror Group in London, said the consortium, made up of itself, Newspaper Publishing management and European shareholders, already owns about 47% of the company.
The consortium will make a bid for the remaining 53%.
Mirror Group didn't disclose any of the details of its bid.
A report in the Sunday Times said the bid could be between 250 pence ($3.73) and 300 pence a share, valuing the group at #55 million ($82 million) to #70 million.
