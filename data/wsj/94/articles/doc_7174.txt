A unit of McDermott International Inc. signed a contract for about $45 million of construction at a power station in Reesedale, Pa., 50 miles northeast of Pittsburgh.
Babcock & Wilcox Construction Co. will demolish and rebuild two boilers at West Penn Power Co.'s Armstrong Station units 1 and 2.
The construction company is a unit of Babcock & Wilcox Co., which itself is a subsidiary of McDermott.
