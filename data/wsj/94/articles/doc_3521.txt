W.H. Smith Group PLC and Virgin Retail Group Ltd. are enhancing their retailing joint venture in the U.K. and Ireland.
The enlarged joint venture will operate the existing 305 Our Price stores, owned by W.H. Smith, and 24 Virgin "megastores" in Ireland and the U.K.
Both chains market records, CDs, tapes, videos and other home entertainment products.
The joint venture will be managed by an executive committee with equal participation by Virgin and W.H. Smith executives, the companies said.
W.H. Smith will own 75% and Virgin 25% of the larger business, which is expected to have sales exceeding 350 million ($521 million) in the current financial year.
