W. James Whittle, 41, was named vice president and chief financial officer of this management and control systems maker.
Mr. Whittle, formerly vice president, corporate controller, succeeds Michael D. Stashower, 67, who is retiring but remains a director.
Also, Michael Ruggieri, 42, was named vice president, strategic marketing, a new post.
Mr. Ruggieri was vice president of customer service.
