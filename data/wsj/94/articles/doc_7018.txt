Inter-Regional Financial Group Inc., its brokerage operations boosted by the stock market's continued strength, reported a 34% increase in fourth-quarter earnings on a 12% upturn in revenue.
Net for the parent of regional brokerage firms Dain Bosworth Inc. and Rauscher Pierce Refsnes Inc. jumped to $12.8 million, or $1.51 a share, from $9.5 million, or $1.11 a share.
Revenue rose to $134.3 million from $120 million.
"Exceptional markets" helped the holding company to a record earnings year, Irving Weiser, president and chief executive officer, said in Minneapolis, but he also cited productivity gains and cost-control efforts.
