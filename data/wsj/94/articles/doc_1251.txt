JeanPierre Rosso, 53 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of the J I Case construction and farm equipment division of this diversified manufacturing and natural-gas pipeline company.
Mr. Rosso, formerly president of Honeywell Inc.'s home and building control business, succeeds Edward J. Campbell, 66, who will retire as president.
As chief executive officer, he succeeds Dana Mead, 58, who remains chairman of Case and president and chief executive officer of the parent.
