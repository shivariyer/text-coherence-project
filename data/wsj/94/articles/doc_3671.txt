General Accident PLC swung to a record high pretax profit of #294.9 million ($438.22 million) in 1993 from a year-ago loss of #29.3 million after a strong performance in the fourth quarter and announced a 2.8% rise in its dividend, to 27.5 pence.
The London insurer said profit in the fourth quarter totaled #88.4 million.
Its world-wide underwriting deficit fell by more than half to #229 million in 1993 compared with a #510.1 million loss the year before.
In the U.K., it recorded an underwriting profit of #59.7 million, a sharp turnaround from the #175.2 million loss in the year before.
