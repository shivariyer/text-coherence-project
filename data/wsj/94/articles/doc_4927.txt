Ply Gem Industries Inc. reported higher-than-expected earnings for the fourth quarter as sales jumped 20%.
The New York-based maker and distributor of specialty products for the home-improvement industry said net income rose 66% to almost $3 million, or 23 cents a share, from $1.8 million, or 16 cents a share.
Analyst estimates had called for net to rise 17% to about $2.1 million, or 18 cents or 19 cents a share.
Yesterday, in American Stock Exchange composite trading, Ply Gem's shares rose $1.25, or 5.1%, to $25.625.
Sales grew to $182.9 million from $151.8 million.
The company attributed the jump to its strategy of cross-selling its various product lines.
