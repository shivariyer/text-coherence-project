The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended March 11, 1994.
The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive.
Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer.
WESTCHESTER COUNTY INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT AGENCY will redeem on April 11, 1994, the following resource recovery revenue bonds, series A, due Jan. 1, of the indicated years: its 10.50% bonds, due 2000; and its 10.25% bonds, due 2005.
State Street Bank & Trust Company, New York, is paying agent.
FEDERAL NATIONAL MORTGAGE ASSOCIATION will redeem on March 11, 1994, the following debenture issues due April 10 of the indicated years: its 8.00% debentures, series SM-1996-M, due 1996; and its 9.25% debentures, series SM-1997H, due 1997.
Federal National Mortgage Association, Washington, is paying agent.
