The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended Feb. 18, 1994.
The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive.
Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer.
DADE COUNTY, FLA., will redeem on, April 1, 1994, its seaport revenue bonds, series 1978, due Oct. 1, 2000.
Chemical Bank, New York, is paying agent.
POPE & TALBOT Inc. will redeem on, March 4, 1994, its 6% convertible subordinated debentures, due March 1, 2012.
Chemical Trust Co., Calif., is paying agent.
PUBLIC SERVICE ELECTRIC & GAS Co. will redeem, on March 1, 1994, its first and refunding mortgage bonds, 4 5/8% series, due 1994.
First Fidelity Bank, Philadelphia, and Morgan Guaranty Trust Co., New York, are redeeming agents.
RIGGS NATIONAL Corp. will redeem, on March 22, 1994, its floating rate subordinated notes, due 1996.
Bankers Trust Co., London, is paying agent.
