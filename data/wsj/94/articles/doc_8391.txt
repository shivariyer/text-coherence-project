On Assignment Inc. signed a letter of intent to acquire privately held 1st Choice Mortgage Personnel Inc.
On Assignment said it will pay $700,000 in a combination of cash and stock for the firm.
On Assignment said it also gave key employees at 1st Choice Mortgage, options to buy a total of $150,000 of 1st Choice common stock.
The transaction is subject to numerous conditions.
On Assignment said it expects the acquisition to contribute "positively" to 1994 revenue and earnings.
1st Choice Mortgage Personnel, of Walnut, and San Fernando, Calif., provides temporary employees to the mortgage banking and financial-services industries.
The company had revenue of $2.6 million for its fiscal year ended March 31, 1993.
