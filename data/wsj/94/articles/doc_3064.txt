Chiquita Brands International Inc. said it called for redemption all its 11 7/8% subordinated debentures due May 1, 2003.
There are currently $125 million of the debentures outstanding.
The debentures will be redeemed May 1 at 104% of par value.
The scheduled payment of interest accrued through May 1 will be paid separately to holders of record April 15, the company said.
Securities Transfer Co., of Cincinnati, is the paying and redemption agent.
Chiquita is an international marketer, processor and producer of food products.
