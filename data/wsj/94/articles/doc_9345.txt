Laura T. Kirsner, 48 years old, was named vice president and controller of this retailer, effective Feb. 1.
Ms. Kirsner, assistant controller since 1983, succeeds Thomas E. Page, 65, who is retiring.
Separately, Philip H. Geier Jr., 58, was named a director, effective Feb. 9.
Mr. Geier, chairman, president and chief executive officer of the Interpublic Group of Cos., fills a vacancy on the 12-member board.
