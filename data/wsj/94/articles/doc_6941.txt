Burlington Northern Railroad said it expects its capital investments to increase 26% to $850 million in 1994.
Gerald Grinstein, chairman and chief executive officer, said capital spending totaled $676 million last year and the company is set to purchase more equipment than ever this year.
For 1994, about $485 million is earmarked for roadway improvements, while $365 million is to be spent for new equipment, including locomotives, covered grain hopper cars and aluminum coal cars.
The railroad is a unit of Burlington Northern Inc.
