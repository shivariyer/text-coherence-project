Elliot B. Ross, 47 years old, was named chief operating officer at this designer, maker and marketer of products made from engineered plastics.
The appointment is effective Monday .
Mr. Ross was also named executive vice president of the company, an Essef spokesman said.
Both are new positions.
Mr. Ross is co-chairman of Cleveland-based Inverness Partners, a privately held supplier of die castings to the automotive industry, a position he will leave upon joining Essef.
