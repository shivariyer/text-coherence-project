Money-market mutual fund assets fell $4.86 billion to $593.35 billion from a revised $598.21 billion for the week ended Wednesday, the Investment Company Institute said.
Assets of 661 retail money-market mutual funds decreased by $436.2 million to $412.8 billion.
Among retail funds, assets of 411 taxable money-market funds slipped $113.3 million to $319.01 billion.
Assets of 250 tax-exempt funds fell by $322.9 million to $93.78 billion.
Assets of 285 institutional money-market funds decreased by $4.42 billion to $180.56 billion.
Among institutional funds, 231 taxable money-market fund assets fell by $4.21 billion to $156.85 billion.
Assets of the 54 tax-exempt funds decreased by $203.6 million to $23.70 billion.
