Louis B. Lichtenfeld, 41 years old, formerly managing director of corporate finance at Rodman & Renshaw Inc., was named chief financial officer of this developer of computer systems for the hospitality, legal and real-estate markets.
Mr. Lichtenfeld fills a position that has been vacant for about three years.
During that time, the duties of financial chief at Sulcus had been divided among several executives, including the controller and chief accounting officer.
