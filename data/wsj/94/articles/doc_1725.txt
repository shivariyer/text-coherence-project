ALIAS RESEARCH Inc., Toronto, said it plans to issue up to 2.3 million shares to raise funds for working capital and possible acquisitions.
An official of the software-design concern, said the issue will be priced in the next two months.
Alias has about 8.5 million shares outstanding.
On the Nasdaq Stock Market yesterday, Alias closed at $17.50, off 37.5 cents.
Underwriters for the issue are Robertson, Stephens & Co. and Salomon Brothers Inc.
