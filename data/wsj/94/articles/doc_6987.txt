The Treasury plans to pay down $1.08 billion on the public debt with the sale Monday of about $25.2 billion in short-term bills.
Maturing bills outstanding total $26.27 billion.
The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week bills (Cusip 912794K52) maturing May 12, 1994, and 26-week bills (Cusip 912794M76) maturing Aug. 11, 1994.
Noncompetitive tenders must be received by noon EST Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders must be received by 1 p.m. EST.
The bills are available in minimum denominations of $10,000.
