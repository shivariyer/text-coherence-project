McClatchy Newspapers Inc., Sacramento, Calif., said the company and certain shareholders plan to sell 1,350,000 shares of Class A common stock.
In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, the newspaper publisher said it will issue 750,000 new shares, with the remainder to be sold by members of the McClatchy family and a family-established charitable foundation.
The offering will be managed by Goldman, Sachs & Co. and Merrill Lynch & Co.
The company said its share of the proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes.
