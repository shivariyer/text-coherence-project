Canadian primary steel production totaled 246,071 metric tons in the week ended Saturday, a 7.8% decline from 266,846 tons the previous week and an 11% decline from 277,021 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a government agency, said.
A metric ton is equal to 2,204.6 pounds.
For the year to date, primary steel output totaled 1,516,055 tons, a 7.1% decline from 1,631,345 tons a year earlier.
