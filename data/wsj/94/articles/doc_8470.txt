Anthem Electronics Inc., San Jose, Calif., said fourth-quarter net income plummeted 94%, mainly because of a $7.8 million charge for the sale of its Eagle Technology unit.
The distributor of computer parts said it earned $472,000, or four cents a share, compared with $7.9 million, or 64 cents a share, a year earlier.
Sales rose 23% to $184.9 million from $150.0 million.
For 1993, Anthem earned $18 million, or $1.46 a share, down 41% from $30.4 million, or $2.48 a share, in 1992.
Sales rose 23% to $663.2 million from $538.4 million.
The sale of Eagle Technology to Artisoft Inc. was completed Jan. 5.
