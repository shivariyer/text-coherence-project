QualMed Inc., Pueblo, Colo., said its stockholders approved the company's proposed $775 million merger with closely held HN Management Holdings Inc., Woodland Hills, Calif.
Both companies operate health-maintenance organizations.
QualMed said that about 75% of its shares outstanding were voted in favor of the stock merger.
The company said it expects the transaction to be completed this week following clearance by regulators.
Under the merger, shareholders of each company are to receive stock, valued at $775 million, in the combined company.
