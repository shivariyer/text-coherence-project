Steven G. Staves, formerly vice president of marketing for the Consumer Health Group of this company's Sterling Winthrop Inc. unit, was named president of Sterling Health USA, the U.S. consumer health products division of Sterling Winthrop.
He succeeds Douglas L. Meyer, who resigned from the company to pursue other interests.
Sterling Winthrop develops, makes and markets pharmaceuticals and consumer health products.
Eastman Kodak is a photography, health-care products and chemicals concern.
