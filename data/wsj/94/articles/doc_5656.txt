Robert L. Gerry III, president and chief operating officer, was named vice chairman of the board of this oil and natural-gas company and its management company, Torch Energy Advisors Inc., a unit of Torchmark Corp.
Michael D. Watford, 40 years old, succeeded Mr. Gerry, who is 56.
Mr. Watford, formerly president of Torch Energy Marketing Inc., a unit of Torch Energy Advisors, was also named a director, increasing the number of board members to nine.
