Associated Natural Gas Corp. signed a definitive agreement to acquire Grand Valley Gas Co. in a stock transaction valued at about $56 million.
Associated said the combined natural-gas marketing companies will market about 2.3 billion cubic feet of natural gas daily in North America.
Associated is based in Denver; Grand Valley is in Salt Lake City.
Under terms of the agreement, Associated will exchange 0.25 share for each share of Grand Valley.
Associated said it will issue about 1.6 million shares.
The companies said they hope to complete the deal by July 1.
An Associated spokesman said that based on Friday's closing stock prices, the transaction has a value of about $56 million.
