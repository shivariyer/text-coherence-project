Revco D.S. Inc. said it has acquired five Friendly Drug Stores in the Greater Cleveland area.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The company said the stores will be operated as Revco Drug Stores.
The acquisition is part of its strategy to strengthen its presence in northeastearn Ohio, it added.
During the past 12 months, Revco has added 16 stores in northeastern Ohio, bringing the total number of stores to 119.
