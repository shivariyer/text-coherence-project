Swiss drugs and chemicals group Ciba-Geigy AG announced two new ventures in China.
In one transaction, Ciba set up a company, Ciba Agro (Jiangsu) Ltd., with capital of 18 million Swiss francs.
Plant protection products will be produced at and marketed beginning in 1996.
In the second transaction, Ciba pigments division and Qingdao Dyestuffs Factory signed a pact to set up a joint venture.
