Alex. Brown Inc. formed ABS Capital Partners L.P. to carry out the brokerage firm's merchant-banking business.
The expanded banking business has an initial funding of $111 million.
Alex. Brown, which formed its merchant-banking operation in 1990, has taken ownership positions in five companies through the merchant-banking business.
The brokerage firm will be the largest institutional limited partner in ABS Capital.
Other investors include pension funds, individuals and financial institutions.
