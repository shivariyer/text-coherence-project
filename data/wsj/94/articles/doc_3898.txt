Ray Farnham was named president and chief executive officer of this maker of portable and desktop computer products.
Mr. Farnham, 46 years old, succeeds Opti founder and former President Jerry Chang, 41, who remains chief operating officer of the company.
Mr. Farnham, formerly president of the communications and computing group of National Semiconductor Corp., also succeeds company founder and former Chief Executive Kenny Liu, 40, who continues as chairman.
