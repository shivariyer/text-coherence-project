U.S. Shoe Corp. said retail sales at stores open more than one year increased 4% for the four weeks ended Feb. 26 compared with the year-earlier period.
Total retail sales for the four-week period at the women's apparel, footwear and optical products concern declined 2% to $139.4 million from $142.3 million.
The company said it had 2,242 stores open during the period, down 8.7% from 2,455 stores in the same period last year.
