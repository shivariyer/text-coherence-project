Mobil Corp. said an affiliate signed an exclusive agreement to distribute Mobil-brand lubricants in India.
Mobil said its Mobil Petroleum Corp. and Indian Oil Corp., the largest oil company in India, will establish a joint venture, Indo-Mobil Pte. Ltd.
Mobil said Indo-Mobil will sell Mobil lubricants through Indian Oil's 11,000 service stations and outlets and will also sell the lubricants to businesses throughout India.
The agreement also includes plans to build a lube oil blending plant near New Delhi, Mobil said.
