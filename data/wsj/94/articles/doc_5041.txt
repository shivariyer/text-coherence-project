Royal International Optical Inc. said it completed a recapitalization that reduced its debt by $9 million and its preferred stock obligations by $7 million.
The company said it established a new $12.5 million revolving credit and term loan agreement with Barclay's Business Credit Inc.
The company sold assets that brought about $5 million in proceeds and issued $13 million of 9% cumulative preferred stock, while redeeming $20 million of 13% preferred stock.
Royal International Optical operates optical stores in leased spaces in department stores.
