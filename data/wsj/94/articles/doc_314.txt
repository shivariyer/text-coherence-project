Richard J. Ferayorni was named president of Madison Group Associates Inc.
Mr. Ferayorni is a Madison shareholder and real-estate investor.
Separately, Madison revalued its assets sharply downward and reported a net loss of $4.5 million, or $1.26 a share, for the first quarter ended Dec. 31, 1993.
That compares with a loss of $38,402, or six cents a share, during the year-earlier period.
Formerly a real-estate concern, Madison is trying to remake itself as a provider of country and gospel music programming to television stations and retail stores.
