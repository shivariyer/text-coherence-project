Spar Aerospace Ltd. said its ComStream subsidiary has received a 10 million Canadian dollar (US$7.5 million) contract to upgrade the telecommunications system of Indonesia.
It said the contract calls for Spar to supply Indonesia's public common carrier, PT.
Telkom, with satellite-based trunking equipment to provide toll-quality telephone services, via 20 transmission locations, throughout the islands of Indonesia.
The contract is financed by a loan from the Asian Development Bank, Spar added.
