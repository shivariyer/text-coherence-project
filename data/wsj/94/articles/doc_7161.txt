British publishing company United Newspapers PLC said its United Advertising Periodicals unit finalized the acquisition of Harmon Publishing Co. for $100 million.
Harmon, a unit of closely held Hartz Group Inc. of New York, publishes 216 local real-estate and automotive advertising periodicals in 31 states.
United Advertising in Dallas also publishes advertising periodicals.
Harmon, which reported revenue of $72.1 million for 1993, employs 900.
