Ford Motor Co. said its exports of North American-built vehicles reached a record last year, rising 42% from the previous year.
Ford said it shipped 70,999 cars and light trucks to export markets, up from 50,000 vehicles in 1992.
The top five exported models were the Ford Probe, Tempo, Explorer sport-utility, Taurus and Ranger pickup.
Ford's largest export market is Latin America, which accounted for 30%, or 21,038 exported vehicles.
