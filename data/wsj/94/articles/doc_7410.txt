Sun Healthcare Group Inc. said it signed a definitive agreement to acquire Mediplex Group Inc. for about $316 million in cash and stock.
The Albuquerque, N.M., operator of nursing homes announced Jan. 4 that it had tentatively agreed to acquire Mediplex, based in Wellesley, Mass., for $11 in cash plus 1.28 Sun shares for each share of Mediplex, which has about 9.7 million common shares outstanding.
Mediplex provides sub-acute care, which involves the treatment of patients dependent on ventilators, undergoing chemotherapy or recovering from burns or surgery.
The transaction remains subject to the approval of regulatory agencies and of the shareholders of both companies.
