Allegheny Power System Inc. said Alan J. Noia, 47 years old, was elected president and chief operating officer of this electric utility holding company and its subsidiary, Allegheny Power Service Corp., effective Sept. 1, 1994.
A successor to Mr. Noia, who has served as president of the Potomac Edison Co., an Allegheny subsidiary, since June 1990, wasn't named immediately.
Mr. Noia joined the company in 1969.
As president of the parent, he succeeds Klaus Bergman, who remains chairman and chief executive officer.
Allegheny's principal subsidiaries include Allegheny Power Service Corp., Potomac Edison, Monongahela Power Co. and West Penn Power Co.
