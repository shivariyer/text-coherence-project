Frederick A. Brunn, executive vice president, was promoted to president of this provider of lottery, wagering and ticketing equipment and services.
Mr. Brunn, 49 years old, succeeds James T. Walters, 61, who continues as chairman and chief executive.
The company also elected two new directors to the board, expanding it to nine members.
They are M. Mark Michalko, 39, who was also named vice president of business development, and Tan Sri Dato Vincent Tan, 42, group chief executive officer of Berjaya Group Bhd.
Separately, the company said it intends to change its name to International Lottery & Totalizator Systems Inc. at its annual meeting in May, pending shareholder approval.
