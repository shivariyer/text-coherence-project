United Dominion Industries Ltd. said it agreed to sell an 85% stake in its Dominion Bridge construction unit in Canada to Cedar Group Inc. of Conshohocken, Pa.
United Dominion said it will receive five million Canadian dollars (US$3.8 million) in cash and preferred shares of Cedar exchangeable into common shares.
United Dominion said it will take a fourth-quarter charge of up to US$5 million related to the sale.
United Dominion, a manufacturing, construction and engineering concern, is incorporated in Ontario and based in Charlotte, N.C.
The sale is subject to regulatory approvals and completion of definitive agreements.
The company said it ended a previous tentative agreement to sell the 85% stake in Dominion Bridge to a company related to Manson Group of Montreal.
