Chris-Craft Industries Inc. said fourth-quarter net income rose 36%, aided by sharp gains in operating earnings from its television division.
The New York video concern said profit rose to $24.1 million, or 84 cents a share, from $17.7 million, or 63 cents a share.
For the fourth quarter, television division operating profit rose 73% to $25.3 million, reflecting a 4% gain in operating revenue and a 3% reduction in programming expenses.
During the quarter, income from Time Warner Inc. securities, including sales, fell to $2.9 million from $28.5 million a year earlier.
For the year, net income more than doubled to $149 million, or $5.26 a share, from $65.2 million, or $2.32 a share.
