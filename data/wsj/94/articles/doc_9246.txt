Body Shop International PLC said same-store U.K. sales rose 4% and total sales rose 6% in the nine-week period ended Dec. 25.
The U.K. retailer said its U.S. shops produced same-store sales growth of 10%, with total sales up 47%.
In the year-to-date, U.S. same-store sales have risen 6% and total sales have grown 46%.
