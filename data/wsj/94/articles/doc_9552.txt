Phillips Petroleum Co., as expected, named W.W. Allen chairman and chief executive officer, effective May 1.
Mr. Allen, 57 years old, is the oil company's president and chief operating officer; he will succeed Chairman and Chief Executive C.J. (Pete) Silas, 61, who retires in April.
Succeeding Mr. Allen as president and chief operating officer will be the company's chief financial officer, J.J. Mulva, 47.
Mr. Mulva, who will retain his current position, was also named executive vice president, effective immediately.
In another appointment, J.L. Whitmire, 53, formerly senior vice president, worldwide exploration and production, was promoted to executive vice president, effective immediately.
Both Messrs. Mulva and Whitmire were elected to the board, increasing the size to 15.
