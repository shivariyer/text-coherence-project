Rust International Inc.'s overseas unit, Rust Ltd., completed its acquisition of U.K.-based Kennedy & Donkin Ltd.
Terms of the transaction weren't disclosed.
Kennedy & Donkin, an engineering concern, also has offices in Hong Kong and Dubai.
Rust International is based in Birmingham, Ala.
Kennedy & Donkin will become part of Rust International's overseas network.
Rust International, based in Birmingham, Ala., is a unit of WMX Technologies Inc.
