Bancol SA said it purchased a majority stake in Banco de Colombia, closing the privatization of the Colombian bank in a transaction valued at more than $360 million.
Bancol, a Bogota, Colombia, holding company owned by industrial and banking investors Isaac and Jaime Gilinski, said it purchased and will hold the 75% Banco de Colombia stake.
Banco de Colombia has assets of $2 billion, 6,200 employees, 246 branches in Colombia, a bank in Panama, a bank in the Cayman Islands and five-branch Eagle National Bank in Miami.
