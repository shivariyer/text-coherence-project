American Electric Power Co. said its Columbus Southern Power unit will redeem its entire $99 million outstanding principal amount of 9% first-mortgage bonds due 2017 that weren't previously called.
The redemption date is March 26.
The bonds will be redeemed at 105.85% of the principal amount plus accrued interest from March 1 through March 26.
Citibank, New York, is the redemption agent, American Electric Power said.
