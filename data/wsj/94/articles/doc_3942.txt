Reebok International Inc.
Chairman Paul Fireman and his wife, Phyllis, completed their sale of four million shares of Reebok stock Friday, at $33.125 apiece, raising $132.5 million.
The sale reduces the Firemans' combined stake in the company to 17% from 21%.
Mr. Fireman now holds 7.9 million shares, or a 9.5% stake, and Mrs. Fireman holds 6.3 million shares, or 7.4%.
