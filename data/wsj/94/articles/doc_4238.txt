HOME SHOPPING: Specialty retailer Spiegel Inc. and Time Warner Inc.'s Time Warner Entertainment Co.
L.P. chose four markets for the debut of their catalog-based cable-TV shopping network: Rochester, N.Y.; Milwaukee, Wis.; Nashua, N.H.; and Columbus, Ohio.
Also, Bombay Co. will join eight other catalog companies participating in the network.
The "Catalog 1" service begins March 28.
