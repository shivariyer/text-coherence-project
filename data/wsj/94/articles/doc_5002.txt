Penn Central Corp. said on March 25 it will redeem all $16.2 million of its outstanding 9 1/2% subordinated debentures due Aug. 1, 2002.
The debentures will be redeemed at a price of 100% of the principal amount plus accrued and unpaid interest through the redemption date.
The company said it will fund the redemption with internal cash reserves and proceeds from the liquidation of temporary investments.
Penn Central is engaged primarily in the specialty property and casualty insurance businesses.
