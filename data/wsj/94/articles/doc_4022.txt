The Treasury plans to raise $1.68 billion in new cash with the sale Thursday of about $16.5 billion of 52-week bills to redeem $14.83 billion in maturing bills.
The Cusip number for the bills is 912794Q80.
The bills will be dated March 10 and will mature March 9, 1995.
They will be available in minimum $10,000 denominations.
Noncompetitive tenders must be received by noon EST Thursday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders must be received by 1 p.m.
