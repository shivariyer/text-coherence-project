Westinghouse Electric Corp., Pittsburgh, said it completed the sale of its Westinghouse Electric Supply Co. to an affiliate of Clayton, Dubilier & Rice Inc., a New York private investment firm.
The total purchase price was $340 million.
The new company, Wesco Distribution Inc., operates 260 wholesale distribution locations throughout the U.S. and Canada.
The company employs over 3,000 people and has annual sales of $1.6 billion.
B. Charles Ames, a Clayton, Dubilier principal who will serve as chairman of Wesco, said it "has the potential to become a distribution powerhouse because of its broad product offering, strong network of distributors and solid base of national accounts."
Clayton, Dubilier manages more than $1 billion in equity capital, and since 1978 has invested in 20 businesses with total annual sales volume exceeding $10 billion.
