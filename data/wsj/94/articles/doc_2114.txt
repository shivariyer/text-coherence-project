South Korea's trade balance registered a $480 million deficit in February, marking two straight months of deficits, the Korea Customs Administration said.
The February deficit compared with the previous year's deficit of $550 million, the customs administration added.
Exports for February rose 4.9% to $6.23 billion on a customs clearance basis from $5.94 billion in the previous year.
Imports were $6.71 billion in February, up 3.4%.
