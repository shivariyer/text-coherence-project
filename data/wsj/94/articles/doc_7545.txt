Kansas Gas & Electric Co., a unit of Western Resources Inc., said it is redeeming early bonds valued at $27,965,000, which it issued jointly with Kansas City Power & Light Co.
The utility company said it would pay a 100% redemption price plus accrued interest to the redemption date for 50% of the 5 3/4% pollution-control revenue bonds series 1973 issued to the city of LaCygne, Kansas.
Kansas City Power & Light will pay for the remaining 50%.
The redemption date is March 1.
