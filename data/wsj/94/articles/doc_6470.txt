Circuit City Stores Inc. said January sales were $357.5 million, a 24% increase from year-earlier sales of $287.9 million.
Sales at stores open more than one year rose 6%.
Circuit City also said it reached an agreement to assume the leases of 18 Los Angeles area Silo stores, as well as the extended-service contracts on merchandise previously sold from those stores, effective March 1.
Both Silo and Circuit City are retailers of consumer electronics and appliances.
