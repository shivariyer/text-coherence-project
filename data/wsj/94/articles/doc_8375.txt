Univest Corp.'s Union National Bank unit reached an agreement to acquire Continental Bank's Plumsteadville, Pa., branch office.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Univest offers financial services to 16 Montgomery, Pa., and Bucks County communities.
Continental Bank is a unit of Midlantic Corp.
In a news release, Univest said the acquisition would increase Union National's deposits by about $16 million.
Subject to regulatory approval, Union National plans to assume complete operations of the branch in the second quarter.
