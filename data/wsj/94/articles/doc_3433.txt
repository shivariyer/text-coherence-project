David J. Wagner, 39 years old, has been named to succeed John C. Canepa as president of this bank holding company.
Mr. Wagner will continue as president and chief executive officer of the bank's Old Kent Bank & Trust Co. subsidiary.
Old Kent also said Mr. Wagner will become chief executive officer next March, when Mr. Canepa, 63, will step down as chief executive.
Mr. Canepa will remain chairman until his retirement in 1997.
