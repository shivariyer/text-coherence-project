Alco Standard Corp. acquired Alexander's Office Systems Inc. of Charlotte, N.C., and Office Automation Inc. of Wichita, Kan., both copier and office supply dealers.
Terms were not disclosed.
Alco Standard said Alexander's is a distributor of Canon copiers and facsimile machines and will become a branch of Alco Standard's Modern Office Machines Inc. unit.
Alco Standard said Office Automation is a distributor of Ricoh and A.B. Dick products and will become a core operating company in Alco Standard's Alco Office Products unit.
