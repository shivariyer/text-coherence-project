Dean Witter Discover & Co. reported a 60% jump in fourth-quarter net income although trading revenue fell 13%.
The New York securities and credit-card company said net income rose to $138.5 million, or 81 cents a share, from $86.8 million, or 51 cents a share, a year ago.
Revenue rose 14% to $1.52 billion from $1.33 billion.
The company said earnings from credit-service businesses rose 79% in the quarter to $63.6 million while securities earnings rose 46% to $74.9 million.
Revenue from commissions rose 28% to $237.1 million while principal-transactions revenue fell to $89.5 million from $103.3 million, because of reduced sales of fixed-income securities.
Investment-banking revenue rose 41% to $90.9 million and asset management and custodial fees rose 28% to $223.9 million.
