Comcast Corp. said it formally adopted a plan to buy the 8% of Comcast Cablevision of Philadelphia that it doesn't already own.
Comcast, a cable communications systems concern, said in October that it planned to pay shareholders of Comcast Cablevision of Philadelphia $95 a share, or a total of about $14 million, for the 150,000 Comcast Cablevision shares.
It said it expects the merger to be completed in about 60 days.
Comcast Cablevision of Philadelphia is the partnership that owns a cable television system serving parts of Philadelphia.
