REPUBLIC NEW YORK Corp., New York, raised its quarterly dividend 22%, to 33 cents a share from 27 cents a share, payable April 1 to shares of record March 15.
The banking company said fourth-quarter net income rose 20%, to $79.9 million, or $1.38 a share, from a year-earlier $66.8 million, or $1.14 a share.
The provision for loan losses was $15 million in the fourth quarter, compared with $35 million a year earlier.
Total assets at year's end rose to $39.49 billion from a year-earlier $37.15 billion.
Republic reaffirmed its intention to purchase up to 500,000 of the company's common shares to lessen the effect on per-share earnings of charges for employee-benefit plans.
