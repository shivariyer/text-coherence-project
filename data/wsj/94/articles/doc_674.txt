Lone Star Industries Inc. said it expects to emerge from bankruptcy proceedings no later than April 15, partly due to a regulatory delay.
The cement maker had expected to emerge from Chapter 11 by March 29.
Union Pacific Corp.'s Union Pacific Railroad reached a four-year, $300 million agreement to purchase at least 140 of General Electric Co.'s 4,400-horsepower alternating-current traction locomotives beginning in 1995.
PennFirst Bancorp Inc. completed its acquisition of ESB Bancorp Inc. in a transaction valued at about $25 million.
York International Corp. said its Italian subsidiary, York International SRL, has agreed to purchase the assets of Italian air-conditioning equipment maker Seveso Clima.
Price Waterhouse has been awarded an $11 million contract to automate the Alameda County Court system in California.
