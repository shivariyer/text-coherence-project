Anne M. Veneman and Robert E. Baker were elected directors of this agribusiness biotechnology concern, expanding the board to 10 members.
Ms. Veneman, 45 years old, is a counsel with the Washington law firm Paton, Boggs & Blow and is a former deputy secretary of the U.S. Department of Agriculture.
Mr. Baker, 47 years old, is a vice president of marketing and strategy for Specialty Foods Corp., Deerfield, Ill.
