IBM Federal Systems Co. said it won a $154.8 million contract to build and install 3,144 automated mail sorters for the U.S. Postal Service.
The International Business Machines Corp., subsidiary, which specializes in software for sensors and communications devices, will install the devices in post offices throughout the country over the next 28 months.
IBM recently signed a definitive agreement to sell its Federal Systems division to Loral Corp. for $1.56 billion.
