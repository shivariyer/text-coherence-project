Alusuisse-Lonza Holding Ltd. said the Lawson family of London, Ontario, agreed to tender its stake in Lawson Mardon Group Ltd. to a takeover bid by a unit of Alusuisse.
The family holds about two million, or 16.4%, of the Class B shares of Lawson Mardon.
Cragnotti & Partners Capital Investment SA of Luxembourg already tendered its 52.8% voting control of Lawson Mardon, a Toronto packaging concern.
The bid expires tomorrow.
Alusuisse is a Swiss manufacturing concern.
