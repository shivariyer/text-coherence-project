Banco Espirito Santo of Portugal said its unconsolidated profit rose 15% in 1993 to 20.2 billion pesetas ($141.7 million) from 1992.
Espirito Santo didn't specify if the profit was net or pretax and didn't give a year-earlier figure.
Consolidated results aren't yet available, but will be only marginally different from parent bank results, the bank said.
Espirito Santo's unconsolidated cash flow rose 16% in 1993 to 59 billion pesetas, the bank said without giving year-earlier figures.
Banking-activity revenue grew 7.6% in 1993, while operating costs grew 1.6%, the bank said without providing escudo figures.
