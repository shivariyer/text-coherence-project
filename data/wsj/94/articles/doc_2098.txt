Jerry Silverman was named president of the Stride Rite International Corp. unit of the footwear maker, effective April 4.
Mr. Silverman, 37 years old, was previously national sales manager for Men's Dockers in the Dockers division of Levi Strauss & Co.
He succeeds Alain Ronc, 46, who resigned in January to pursue other interests, Stride Rite said.
