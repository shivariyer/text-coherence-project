David F. Brigante, chief executive officer of this maker of printing products, was named to the additional post of chairman.
Mr. Brigante, 41 years old, succeeds Hubbard C. Howe, 65, who was named vice chairman.
The company also named John P. Rochon a director, increasing the size of the board to eight members.
Mr. Rochon, 42, is chief executive of Mary Kay Corp.
