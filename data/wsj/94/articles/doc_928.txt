Sweden's industrial production rose 0.8% in January from December after a 0.3% advance the month earlier, according to preliminary figures released by SCB, the national statistics agency.
A sector breakdown showed production gains in most sectors.
The sharpest increase, 8%, was reported by the mining and mineral sector.
Production was up 4% in the iron, steel and metal sector, while the manufacturing engineering sector advanced 2%, said SCB.
Declining production was reported in the pulp and paper industry, which was down 3%.
