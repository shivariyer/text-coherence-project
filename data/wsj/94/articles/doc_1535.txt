Lucky Ltd. said in Seoul, South Korea, that it signed an agreement with China's Zhejiang 2nd Light Industry Enterprises Group for a $3.5 million cosmetics joint venture.
The venture, for which Lucky will finance 62% of equity, will start full operations early next year, Lucky officials said.
In addition, Lucky will receive 5% of annual sales in royalty from its Chinese partner in return for its technology transfer.
The output will be sold in China, Taiwan and Southeast Asian countries, officials said.
