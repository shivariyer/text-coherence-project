Philip Morris Espana SA, the Spanish unit of food and tobacco concern Philip Morris Cos. of the U.S., sold its 1.8% stake in the Spanish state-run tobacco monopoly Tabacalera SA.
A Tabacalera spokesman said the stake was sold to institutional investors and declined to disclose the price.
Philip Morris Espana, 50%-held by Philip Morris and 50%-held by Tabacalera, produces and markets Philip Morris brands in Spain.
