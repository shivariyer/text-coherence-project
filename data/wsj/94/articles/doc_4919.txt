Turner Corp. unit Turner Construction Co. will be general contractor for a $90 million federal courthouse and office building in Minneapolis.
The value of the contract to the company wasn't disclosed.
New York-based Turner said its unit is part of BPT Courthouse Associates LP, a team that won the General Services Administration design/build competition for the project.
Construction is scheduled to begin in April and to be completed in the fall of 1996, the company said.
The site will include a 300,000-square-foot courthouse and office building, a one-acre public plaza and a 202-space parking facility, the company said.
