AMERICAN STORES Co., Salt Lake City, declared a 2-for-1 stock split and boosted its quarterly dividend 20%, to 12 cents a share on a postsplit basis from 10 cents a share.
The split is payable April 21 to stock of record April 7.
The grocery and drugstore chain currently has 71.2 million common shares outstanding.
The new dividend is payable April 13 to stock of record April 1.
