This broadcasting concern named three vice presidents to its multimedia group: Robert J. Ackley, 33 years old; Katherine Dillon, 33; and Lauren Marrus, 32.
Mr. Ackley was most recently vice president, finance and operations, at the company's video publishing unit.
Ms. Dillon has been managing director, ABC News Ventures, and Ms. Marrus was previously vice president and Journals publisher at Institutional Investor Inc., a business publishing company that is a unit of Capital Cities.
