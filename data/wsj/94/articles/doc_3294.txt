Three executives were promoted at this bank holding company.
Jeffrey D. Kelly, 40 years old, formerly senior vice president of investments, was named executive vice president of investments; Robert J. Ondercik, 47, formerly senior vice president of credit, was named executive vice president of credit administration.
And J. Armando Ramirez, 38, formerly vice president of mergers and acquisitions, was named senior vice president of mergers and acquisitions.
Their duties will not change.
