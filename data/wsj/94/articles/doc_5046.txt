Zurich Reinsurance Centre Holdings Inc. swung from a year-earlier loss in the fourth quarter.
The New York reinsurance company earned $833,000, or three cents a share, compared with a year-earlier loss of $3.9 million, or 23 cents a share.
The company had a loss of $188,000 on operations compared with a $4.4 million loss a year earlier.
Earnings growth came from investing the money raised by last year's public stock offering and a debt offering.
The company estimates losses from the Los Angeles earthquake will not exceed $5 million.
