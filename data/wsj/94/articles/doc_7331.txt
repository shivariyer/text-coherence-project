Brian Duperreault, 46, president of this insurance concern's overseas property-casualty unit, American International Underwriters, was named chairman and chief executive officer of the unit.
Evan G. Greenberg, 38, chief executive officer of the parent company's AIG Cos. in Japan and Korea, will return to the U.S. as president and chief operating officer of AIU.
Mr. Greenberg remains chairman of AIG Cos. in Japan and Korea.
Ronald J. Anderson, AIU senior vice president, external affairs, will succeed Mr. Greenberg as president and chief operating officer of AIG Cos. in Japan and Korea.
