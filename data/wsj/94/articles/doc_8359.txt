SHERRITT Inc., Fort Saskatchewan, Alberta, said it expects to receive 194 million Canadian dollars (US$148.1 million) after expenses from the sale to a group of underwriters of 18.5 million new common shares.
The shares are being sold at C$11 each for a total sale price of C$203.5 million.
The underwriting group is led by Gordon Capital Corp. and ScotiaMcLeod Inc.
Sherritt currently has 43.3 million shares outstanding.
Sherritt, which has interests in natural resources and fertilizers, said it will use the proceeds for general corporate purposes and to help finance possible future acquisitions.
The company said this could include its proposed acquisition of Toronto-based Imperial Oil Ltd.'s fertilizer operations for an estimated C$365 million.
