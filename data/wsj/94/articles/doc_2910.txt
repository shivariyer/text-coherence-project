John S. McLellan was named a vice president of this military aerospace and electronics concern.
He was also appointed executive vice president of the company's Lockheed Aeronautical Systems Co. subsidiary, succeeding H. Bard Allison, 62 years old, who retired last year.
Mr. McLellan, 52, previously was president of Lockheed Aircraft ServiceOntario.
Arthur J. Schuetz, 47, succeeds Mr. McLellan as the executive in charge of the Ontario operations, with the title of executive vice president and general manager of Lockheed Aircraft Service Co.
Mr. Schuetz had been vice president and program manager of Lockheed's U-2 Aircraft Program with Lockheed Advanced Development Co., also known as the "Skunkworks."
