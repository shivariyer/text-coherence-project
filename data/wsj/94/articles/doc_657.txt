ILC Technology Inc. said it will take charges in its fiscal second quarter totaling $4.7 million, or 93 cents a share, related to poor performance in its Precision Lamp Inc. unit.
About $3.4 million of the total is related to writing down goodwill in Precision Lamp.
About $500,000 is for writing down inventory.
The rest is for early-retirement payments for various workers who are leaving the company.
The quarter ends Saturday.
In the year-earlier period, ILC had net income of $2.4 million, or 48 cents a share, on sales of $12.2 million.
ILC develops, makes and distributes light products for several markets.
