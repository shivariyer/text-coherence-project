Canadian primary steel production totaled 266,846 metric tons in the week ended Feb. 5, a 13% increase from 235,616 tons the previous week and a 2.4% increase from 260,617 tons a year earlier, said Statistics Canada, a government agency.
A metric ton is equal to about 2,204.62 pounds.
For the year to date, primary steel output totaled 1,269,984 tons, a 6.2% decline from 1,354,324 tons a year earlier.
