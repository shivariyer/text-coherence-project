Four senior vice presidents were named at this publisher and direct-mail marketer: Peter J. Davenport, 53 years old, senior vice president, global direct marketing; Kenneth A. Gordon, 56, senior vice president and president, U.S.; Joseph M. Grecky, 54, senior vice president, human resources; and Anthony W. Ruggiero, 52, senior vice president and chief financial officer.
All four were formerly vice presidents, and the posts to which they were named are new.
