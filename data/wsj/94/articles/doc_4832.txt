Nobel Insurance Ltd. said it will report a one-time pretax gain of $5.7 million, or 82 cents a share, for its fourth quarter and 1993 full-year results, from reductions in claims reserves for prior years.
In the year-earlier fourth quarter, the company reported net income of $14.3 million, or $2.14 a share.
For 1992, the company reported net income of $19.1 million, or $2.89 a share.
The company said 1993 results will be released in mid-March.
