Microcom Inc. named Roland D. Pampel president and chief executive officer.
Mr. Pampel, 51 years old, succeeds James M. Dow, founder of this hardware and software concern.
Mr. Dow, 43, remains chairman.
Mr. Pampel, whose long career in the computer industry includes executive positions at Prime Computer Inc. and Apollo Computer Inc., was most recently president and chief executive at Nicolet Instrument Corp.
Mr. Dow, who initiated the search for a successor, said "it was time to introduce new ideas and management practices at Microcom."
