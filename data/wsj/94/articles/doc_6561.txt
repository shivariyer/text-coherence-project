Dress Barn Inc. said sales for the four weeks ended Saturday fell 10% to $16.3 million from $18.2 million, while sales at stores open for at least a year dropped 18%.
For the 13 weeks ended Saturday, sales rose 8.2%, to $106.6 million from $98.5 million, while same-store sales fell 4%.
The retailer of women's off-price apparel stores in 41 states operated 657 stores as of Saturday, a 10% increase from a year ago.
The company said sales were hurt by the severe cold and snowstorms.
