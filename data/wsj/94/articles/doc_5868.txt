American Telephone & Telegraph Co. said it formed a joint venture to supply telecommunication products in Venezuela.
AT&T said it owns 51% of the joint venture, with the remaining interest owned by Electroconductores C.A., a Venezuelan producer of telecommunications and power cables.
A spokeswoman said AT&T is investing $8 million to $12 million in the $20 million venture, which is called AT&T Elecon Telesistemas C.A.
