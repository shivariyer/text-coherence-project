Capital Holding Corp. said it will redeem at face value all $100 million of its adjustable-rate cumulative preferred stock, Series F, March 2.
The company has one million Series F shares outstanding.
The redemption price will be $100 a share, plus accrued and unpaid dividends to the redemption date.
The Series F stock carries an adjustable quarterly dividend rate with a minimum annual rate of 6.75% and a maximum annual rate of 14%, the company said.
Capital Holding, a multi-line insurance holding company, said it paid quarterly cash dividends at an annual rate of 6.75%.
