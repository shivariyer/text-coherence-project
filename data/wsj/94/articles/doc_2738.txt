Craig E. Davies, 51 years old, has been named president and chief executive officer and a member of the board at this servicer of underground utilities.
Mr. Davies, who had been chief operating officer at Sithe Energies Inc. in New York, succeeds Granville W. Holman, 64, chairman, who had been acting president.
Mr. Davies's seat on the board fills the one vacated by John Potter, 48, former president and chief executive, who resigned from the board recently.
