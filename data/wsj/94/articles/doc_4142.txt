J. M. Haggar III, 42-year-old president and chief executive officer of this maker of men's apparel, was named to the added post of chairman.
Mr. Haggar succeeds J. M. Haggar Jr., 69, who will continue as a director.
Additionally, Rae F. Evans, 45, was named to the board, succeeding Kevin Chisholm, a Dallas accountant.
Mrs. Evans is vice president, national affairs, of Hallmark Cards Inc.
