Shorewood Packaging Corp. said it completed acquisition of the assets of Somerville Packaging's premium packaging group from Cascades Paperboard International Inc. for $100 million and a possible contingent $5 million payment.
Somerville, a manufacturer of folding cartons for the tobacco, cosmetics, toiletries and general-consumer industries, reported $139 million in sales for the year ended Aug. 31.
Shorewood said it had about $184 million in sales for the year ended April 30.
