Mesa Airlines said its December 1993 traffic rose 0.3%, to 76 million revenue passenger miles from the year-earlier period's 75.8 million.
A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.
December's load factor, or percentage of seats filled, was 49%, down from a year-earlier 51%.
The company said full-year 1993 traffic rose 41%, to 928.7 million revenue passenger miles from 656.8 million.
Load factor in both years was 50%.
