The company's Occidental Oil & Gas Corp. subsidiary named two new executive vice presidents.
John A. Carver was appointed executive vice president, world-wide exploration and James B. Taylor was appointed executive vice president, international operations.
Mr. Carver, 62 years old, previously was vice president, international exploration and Mr. Taylor, 55, continues in his previous position as executive vice president, corporate development with the oil and natural gas unit, based in Bakersfield, Calif.
The parent is a diversified company with interests in natural resources, chemicals and food processing.
