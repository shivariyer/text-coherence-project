Developers Diversified Realty Corp. said it acquired two shopping centers for $17.8 million.
The real-estate investment trust said one shopping center, located in Atlanta, was purchased from Pleasant Hill Plaza L.P. of Atlanta, and the other, located in Ahoskie, N.C., was sold by two concerns, Ahoskie Commons L.P. and Ahoskie Market Place L.P.
The assets purchased contain a total of 407,482 square feet.
Developers Diversified acquires, develops, owns, leases and manages shopping centers and business centers.
