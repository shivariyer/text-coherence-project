Ben Charlson, 64 years old, was named to the additional position of chairman at the Stone & Webster Engineering Corp. unit of this engineering and construction concern.
Mr. Charlson, formerly president, continues as chief executive officer of the unit.
The position of chairman had been vacant for about two years.
S. Fuad Koseoglu , 64, succeeds Mr. Charlson as president.
Mr. Koseoglu was most recently an executive vice president.
Richard B. Kelly, 58, formerly senior vice president, was named senior executive vice president, and two senior vice presidents were named executive vice presidents: Charles E. Crocker Jr., 45, and Robert C. Wiesel, 43.
