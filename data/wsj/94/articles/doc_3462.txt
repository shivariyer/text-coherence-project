Humana Inc. said it completed its previously announced acquisition of Group Health Association for about $55 million.
Washington-based Group Health, which provides comprehensive medical coverage to about 125,000 members in the metropolitan Washington area, has annual premium revenue of about $250 million, Humana said.
Humana provides managed-care health-plan services to over 1.6 million members in 11 states, mainly through health-maintenance and preferred-provider organizations.
