William J. Carone was named chairman of this maker of women's clothing, succeeding Matthew A. Berdon, who had been acting chairman since September.
Mr. Carone, 48 years old, is a senior vice president of Rosenthal & Rosenthal, a New York finance company.
He has been a director of He-Ro since Jan. 1.
Mr. Berdon, a partner of the New York accounting firm Ferro, Berdon & Co., assumed the duties of He-Ro chairman after the death of Herbert Rounick.
He-Ro also said Della Rounick was elected a director, filling a vacancy on the three-member board.
Ms. Rounick, the controlling shareholder of the company, is a designer of He-Ro's evening wear.
