First Bancorp. of Ohio said it bought Life Federal Savings Bank of Clearwater, Fla., from the Resolution Trust Corp., the federal agency charged with disposing of failed banking institutions, for $2.3 million.
The Ohio bank said it planned to inject $5 million of new capital into Life Federal.
Life Federal, with deposits of about $25 million, has three branches in Clearwater.
First Bancorp. owns Bancorp Trust Co. in Florida and six banking affiliates in Ohio.
