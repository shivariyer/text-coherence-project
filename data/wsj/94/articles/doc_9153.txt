Cross Timbers Oil Co. said it completed $28.4 million in acquisitions, increasing its working interest in an oil and gas-producing field in West Texas.
The oil and natural gas company said it now owns a 91.5% working interest in the Prentice Northeast Unit, which it will operate.
Cross Timbers said it acquired a 29.4% working interest from a unit of Amoco Corp. and a 13.2% working interest from a closely held company didn't identify.
