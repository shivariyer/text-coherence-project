RJR Nabisco Holdings Corp.'s Nabisco Foods Group said it acquired the Knox gelatin business in the U.S. and Puerto Rico from Thomas J. Lipton Co., an operating unit of Unilever Group.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The pact also calls for Nabisco Foods, Parsippany, N.J., to buy two gelatin-packaging lines from Lipton, which it will install at its Seville, Ohio, plant.
John Greeniaus, chairman and chief executive officer of Nabisco Foods, said the acquisition is part of a strategy to enhance profitability in its Royal Desserts business.
RJR Nabisco Holdings is a New York-based food and tobacco company.
