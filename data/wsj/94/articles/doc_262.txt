Schlumberger Ltd. said it intends to resume its common-stock repurchase program.
The huge oil-field services and measurement company said under an authorization by directors, the company may purchase as many as seven million shares on the open market depending on market conditions.
Schlumberger had an average 243 million shares outstanding at year end.
In late composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange, its shares were down $2 at $53.
