Japanese corporate insolvencies in February fell 7.5% from a year earlier, to 1,030 cases, private credit research agency Teikoku Databank Ltd. said.
Despite the drop, the number of cases remained above 1,000 for the 13th consecutive month, the agency said.
Debt left behind by insolvent companies jumped 14% from a year earlier, to 507.68 billion yen ($4.78 billion).
