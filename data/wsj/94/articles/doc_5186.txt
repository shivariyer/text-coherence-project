European Gas Turbines said it received a contract to provide 500-megawatt turbines to Malaysia's Powertek, which is building a power plant in Malaysia.
The value of the contract wasn't disclosed.
European Gas is a unit of Anglo-French industrial group GEC-Alsthom, which is a joint venture between France's Alcatel-Alsthom SA and the United Kingdom's General Electric Co.
Cegelec, a unit of Alcatel-Alsthom, will supply the transmission lines from the plant and high-voltage substations.
Zelleco of Malaysia will perform the civil-engineering work.
