Mark S. Brandin, 43 years old, was named executive vice president, marketing and advertising, of this company's Quick & Reilly Inc. discount brokerage unit.
Mr. Brandin, most recently executive vice president, capital markets/trading at discount broker Charles Schwab & Co., succeeds William A. Coppel.
Mr. Coppel, 38, was named vice president of corporate administration and special projects for Quick & Reilly Inc.
