FFY Financial Corp., holding company for First Federal Savings Bank of Youngstown, said it plans to repurchase as much as 5% of its shares outstanding, or 314,925 shares, in the open market over the next six months.
The company said it will buy the shares at prevailing market prices.
The repurchased shares will become treasury shares available for general corporate purposes, the company said, including the issuance of shares in connection with the exercise of stock options.
