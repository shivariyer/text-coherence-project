Owens-Corning Fiberglas Corp., Toledo, Ohio, sold its 25% interest in CAE Fiberglass Ltd., to ZCL Manufacturing Canada Inc., of Edmonton, Alberta.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Campbell Soup Co., Camden, N.J., will spend $57 million for improvements at its Sacramento, Calif., plant.
Partech Holdings Corp., Columbus, Ohio, agreed to buy WMLO-FM in Tallahassee, Fla., from Edward Winton.
Terms weren't disclosed.
OHM Corp., Findlay, Ohio, finalized a contract with the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency for emergency cleanups.
The agency picked up the first of four option years.
The value of the contract could be as much as $100 million over five years.
