George H. Conrades, 54, was named a director of this electrical equipment, broadcasting and power generation concern, increasing the board to 10 members.
Mr. Conrades is the newly appointed chief executive officer of Bolt Beranek & Newman Inc. in Cambridge, Mass.
In 1992, he ended a 31-year career at International Business Machines Corp., where his last post was senior vice president of corporate marketing and services.
