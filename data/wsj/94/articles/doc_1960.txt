The Treasury plans to raise $825 million in new cash with the sale Monday of about $25.2 billion in short-term bills to redeem $24.37 billion in maturing bills.
The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing on June 23, 1994, and Sept. 22, 1994, respectively.
The Cusip number for the three-month bills is 912794L36.
The Cusip number for the six-month bills is 912794L77.
Noncompetitive tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by noon EST Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders for the bills must be received by 1 p.m. EST.
