Asahi Denka Kogyo, Mitsubishi Corp. and Mitsubishi International Corp. said they formed a U.S.-based joint-venture company, Amfine Chemical Corp., to make plastic additives.
Amfine will make and market environmentally safe, high-performance plastic additives.
The companies said Amfine is 60% owned by Asahi Denka, 30% by Mitsubishi and 10% by Mitsubishi International.
