Consolidated Stainless Inc. said it expects to report net income of roughly $75,000 for the 1993 fourth quarter, compared with $22,000 a year earlier.
Earnings per share for the fourth quarter will total between four and five cents, compared with roughly 1.3 cents in the year-earlier period.
Revenue fell about 28% to approximately $5.5 million from $7.6 million, but earnings were boosted by lower costs and improved yields.
Consolidated, based in Longwood, Fla., makes stainless steel pipe and fittings.
The company will officially report its fourthquarter earnings in two to three weeks.
For 1993, earnings totaled about $600,000, or about 34 cents a share, on revenue of $25 million.
That compares with a 1992 loss of $27,000, or 1.5 cents a share, on revenue of $24 million.
For this quarter, the company expects net to fall 44% to $270,000, or 10 cents a share, on revenue of roughly $6 million.
