Infinity Broadcasting Corp. agreed to acquire Detroit radio station WXYT-AM from Fitz Broadcasting Inc. for about $23 million, subject to various terms and conditions, including Federal Communications Commission approval.

Terms weren't disclosed.
S-K-I Ltd.'s Killington, Vt., ski resort plans to install a new gondola at a cost of more than $10 million.
