Biosensor Corp. acquired substantially all the assets of Marcom Inc. of New Hope, Minn.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Biosensor said the assets relate to or are used in the manufacture of Marcom monitoring devices.
The company said the assets include technology and proprietary rights, inventories, machinery and equipment, furniture, trademarks, sales backlog, and marketing, manufacturing and service records.
Marcom manufactures equipment that sends heart signals to and displays them from a nursing station using radio waves.
Biosensor, based in Plymouth, Minn., develops, manufactures and sells diagnostic equipment.
