Stephen E. Wall, 51 years old, was named president and chief operating officer of Society National Bank, the largest banking unit of the bank holding company.
He succeeds Henry L. Meyer III, 44, as president of the unit; the operating-chief post had been vacant for several years.
Mr. Meyer continues as chairman and chief executive officer of the bank.
Mr. Wall was formerly vice chairman of the bank and an executive vice president of Society Corp., which recently merged with KeyCorp.
