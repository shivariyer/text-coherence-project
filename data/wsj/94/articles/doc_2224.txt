ACS Enterprises completed its acquisitions of the Cleveland and Bakersfield, Calif., wireless cable-television systems for a total of $28 million.
The deals were funded with proceeds from the company's public offering of common shares in January.
The Cleveland wireless cable system has about 13,000 subscribers, and the Bakersfield system has about 2,500 subscribers, bringing the company's total number of subscribers to 42,000.
