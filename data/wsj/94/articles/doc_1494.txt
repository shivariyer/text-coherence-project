Phoenix Resource Cos. said Goldman, Sachs & Co. offered 1,160,000 Phoenix Resource common shares at $28.50 apiece.
Goldman Sachs is selling all its 27% stake in the oil and gas company.
A spokeswoman for Phoenix Resource said Goldman Sachs had held debt in a predecessor company and acquired its stake in Phoenix when the company reorganized.
Underwriters for the offering are being managed by Salomon Brothers Inc.
