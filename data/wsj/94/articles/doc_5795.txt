Pennsylvania & Southern Gas Co., Philadelphia, said its shareholders approved the company's agreement and plan of merger with NUI Corp.
In a statement, Pennsylvania & Southern Gas said of the 84.3% of the shares voting, 83.8% approved the merger.
Consummation of the merger is subject to certain regulatory approvals.
As reported, utility holding company NUI agreed to acquire the gas concern in a stock transaction valued at about $17 million.
NUI is based in Bedminster, N.J.
Under the agreement, each Pennsylvania & Southern Gas holder will receive no less than 2.4 and no more than three NUI common shares for each Pennsylvania share held.
NUI shares were at $26.50, down 12.5 cents, in late New York Stock Exchange trading.
