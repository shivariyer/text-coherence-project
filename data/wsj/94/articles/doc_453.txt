Vincent D. Enright was named chief financial officer, a position previously held by Craig G. Matthews, 51 years old, who continues as executive vice president.
Mr. Enright, 50, is also a senior vice president at this natural gas distributor.
The company also announced three newly created senior vice president positions: William K. Feraudo, 44, marketing group; Lenore Puleo, 40, customer service group; and Wallace P. Parker, 44, human resources.
All positions are effective April 1.
