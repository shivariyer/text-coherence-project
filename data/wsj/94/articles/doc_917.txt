ICF Kaiser International Inc. said it signed a $1.7 million contract with the New Jersey Transit Authority for preparation of an environmental impact statement.
The company said the statement is for a 15.3 mile light-rail system in New Jersey that will extend along a corridor from Ridgefield to Jersey City.
ICF Kaiser said it will prepare a draft environmental impact statement for an extension of the line into southern Bayonne, N.J.
ICF Kaiser's Engineering & Construction Group will serve as prime contractor.
