The Treasury plans to raise $600 million in new cash with the sale Monday of about $25.2 billion in short-term bills to redeem $24.60 billion in maturing bills.
The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing on July 7, 1994, and Oct. 6, 1994, respectively.
The CUSIP number for the three-month bills is 912794M35.
The CUSIP number for the six-month bills is 912794N59.
Noncompetitive tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by noon EDT Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders for the bills must be received by 1 p.m. EDT.
