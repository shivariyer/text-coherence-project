New York Life Insurance Co. said net income rose 36% in 1993, with its life-insurance and mutual-funds products showing especially strong growth.
The company said net income was $368 million, up from $271 million a year earlier.
Total income, including premiums and investment income, was up 6% to $15.56 billion.
The insurance concern said its surplus at the end of 1993 was $3.45 billion, up from $2.84 billion in 1992.
