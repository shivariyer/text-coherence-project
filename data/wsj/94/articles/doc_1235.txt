Bowater PLC, a British paper and packaging concern, said its pretax profit in 1993 rose 36%, to #201.6 million ($300.1 million), boosted mainly by acquisitions.
Sales were #2.13 billion, also up 36%.
Foreign-exchange translations that favored Bowater boosted operating profit by about #7 million in 1993.
Bowater said it expects no contribution from foreign exchange this year.
Among Bowater's acquisitions in 1993 are the #37 million purchase of MiTek Inc. and the #298 million acquisition of Speciality Coatings International Inc.
The company booked exceptional gains from the sale of discontinued operations, mainly its Checks in the Mail business for #57 million.
