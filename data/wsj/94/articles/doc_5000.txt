Xerox Corp. said it will consolidate six regional customer administration centers into four locations in an effort to improve productivity.
The company will expand facilities in Tampa, Fla., and Park Ridge, Ill., while maintaining operations in Lewisville, Texas, and Santa Ana, Calif.
Centers in Stamford, Conn., and Arlington, Va., are being consolidated.
Most of the 700 employees affected will be offered transfers to the new facilities.
