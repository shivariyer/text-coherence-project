Pioneer Standard Electronics Inc. said it executed a letter of intent to acquire certain assets and liabilities of United Westburne Inc.'s Zentronics unit for about $18 million to $20 million.
Zentronics, the Canadian electronics distribution division of United Westburne, Montreal, has more than 70 million Canadian dollars (US$51.8 million) in annual sales.
Pioneer-Standard, an electronics distributor, expects the transaction to close in June.
