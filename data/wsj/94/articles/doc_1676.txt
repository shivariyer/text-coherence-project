Loren K. Carroll, formerly executive vice president and chief financial officer of this energy products and services concern, was named president and chief executive officer of M-I Drilling Fluids Co., in which Smith International acquired a 64% stake earlier this year.
He succeeds Bill Lakey, 59 years old, who resigned.
Mr. Carroll, 50, remains a Smith International director.
John Kennedy, Smith International's treasurer, was named to the additional and newly created posts of vice president and chief accounting officer.
Mr. Kennedy, 41, assumes some of the duties vacated by Mr. Carroll who isn't currently being succeeded as chief financial officer.
