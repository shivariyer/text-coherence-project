WHO'S NEWS: James Gianopulos was named president, international theatrical, and Tom Sherak was named senior executive vice president at News Corp.'s Twentieth Century Fox movie studio. . . .
Tom Pollock, chairman of MCA Inc.'s motion picture group, signed a new "long term" contract, the company said.
MCA, a unit of Japan's Matsushita Electric Industrial Co., wouldn't disclose the terms of the contract, but industry executives said Mr. Pollock has agreed to head the motion picture group for at least four more years.
He will also join Cineplex Odeon Corp.'s board, succeeding Sid Sheinberg, MCA president.
