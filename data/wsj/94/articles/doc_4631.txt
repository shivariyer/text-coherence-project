Granada Group PLC made a final bid for LWT Holdings PLC of 784 pence ($11.59) a share, which would value the broadcaster at #811 million ($1.2 billion).
LWT said it received a letter from Granada last week saying the deadline for acceptances is Friday.
Granada's earlier bid valued LWT at about #776 million.
In addition to television, LWT has interests in travel and publishing.
Granada has interests in television, highway services, bowling centers, nightclubs, theme parks and a rental business.
