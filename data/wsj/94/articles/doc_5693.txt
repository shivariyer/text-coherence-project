Money-market mutual fund assets continued to decline this week, falling $4.39 billion to $589.31 billion from a revised $593.70 billion for the week ended Wednesday, the Investment Company Institute said.
Assets of 652 retail money-market mutual funds rose by $2.04 billion to $402.07 billion, the trade group said.
Among retail funds, assets of 405 taxable money-market funds increased by $1.34 billion to $311.34 billion.
Assets of 247 retail tax-exempt funds increased by $700.7 million to $90.74 billion.
Assets of the 282 institutional money-market funds fell by $6.43 billion to $187.23 billion.
Among institutional funds, the assets of 228 taxable money-market funds dropped by $6.39 billion to $164.45 billion.
Assets of 54 tax-exempt funds fell by $45.3 million to $22.78 billion.
