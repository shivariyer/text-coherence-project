Empresas La Moderna of Mexico announced Friday that its Cigarrera La Moderna subsidiary will develop a new cigarette brand in China with China National Tobacco Corp.
The two companies will also develop a Chinese herb cigarette brand in Mexico for the North American market.
Financial terms of the partnership weren't disclosed.
Cigarrera La Moderna, based in Monterrey, Mexico, holds a 56% share of Mexico's retail cigarette market.
