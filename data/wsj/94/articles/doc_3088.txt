James L. Fishel, 62-year-old vice president and manager of corporate credit operations of General Electric Capital Corp., and Louis T. Rosso, 60, chairman and chief executive officer of Beckman Instruments Inc., were elected to the board of this real estate investment trust, succeeding Walter L. Weishman and Robert L.B. Diener.
The company said that Mr. Weishman, 57, and Mr. Diener, 44, chose not to stand for re-election.
