JB's Restaurants Inc. said it plans to sell a majority or all of its common shares in HomeTown Buffet Inc. as part of the public offering previously announced by HomeTown Buffet.
Don M. McComas, president and chief executive officer of JB's Restaurants, said the company intends to use the proceeds from the sale of its HomeTown Buffet shares to develop HomeTown Buffet Restaurants, repay debt and invest in JB's Restaurants.
