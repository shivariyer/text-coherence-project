Sonoco Products Co. said it offered to purchase M. Harland & Son Ltd., a British label producer, for between $30 million and $40 million.
Sonoco, a Hartsville, S.C., maker of packaging products, said the offer is subject to approval of each company's board, a satisfactory due-diligence study by Sonoco and negotiation and execution of a definitive agreement.
Harland, based in Hull, England, has annual revenue of about $32 million.
