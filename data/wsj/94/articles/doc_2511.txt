Sovereign Bancorp Inc. said it entered into a definitive agreement to acquire Rochester Community Savings Bank's Shadow Lawn Savings Bank for $74.6 million in cash.
Shadow Lawn, based in West Long Branch, N.J., has 17 offices in Monmouth and Ocean counties in New Jersey.
It will be combined with the Sovereign Bank of New Jersey division.
Sovereign Bancorp, with about $4.5 billion in assets, is based in Wyomissing, Pa., and has 81 community banking and loan offices in eastern Pennsylvania and central New Jersey.
Rochester Community is a $4.2 billion savings bank based in Rochester, N.Y.
The acquisition is expected to close this summer subject to regulatory approvals.
