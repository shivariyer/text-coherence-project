Oceaneering International Inc. said it acquired the operating units of an Edisto Resources Corp. subsidiary for $12 million.
The Houston underwater services company said it bought Multiflex Inc., Multiflex U.K. Inc. and Specialty Wire & Cable Co. from Edisto's Multiflex International Inc.
The companies, which have plants in Texas and Scotland, make electrical wire, specialty cable products and high-pressure hoses used in the offshore oil and gas industry.
The companies purchased had 1993 revenue of about $29 million.
