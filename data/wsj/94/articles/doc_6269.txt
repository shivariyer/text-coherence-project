The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended February 4, 1994.
The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive.
Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer.
Ashland City, Ky., will redeem, on March 1, 1994, $3,800,000 principal amount of its 5.80% environmental improvement revenue bonds, 1978 series, due March 1, 2003.
National City Bank, Kentucky; Cleveland, is trustee.
Illinois Environmental Facilities Financing Authority will redeem, on March 11, 1994, the following pollution control revenue bonds: its 8 3/8% bonds, series 1979, due November 1, 2004; and its 8 1/2% bonds, series 1979, due November 1, 2009.
The authority will also redeem, on April 1, 1994, its 9 3/4% pollution control revenue bonds, series 1983, due April 1, 2013.
First National Bank of Chicago is trustee.
