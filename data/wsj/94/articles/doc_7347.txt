Alice T. Kane, 46 years old, was named an executive vice president, asset management and investment products operations.
She succeeds Thomas P. O'Connor, 49, who said he is retiring in June.
Ms. Kane, a member of the executive management committee, remains general counsel for the company.
Separately, senior vice presidents George J. Trapp and Frederick Sievert, both 46, were named to the executive management committee.
Also, Jefferson C. Boyce, 36, was named a senior vice president responsible for the mutual funds and structured finance departments and the NYLIFE Securities division.
Mr. Boyce has been a vice president in the company's pension department.
Michael Gallo, 39, was promoted to senior vice president responsible for the Northeastern agencies.
He had been vice president and chief administrative officer for individual operations.
