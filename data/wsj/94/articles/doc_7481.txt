Shareholders of the Dutch chemical group Akzo NV approved the previously announced merger with Nobel Industries AB of Sweden.
The stockholders also approved the issue of up to 17 million Akzo shares to finance the takeover of Nobel in an offer of stock and cash.
An Akzo spokesman said now that the merger and the share issue have been approved by stockholders, his company can go ahead with the public tender offer for Nobel in the coming days.
The offer likely will close in mid-February.
