Boosted by sales of its computer-networking products, Cisco Systems Inc. said its net income nearly doubled for its fiscal second quarter.
For the quarter ended Jan. 30, Cisco, Menlo Park, Calif., posted net income of $77.5 million, or 29 cents a share, compared with year-earlier net of $38.7 million, or 15 cents a share.
The per-share figures reflect a two-for-one stock split effective March 4.
Sales in the quarter more than doubled to $302 million compared with $145 million a year earlier.
For the six months, Cisco earned $141 million, or 53 cents a share, compared with year-earlier net of $72 million, or 28 cents a share.
Sales increased to $550.6 million compared with $271.5 million a year ago.
