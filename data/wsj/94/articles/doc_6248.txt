Alliance Entertainment Corp. completed its purchase of closely held Airlie Inc., known as Abbey Road Distributors, for $35.5 million in cash, common stock and notes.
Abbey Road, based in Santa Ana, Calif., is a West Coast wholesale distributor of recorded music that earned $4.8 million on revenue of $80.7 million for the fiscal year ended Aug. 31, 1993.
Alliance, a wholesale distributor of prerecorded music, generated an estimated $285 million in revenue in 1993.
The company went public last December and is traded on the Nasdaq Stock Market.
