Laurence W. Cheng, Robert R. Douglass and Zaid Pedersen were elected directors of this insurance holding company, increasing the size of the board to 13.
Mr. Cheng, 46 years old, is president and chief executive officer of Zurich International (Bermuda) Ltd. and chief operating officer of Centre Reinsurance (Bermuda) Ltd.
Mr. Douglass, 62, is an attorney at the law firm of Milbank, Tweed, Hadley & McCloy and chairman-elect of the board at Cedel in Luxembourg.
Mr. Pedersen is executive vice president and controller of Trygg-Hansa SPP in Stockholm.
