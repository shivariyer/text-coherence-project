Sir Colin M. Marshall, chairman of British Airways PLC, and Clifton Wharton Jr., a director of Ford Motor Co. and former deputy secretary of state, were nominated to become directors of this stock exchange to represent the public.
Deryck C. Maughan, chairman and chief executive officer of Salomon Brothers Inc., was nominated to represent the securities industries.
Directors are elected for two-year terms on the board, composed of 12 public members, 12 industry members and two officers from the exchange.
