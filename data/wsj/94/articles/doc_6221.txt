H. Earl Joudrie, a director, was named nonexecutive chairman, succeeding Hugh L. Macaulay, who will step down from the position in May.
Mr. Macaulay, 69 years old, will remain on the board.
Mr. Joudrie, 59, also is chairman of several other Canadian companies.
Canadian Tire is a retailing concern in Toronto that sells hardware, sporting goods and automotive-repair services.
