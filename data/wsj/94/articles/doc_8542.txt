Freeport-McMoRan Copper & Gold Inc. said it redeemed $118.6 million in liquid yield option notes outstanding due 2001.
The mining concern said $118.3 million of the notes were converted into 6,695,444 shares of its Class A common stock, and the balance of the notes were redeemed for cash.
The company is an affiliate of Freeport-McMoRan Inc.
