ITT Corp.'s ITT Automotive subsidiary opened a new fluid handling systems facility in Guaymas, Mexico.
ITT Automotive said the facility will produce vacuum, air suspension and seat bolster inflation harness components for customers in Mexico and the U.S.
Robert Zimcosky has been named plant manager.
Mr. Zimcosky was formerly with ITT Automotive Fluid Handling Systems in Oscoda, Mich., the company said.
