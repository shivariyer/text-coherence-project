I.C.H. Corp. said it has restated results for the second and third quarters of 1993 to correct accounting errors in some of its health reinsurance activities and in its quarterly income tax provisions.
The Louisville, Ky., insurance holding company said the 1993 second-quarter loss was revised to $8.6 million, or 34 cents a share, compared with the previously stated loss of $1.9 million, or 20 cents a share.
Third-quarter net income for 1993 was revised to $125.7 million, or $2.46 a share, compared with the previously stated earnings of $133 million, or $2.61 a share.
