This home-financing bank named five directors, bringing the board to 17.
Serving two-year terms are Angel Alvarez-Perez, 47 years old, president and chief executive officer of First Federal Savings Bank in Puerto Rico; Herbert G. Chorbajian, 55, chairman, president and CEO of both Albank Financial Corp. and its Albany Savings Bank unit, Albany, N.Y.; James M. Large Jr., 61, chairman and CEO of Anchor Savings Bank, Hewlett, N.Y.; and George Strayton, 50, president and CEO of Provident Savings Bank, Haverstraw, N.Y. Reese W. Fayde, 44, founder of Reese, Fayde & Associates, a New York management consulting firm, was named to a four-year term.
