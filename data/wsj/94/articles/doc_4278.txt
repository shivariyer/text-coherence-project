The directors of First Virginia Banks Inc. approved the purchase of as many as one million shares of the company's common stock to be used for employee stock plans, acquisitions and other corporate purposes.
The board also approved the issuance or buyback of as many as an additional one million shares over the next five to 10 years for its dividend reinvestment and stock purchase plan.
As of the end of 1993, the bank holding company had an average of 32.5 million shares outstanding.
