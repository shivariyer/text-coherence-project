Amoco Production Co., a subsidiary of Amoco Corp. said it signed a letter of intent with a unit of Trident NGL Holdings Inc. to build a natural-gas liquids processing plant in the Hugoton field of southwest Kansas.
Amoco, an oil and natural-gas company, declined to give the value of the proposed plant, which it will operate.
Trident NGL is a natural-gas processing company based in The Woodlands, Texas.
