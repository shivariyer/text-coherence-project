Boosted by one-time gains, Morrison Knudsen Corp. posted higher fourth-quarter earnings.
The Boise, Idaho, engineering and construction concern reported profit of $10.4 million, or 33 cents a share, up 51% from year-ago profit of $6.9 million, or 23 cents a share.
The quarter's results include a gain of $7.5 million from two stock sales.
Revenue for the quarter was $773.1 million, up from $666.7 million a year earlier.
For the year, Morrison Knudsen had profit of $35.8 million, or $1.15 a share, compared with a loss of $7.1 million, or 23 cents a share, after one-time charges.
Revenue for the year was $2.72 billion, up from $2.28 billion.
