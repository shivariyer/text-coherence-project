Chester Holdings Ltd. said it will purchase Joey's Children's Wear, a children's retail clothing chain with seven stores in the New York metropolitan area that is currently operating under Chapter 11 bankruptcy-law protection.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Chester said that the disclosure statement has been approved by the bankruptcy court, and that the confirmation hearing is scheduled for the first week in March.
The company said it will consider expanding Joey's in New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania and Connecticut.
