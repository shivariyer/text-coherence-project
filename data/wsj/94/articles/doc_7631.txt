Gillette Co. reported fourth-quarter net income of $4.9 million, or two cents a share, after a charge for a previously announced realignment, on sales of $1.62 billion.
Excluding the charge, earnings were $169 million.
The one-time charge of $164.1 million reduced profit by 74 cents a share.
For the year-ago quarter, Gillette reported net income of $135 million, or 61 cents a share, on sales of $1.51 billion.
The Boston personal-care products company has announced plans to reorganize its facilities world-wide, shedding 2,000 employees (mostly overseas) as it adds about that many jobs elsewhere.
For 1993, the company had net income of $288.3 million, or $1.29 a share, compared with net of $513 million, or $2.32 a share, in 1992.
Sales were $5.41 billion, up 5% from $5.16 billion in 1992.
