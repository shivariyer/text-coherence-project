Richard J. Hadala, 36 years old, was named vice president of strategic management of this power generation, defense electronics and broadcasting concern.
Mr. Hadala will be responsible for strategic planning and business development and will work closely with the company's business units to strengthen their strategic plans, Westinghouse said.
He will report directly to Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Michael H. Jordan, who took over the top posts last June.
Mr. Hadala formerly was a partner with McKinsey & Co., the consulting firm where Mr. Jordan worked from 1964 to 1974.
