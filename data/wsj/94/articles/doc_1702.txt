United Meridian Corp. said it discovered oil off the coast of West Africa.
The Houston company said the Lion No. 1 well offshore the Ivory Coast tested at a rate of 14,263 barrels of oil a day from three intervals at depths ranging between 10,176 feet and 10,407 feet.
The oil and natural gas company said it expects the well will start production late this year or early next year.
United Meridian operates the well and owns a 25% working interest.
The Ivory Coast's national oil company owns a 40% working interest; International Finance Corp., a subsidiary of World Bank, owns a 15% interest; Global Natural Resources Corp., Houston, owns a 10% interest; and Pluspetrol SA, Buenos Aires, owns 10%.
