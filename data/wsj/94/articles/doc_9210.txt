Diebold Inc said it bought 50% of a Venezuelan concern, OLTP ATM Systems C.A. of Caracas, to distribute and service Diebold's automated-teller machines and electronic-security systems in Venezuela.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Diebold has more than 40% of the 2,700 ATMs in Venezuela through InterBold, its ATM joint venture with International Business Machines Corp.
