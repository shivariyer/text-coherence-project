Robert J. Bronchetti, 46 years old, formerly executive vice president and chief financial officer of this automobile rental and financing concern, was named president of the company.
He retains the post of chief financial officer.
As president, he succeeds Terry W. Holt, 39, who resigned his post as president and chief executive officer of the company last Friday.
The company declined to commment on the reason for Mr. Holt's resignation, and Mr. Holt couldn't be reached for comment.
The company has not named anyone to succeed Mr. Holt as chief executive.
Also, Edward Burkhart, 35, was named to the new post of executive vice president in charge of rental operations.
He was formerly senior vice president.
