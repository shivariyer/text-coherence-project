KPMG Peat Marwick said it is acquiring a small Los Angeles consulting firm as part of its expansion into regulatory compliance services.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Smith Banking Consultants specializes in helping financial institutions comply with regulatory requirements such as the Community Reinvestment Act by performing detailed analyses of lending activities and through fair-lending reviews.
KPMG Peat Marwick provides audit, tax and consulting services in more than 131 countries.
