URS Consultants Inc., a unit of URS Corp., said it received a contract to provide environmental services for the U.S. Air Force Center for Environmental Excellence.
The company said the service is for one year, with four additional one-year options.
The potential total value of the contract is about $50 million, it said.
URS said it will provide preliminary assessment and site inspections, remedial investigations and feasibility studies, remedial designs and Title II inspection services at Air Force installations.
