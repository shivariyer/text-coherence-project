Kranzco Realty Trust said it completed the acquisition of two shopping centers from two private partnerships for $19.9 million.
The company said the properties purchased -- Suburban Plaza in Hamilton Township, near Trenton, N.J., and 69th Street Center in Upper Darby Township, Pa. -- have about 280,000 square feet of gross leasable space and are 100% leased.
Kranzco purchased the 69th Street Center for $3.6 million and the Suburban Plaza Center for $16.3 million.
