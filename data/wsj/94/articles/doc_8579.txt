John A. Hill, chairman of First Reserve Corp., and David H. Kennedy, managing director at First Reserve, were elected to the board of this oil and gas company.
The election of Mr. Hill, 51 years old, and Mr. Kennedy, 44, increases the board to 10 members.
PetroCorp said that its USF&G Corp. and Cigna Corp. units completed their previously announced sale of 1,650,000 PetroCorp common shares to First Reserve at $10.50 a share, giving First Reserve a 24.7% equity interest in PetroCorp.
