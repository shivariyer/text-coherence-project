Frederick W. Buckman, president and chief executive officer of CMS Energy Corp.'s principal subsidiary, Consumers Power Co., is resigning to accept the same positions at PacifiCorp, a Portland, Ore., utility.
Michael G. Morris, 47, formerly chief operating officer of the Consumers Power unit, was named to succeed Mr. Buckman as president and chief executive officer.
Mr. Morris joined Consumers Power in 1988 and led negotiations settling a dispute with the Michigan Public Service Commission over conflicts arising from the amount of energy it purchased from its 49%-owned Midland energy plant.
Mr. Buckman, 47, began his career with Consumers Power as an intern in 1967.
He has been president of the subsidiary since 1988 and chief executive officer since 1992.
