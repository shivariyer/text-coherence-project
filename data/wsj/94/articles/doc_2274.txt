Canadian rail freight volume totaled 4.3 million metric tons in the week ended Feb. 28, down 2.2% from a year earlier, Statistics Canada said.
For the year to date, rail freight volume was down 1.6% from a year earlier.
The number of rail cars loaded during the week was down 2.7% from a year earlier.
A metric ton is equal to 2,204.62 pounds.
