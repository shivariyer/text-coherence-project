The company's Marriott Lodging division named Todd Clist and Ed Fuller to the newly created posts of executive vice presidents.
Mr. Clist, 52 years old, will have responsibility for hotels in the U.S. and Canada.
He previously served as executive vice president for the Fairfield Inn and Courtyard segments of the division.
Mr. Fuller, 48, who will oversee the company's properties in countries outside the U.S. and Canada, previously was senior vice president and managing director of Marriott Lodging International.
The parent is a hotel and management services concern.
