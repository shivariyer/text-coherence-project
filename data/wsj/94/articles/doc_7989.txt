James R. Christie was named president of this machinery maker's Valenite Inc. unit.
Mr. Christie, 48 years old, succeeds Donald Otero, who retired last year.
Cincinnati Milacron acquired Valenite, which makes carbide cutting-tool inserts and steel insert holders for metalworking, from GTE Corp. last February.
Mr. Christie has been chairman and chief executive officer of London-based Hickson International PLC's Applied Chemicals & World Timber Co. unit.
