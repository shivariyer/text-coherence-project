First Fidelity Bancorp. said it completed the previously announced acquisition of BankVest Inc. and subsidiary First Peoples National Bank of Edwardsville, Pa., for $19.3 million.
First Peoples National Bank has $100 million in assets, $83 million in deposits and two offices in northeastern Pennsylvania.
First Fidelity has $33.8 billion in assets and operates in New Jersey, Pennsylvania, New York, and Connecticut and is acquiring Baltimore Bancorp in a strategy to expand from Maryland to Massachusetts.
