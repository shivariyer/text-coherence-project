U.S. HEALTHCARE Inc., Blue Bell, Pa., approved a 3-for-2 stock split on common and class B shares.
The provider of managed health-care services said the split will be payable March 29 to stock of record March 15.
The company, which recently raised its quarterly dividends to 20 cents on common and 18 cents on class B shares, said it intends to pay quarterly dividends of $0.1334 on common and $0.12 on class B shares after the split.
