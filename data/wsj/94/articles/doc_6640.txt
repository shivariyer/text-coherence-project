This fashion company said it named Ronald L. Frasch, senior vice president in charge of women's apparel for the Neiman Marcus unit of Neiman Marcus Group Inc., to be the president of its Escada USA division.
At Neiman Mr. Frasch, 45 years old, worked closely with Escada, which has been one of Neiman's top-selling designer collections.
Based in New York, Escada USA is a wholesaler and retailer of women's designer apparel with 1993 sales of about $120 million.
Mr. Frasch replaces Peter Laniak, 63, who served as Escada USA's president since 1986 and had previously announced his plans to retire.
Escada said that Mr. Laniak has been elected vice chairman of its U.S. board.
