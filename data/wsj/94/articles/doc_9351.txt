Jeff Cross, 40 years old, was named to the newly created post of executive vice president of operations for this company's division for persons with disabilities, effective Jan. 1.
He was previously vice president of operations.
Res-Care provides residential, training and support services for mentally retarded persons and individuals with other developmental disabilities.
The company also operates vocational training centers and owns 68% of Home Care Affiliates Inc., a concern that provides home nursing in four states.
