Apache Corp. said it discovered oil in a new field near Ely, Nev.
The oil and gas company, which owns a 38.4% working interest in the Grant Canyon 22-21 well, said the well tested at 4,500 barrels of oil a day from depths of 4,035 feet to 4,065 feet and is producing at a rate of 2,500 barrels a day.
Apache, operator of this well, shares ownership with numerous minority owners.
