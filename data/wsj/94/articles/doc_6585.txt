This manufacturer of personal-care products elected four directors, increasing board membership to nine: Judith Rodin, 49 years old, provost of Yale University and president (designate) of the University of Pennsylvania; Walter A. Forbes, 51, chairman and chief executive officer of CUC International; John J. Lee, 57, chairman and chief executive of Hexcel Corp; and Craig E. Weatherup, 48, president and chief executive of Pepsi-Cola North America.
