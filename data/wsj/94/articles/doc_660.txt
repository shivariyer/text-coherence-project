KeyCorp, Cleveland, said it completed its acquisition of Commercial Bancorp. of Colorado.
Under the terms of the transaction, CBC shareholders will receive 0.899 share of KeyCorp common stock for each share of CBC common stock.
Any fractional shares due CBC shareholders will be paid in cash at the rate of $31.35 a share.
This acquisition will result in the issuance of about 2.7 million common shares of KeyCorp.
CBC was the holding company for 11 Colorado Century Banks, with $397.3 million in assets and $353.5 million in deposits as of Dec. 31, 1993.
KeyCorp has assets of nearly $60 billion.
