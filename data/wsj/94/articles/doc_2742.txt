Continental Airlines said February traffic fell 7% from a year earlier.
The carrier said traffic dropped to 2,766,848,000 revenue passenger miles from 2,975,623,000.
A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.
Load factor, or percentage of available seats filled, rose to 59.2% from 56.9%.
For the year to date, traffic fell 7.8% to 5,761,775,000 revenue passenger miles from 6,248,573,000 a year earlier.
Load factor rose to 58.2% from 56.5%.
