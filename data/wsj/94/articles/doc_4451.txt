The Treasury plans to pay down $352 million on the public debt with the sale Monday of about $25.2 billion in short-term bills.
Maturing bills outstanding total $25.52 billion.
The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week and 26-week bills maturing on June 2, 1994, and Sept. 1, 1994, respectively.
The Cusip number for the three-month bills is 912794K86.
The Cusip number for the six-month bills is 912794M92.
Noncompetitive tenders for the bills, available in minimum $10,000 denominations, must be received by noon EST Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders for the bills must be received by 1 p.m. EST.
