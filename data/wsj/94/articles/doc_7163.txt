Whitmire Distribution Corp. said it signed an agreement with Kmart Corp. under which Whitmire will expand its role as a supplier of prescription and over-the-counter pharmaceutical products to most of Kmart's 1,650 pharmacies in the U.S. Whitmire recently agreed to be acquired by Cardinal Distribution Inc. for about $303 million in stock.
Whitmire is owned by members of management, Apollo Investment Fund LP and Chemical Venture Partners, an affiliate of Chemical Banking Corp.
