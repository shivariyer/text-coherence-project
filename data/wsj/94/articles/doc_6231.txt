J. David Edwards, 47 years old, was named president of this diamond-exploration company.
Mr. Edwards succeeds William M. Tonking, 64, who will retain his other post as chief executive officer.
Mr. Edwards said Mr. Tonking decided to "slow down" as he approached retirement.
Mr. Edwards previously was an executive vice president for United Services Advisors Inc., San Antonio, where he supervised several large gold and natural-resources mutual funds.
