Delta Air Lines said February traffic increased 4.5% to 5.81 billion revenue passenger miles from 5.56 billion a year ago.
A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.
Delta's load factor, or percentage of seats filled, was 60%, compared with 56.3% a year ago.
The airline said that for the first two months of the year, it flew 12.07 billion revenue passenger miles, up 4.8% from 11.51 billion.
Load factor rose to 59.01% from 54.98% a year ago.
