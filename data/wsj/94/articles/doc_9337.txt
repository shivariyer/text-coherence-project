Bankruptcy filings in 1993 by businesses and individuals in Switzerland exceeded 10,000 for the first time, according to Schweizerisher Verband Creditreform, a private credit-monitoring agency.
The number of businesses filing for bankruptcy surged 16% to 6,014 from 5,195 in 1992, while the number of individuals filing climbed 2.6% to 4,499 from 4,383.
Total bankruptcy filings rose 9.8% to 10,513 from 9,578.
