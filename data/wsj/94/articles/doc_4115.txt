Co-Steel Inc. said it sold about 1.15 million shares of Texas Industries Inc., or about a 10% stake, in the open market for $40.2 million.
Co-Steel, a steel producer, still owns about 1.2 million shares of Texas Industries, or 11% of the company's shares.
Texas Industries is a Dallas-based maker of concrete and steel products.
Co-Steel said it will use the proceeds for general corporate purposes and will record a gain of more than 10 million Canadian dollars (US$7.4 million) from the sale.
A Co-Steel spokesman said the company has no current plans to sell more shares of Texas Industries.
