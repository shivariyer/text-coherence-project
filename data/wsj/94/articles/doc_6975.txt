Ron Kaplan, vice president of sales and marketing, was named president of this gift and snack maker.
Mr. Kaplan, 28 years old, succeeds his father, Warren Kaplan, 56.
His wife, Judith Kaplan, remains chief executive officer.
A company spokesperson said the change follows the expiration of the senior Mr. Kaplan's employment contract and gives the company a "more aggressive leader."
The Kaplans declined further comment.
