FREEPORT-MCMORAN COPPER & GOLD Inc., New Orleans, said it's making a public offering of 3,750,000 of its new depositary shares, Series II.
The shares are priced at $38.875 apiece and are expected to bring proceeds of $138.5 million.
Proceeds will be used by the mining company to finance capital expenditures by its P.T. Freeport Indonesia Co. subsidiary.
The offering is being managed by Lehman Brothers Inc.
