Mexican telecommunications giant Telefonos de Mexico SA said its board has decided to raise its 1994 dividend to 25 centavos (7.7 U.S. cents) a share from 15 centavos in 1993.
The recommendation requires the approval of holders of Class AA and Class A shares.
The payout would be made in two equal parts in June and November to those shareholders and holders of Class L shares.
Shareholders will meet April 15.
In composite trading on the New York Stock Exchange yesterday, American depositary receipts of Telefonos de Mexico rose 25 cents to $65.375.
