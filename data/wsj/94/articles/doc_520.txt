Parker Hannifin Corp. agreed to buy Finn-Filter Oy from Kemira Oy, of Helsinki, Finland.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Finn-Filter makes filters and has more than $10 million in annual sales.
Lukens Inc. completed the sale of its safety products group to Jackson Products Inc., of St. Louis.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Toll Brothers Inc., a luxury homebuilder, entered the North Carolina market, agreeing to purchase lots in the Raleigh-Durham market.
Columbia/HCA Healthcare Corp. completed the previously announced sale of the 323-bed Medical Center Hospital of Huntsville, in Huntsville, Ala., to the Healthcare Authority of the city of Huntsville.
Terms weren't disclosed.
