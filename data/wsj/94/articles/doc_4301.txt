Melvin L. Cooper was named president of Union Corp., a new position at this provider of accounts-receivable management and debt-collection services.
Mr. Cooper, 66 years old, remains chairman and chief executive officer.
Also, William B. Hewitt, 55, was named executive vice president, a new post.
He continues as chairman and chief executive officer of Union's Capital Credit Corp. unit.
Separately, the Capital Credit subsidiary named James R. Paxton, 46, president and chief operating officer, effective March 1.
Both positions also are new.
Mr. Paxton most recently was executive vice president at Capital Credit.
