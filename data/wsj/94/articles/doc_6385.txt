James G. Cullen, 52 years old, was named a director of this insurer by Supreme Court of New Jersey Chief Justice Robert N. Wilentz.
Under New Jersey law, the chief justice is authorized to appoint six public directors to the 24-member board for a six-year term.
Mr. Cullen, president of Bell Atlantic Corp., succeeds Thomas S. Johnson, who had to resign when he became chief executive officer of Green Point Savings Bank.
