George B. Amoss, 52 years old, was named vice president and controller of this digital-telecommunications switching systems and services supplier.
He succeeds Frank A. Dunn, 40, who was named vice president, finance, at the Nortel North America unit.
The position is new.
Mr. Amoss has been vice president, business analysis and planning.
Also, Stephen A. Holinski, 47, was named vice president and treasurer of the parent, succeeding James E. Wilkinson, 46, who resigned to pursue other interests, the company said.
Mr. Holinski is vice president, finance, products.
All the positions are effective Feb. 16.
