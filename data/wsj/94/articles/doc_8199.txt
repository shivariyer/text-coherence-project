Robert G. Cox, 52 years old, was named to succeed Thomas D. Sayles Jr. as chief executive of Summit and chairman of its Summit Bank.
Mr. Cox was most recently president and chief executive of the unit and president and chief operating officer of the parent.
Mr. Cox's previous duties will now be carried out by a management executive committee reporting to him.
Mr. Sayles, 62, will continue as chairman at the $4.3 billion bank holding company and as a member of its executive committee until he retires in late 1996.
