Advanta Corp. said its Colonial National Bank USA unit is exploring selling as much as $150 million, or 4%, of its credit-card receivables.
Advanta said similar sales have resulted in premium transactions.
CS First Boston Inc. is advising the company on possible offers.
Advanta said any gain from a sale would be dedicated to additional marketing.
Advanta, a consumer financial-services company, had managed assets of $6.1 billion at Dec. 31.
