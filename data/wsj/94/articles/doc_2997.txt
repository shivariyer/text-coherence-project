The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended March 4, 1994.
The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive.
Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer.
CALIFORNIA POLLUTION CONTROL FINANCING AUTHORITY will redeem, on April 1, 1994, $180,000 principle amount of its 6 3/8% pollution control revenue bonds, 1977 series A, due April 1, 2007.
Bank of America National Trust & Savings Association, Glendale, Calif. is trustee.
