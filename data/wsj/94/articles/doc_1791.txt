The Treasury plans to raise a combined $4.58 billion in fresh cash next week with the sale of $17 billion of two-year notes on Tuesday and $11 billion of five-year notes on Wednesday.
The balance of the proceeds will be used to redeem $23.43 billion in maturing twoand five-year notes.
The new two-year notes will be dated March 31 and mature March 31, 1996; the new five-year notes will be dated March 31 and mature March 31, 1999.
The Cusip number for the two-year notes is 912827P30.
The Cusip number for the five-year notes is 912827P48.
Noncompetitive tenders for the twoyear notes, available in minimum denominations of $5,000, must be received by noon EST Tuesday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders must be received by 1 p.m. EST.
Noncompetitive tenders for the fiveyear notes, available in minimum denominations of $1,000, must be received by noon EST Wednesday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders must be received by 1 p.m. EST.
