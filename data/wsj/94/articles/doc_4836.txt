Burns Philp & Co. said it acquired Karl Ostmann GmbH, a German consumer herbs and spices concern, for about 182 million marks ($105.6 million) plus working capital.
The company said the acquisition will complement its existing spice businesses in the U.K., U.S. and Dutch markets.
The acquisition is subject to approval by the German Federal Cartel Office.
Ostmann has annual gross sales of 190 million marks.
