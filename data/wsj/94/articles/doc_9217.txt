Orbit International Corp. has completed the buyback of 250,000 common shares, or about 4% of the company's shares outstanding.
The repurchased shares were bought during the past three months at $4.56 to $5.30 each, the company said.
As a result of the buyback program, Orbit now has 6,350,000 shares outstanding.
The company, which makes electronic components as well as women's and children's apparel, said management may consider buying back additional shares from time to time in the future, depending on the price and other factors.
