Robert L. Swift was appointed president of the company's Rockwell Graphics Systems business, based in Westmont, Ill.
He succeeds Frank J. McKay, 48 years old, who left the company to return to Europe to pursue personal business interests.
Mr. Swift, 65, had been senior vice president of Rockwell Automation's Allen-Bradley Applied Systems Group.
Rockwell International is a diversified high-technology concern with interests in the electronics, aerospace, automotive and graphics businesses.
