Laser Video Network Inc. said it entered an agreement to provide its music-video programming to Marriott International Inc.'s Higher Education services subsidiary.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The company said it will provide the Marriott unit with its Laser Video college network, an interactive multimedia music-video entertainment system that shows music videos and commercials from sponsors such as Coca-Cola Co., MCI Communications Corp. and Chevrolet, a unit of General Motors Corp.
Marriott Higher Education operates university and college dining services.
