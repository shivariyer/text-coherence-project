Franklin D. Haftl, William L. Musser and Stephen L. Way were elected directors of this reinsurance company, increasing the board's size to five.
Mr. Haftl, 59 years old, is president, chief executive officer and a director of Unione Italiana Reinsurance Co. of America Inc.; Mr. Musser, 51, is a general partner of Barker, Lee & Co., an investment management firm; and Mr. Way, 44, is chairman, president and chief executive of HCC Insurance Holdings Inc.
