North American Technologies Group Inc. said one of its units received a credit line of $18 million from Euro Scotia Funding (Barbados) Ltd.
Under terms, North American Technologies Inc., the unit, will be entitled to draw as much as $500,000 a month.
The line will extend through Jan. 31, 1997.
Any amounts unpaid at that time will be repaid over a period of seven years at 8% interest a year.
In lieu of payment in cash, Euro Scotia may elect to convert unpaid amounts into shares of North American at the market price at the time of the election.
North American will use the funds for several purposes including working capital and the expansion of its environmental business.
