Michael Baker Corp. said its Intelcom Support Services Inc. unit was awarded a contract by the U.S. Navy to provide base operations support services at several sites in Hawaii.
The company said the contract, which is valued at $88 million, has a base year and four option years.
Each year of the contract is valued at about $17.7 million, Michael Baker said.
Under the contract, Intelcom will provide facility and utility maintenance, power plant operation, fire and emergency services, facility engineering, waste treatment, and other services related to transportation, food, supplies, fuels and security at the Navy's Pacific Missile Range Facility, Hawaiian Area Barking Sands, which has sites on the islands of Kauai, Oahu and Niihau.
Michael Baker is an engineering, construction, and operations and maintenance services concern in Pittsburgh.
