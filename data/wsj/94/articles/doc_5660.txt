Elizabeth J. Allan, manager of Japan Fund, was named lead manager of two more stock mutual funds advised by Scudder Stevens & Clark Inc.
Ms. Allan succeeds fund manager Andrew Economos, who left Scudder.
Ms. Allan will continue to be lead portfolio manager of the investment team running Japan Fund, which is managed by Scudder.
She will also take the lead manager role at Scudder Pacific Opportunities Fund, which invests in Pacific Rim countries other than Japan.
Scudder mutual funds are run by management teams, with the lead portfolio manager usually making the final decision on buying and selling stocks.
Ms. Allan will also lead the team running Scudder New Asia Fund, a "closed-end," or publicly traded, stock fund, which invests in Asian countries including Japan.
