Boatmen's Bancshares Inc., St. Louis, said its Boatmen's Trust unit agreed in principle to acquire Eagle Management & Trust Co., a Houston-based investment advisory firm currently owned by London-based Guinness Peat Group PLC.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Boatmen's, a bank holding company, said Eagle focuses on qualified retirement plans and endowments for pension and institutional clients; the Texas company has about $1.4 billion in assets under management.
Boatmen's Trust, which has about $34 billion in assets under management, said the acquisition is aimed at boosting its presence in Texas and will enhance its efforts to "explore opportunities in Mexico and Latin America."
