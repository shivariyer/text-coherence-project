GE Capital said its European fleet services business completed the acquisition of the vehicle financing business of Skandic Bilfinans in Stockholm for an undisclosed sum.
The business, which has 37,000 vehicles under lease and management, was acquired from Finans Skandis, a wholly owned subsidiary of Skandanaviska Enskilda Banken.
GE Capital, the financial services unit of General Electric Co., leases and manages more than 700,000 cars, trucks and specialty vehicles through its GE Capital Fleet Services business.
