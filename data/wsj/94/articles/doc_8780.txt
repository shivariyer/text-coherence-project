David P. Gruber, president and chief operating officer of this transportation and defense equipment maker, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer.
In his new position, Mr. Gruber, 52 years old, succeeds John M. Nelson, 62, who remains chairman.
Mr. Gruber's new appointment is effective in April following the annual meeting.
Before joining Wyman-Gordon as president and chief operating officer in October 1991, Mr. Gruber was vice president, Advanced Ceramics, at Compagnie de St. Gobain/Norton Co.
Worcester, Mass.
