Canadian primary steel production declined to 274,364 metric tons in the week ended March 19, a 4% drop from 285,697 tons the previous week and a 2% drop from 279,842 tons a year earlier, said Statistics Canada, a government agency.
A metric ton is equal to 2,204.62 pounds.
Year-to-date production totaled 2,865,784 tons, a 5.9% decline from 3,046,329 tons a year earlier.
