McCormick & Co. and A.V. Thomas Group Cos. of Madras, India, said they will form a joint venture company to produce whole and ground naturally sterilized spices.
The 50-50 partnership, to be based in Cochin, India, will be called AVT McCormick Ingredients Private Ltd.
McCormick, a producer of spices and specialty foods, posted $1.6 billion in sales for the fiscal year ended November 1993.
A.V. Thomas, a group of Indian agri-business companies, had about $70 million in sales for the year ended March 1993.
