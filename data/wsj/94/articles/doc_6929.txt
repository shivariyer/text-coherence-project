CRSS Inc. said it bought back a 19% stake in one of its units for $17 million.
Paribas North America Inc., a unit of the French Banque Paribas, had held the interest in CRSS's independent power subsidiary, CRSS Capital Inc., since June 1989.
CRSS now owns 100% of the unit.
CRSS, a design and construction company, said CRSS Capital contributed more than $17 million to CRSS's fiscal 1993 consolidated operating profit and is expected to contribute significantly to earnings in the current fiscal year, which ends June 30.
