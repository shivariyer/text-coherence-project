Litwin Engineers & Constructors, a unit of United Dominion Industries Ltd., said it was awarded a contract to supply engineering, design, procurement and construction services to Texaco Inc. in connection with the upgrading of its refinery in Colon, Panama.
A spokesman for United Dominion, a construction and engineering concern based in Charlotte, N.C., declined to disclose the value or terms of the multiyear contract.
Litwin said the total cost of the upgrading project is about $80 million.
