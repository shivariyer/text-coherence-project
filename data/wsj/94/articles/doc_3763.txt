General Electric Co.'s GE Industrial & Power Systems said it received a $39 million order for two gas turbine generators from Raytheon Co.'s Raytheon Constructors Inc.
GE said the 83.5-megawatt units will be used by Houston Lighting & Power Co. at a new cogeneration plant situated at a DuPont Co. chemical plant in LaPorte, Texas.
DuPont will buy steam produced by the plant, while Houston Lighting will use the electricity.
