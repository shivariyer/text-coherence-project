Ronald E. Elmquist, 47 years old, was named president, Global Food Service division, and a vice president of this food concern.
Mr. Elmquist previously was chairman and chief executive officer of White Swan Inc., also a food concern.
He fills new positions at Campbell and will be responsible for food-services businesses in the U.S. and Canada, as well as those of the Pepperidge Farm brand.
David W. Johnson, Campbell's president and chief executive officer, said Mr. Elmquist was chosen to lead an aggressive effort to increase the global division's business.
