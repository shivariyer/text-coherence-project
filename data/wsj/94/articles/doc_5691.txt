FEDERAL INDUSTRIES Ltd., Winnipeg, Manitoba, said it expects to raise 84 million Canadian dollars (US$62.5 million) from the sale to a group of underwriters of 10 million Class A common shares at C$8.40 (US$6.25) a share.
Federal, a transportation and metals distribution concern, has 40.9 million Class A shares outstanding.
It said about C$55 million of the proceeds from the sale will be used to redeem its 7 1/2% cumulative redeemable convertible Class II preferred shares, Series D. The balance will be added to working capital.
The underwriting group is led by RBC Dominion Securities Inc.
