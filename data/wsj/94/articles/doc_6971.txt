Glimcher Realty Trust said it acquired a commercial real-estate portfolio of 46 shopping centers from closely held Concord Assets Group of Boca Raton, Fla.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The acquisitions, which cover 18 states, include 33 community centers, 12 single-tenant retail properties and one regional shopping center.
Glimcher said it closed on the acquisition by using the proceeds from its Jan. 26 initial public offering.
Glimcher said it now owns a total of 75 properties in 20 states.
