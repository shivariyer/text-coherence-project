Canadian steel production rose to 285,697 metric tons for the week ended March 12, up 5% from 272,181 tons the previous week and up 3.4% from 276,275 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a government agency, said.
A metric ton is equal to 2,204.62 pounds.
For the year to date, production totaled 2,591,420 tons, a 6.3% decline from 2,766,487 tons a year earlier.
