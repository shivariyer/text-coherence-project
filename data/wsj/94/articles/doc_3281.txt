Joseph H. Reich, 59 years old, was named chairman of this portrait-studio company.
He succeeds Stanley Tulchin, 62, who remain a director.
Mr. Reich, a board member since 1987, is the managing director of Centennial Associates L.P. PCA also named Peter B. Foreman a director, increasing the size of the board to nine members.
Mr. Foreman is president of Sirius Corp.
