The following is a listing of securities called for partial or complete redemption during the week ended February 25, 1994.
The notices are taken from advertisements appearing in editions of The Wall Street Journal, and are not meant to be definitive.
Inquiries regarding specific issues should be directed to the paying agent or, if none is listed, the issuer.
ALEXANDRIA, VA., INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY will redeem, on March 31, 1994, its 6.125% pollution control revenue bonds, series 1977, due July 1, 2007.
NationsBank Trust, Atlanta, is trustee.
DELAWARE COUNTY, PA., INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY will redeem, on April 1, 1994, $110,000 principle amount of its 7.5% commercial development first mortgage revenue bonds, series A, 1977, due April 1, 1997.
Mellon Bank, Philadelphia, is trustee.
KAUKAUNA, WISC., will redeem, on April 1, 1994, $500,000 principle amount of its 6.5% industrial development revenue bonds, series B, due April 1, 1999.
Mellon Bank, Pittsburgh, is trustee.
MONTGOMERY COUNTY, MA., will redeem, on March 31, 1994, its pollution control revenue bonds, 1979 series.
NationsBank Trust, Atlanta, is trustee.
NORTHERN ILLINOIS UNIVERSITY BOARD OF REGENTS will redeem, on April 1, 1994, $1,055,000 principle amount of its 7.125% auxiliary facilities system revenue bonds, series A, due April 1, 2005.
Continental Bank, Chicago, is paying agent.
SOUTHEASTERN OKLAHOMA INDUSTRIES AUTHORITY will redeem, on April 1, 1994, $200,000 principle amount of its 5.625% pollution control facilities revenue bonds, series 1973, due April 1, 2003.
Continental Bank, Chicago, is trustee.
CINCINNATI GAS & ELECTRIC CO., will redeem, on April 1, 1994, its cumulative preferred stock, 9.28% series.
