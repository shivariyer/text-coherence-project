George W. Dougan, 54 years old, was named president and chief executive officer of this bank holding company, effective March 7.
Mr. Dougan will be responsible for overall strategic planning, management and performance of the holding company and its three subsidiary banks: First National Bank of Glens Falls, Evergreen Bank in Albany and Keeseville National Bank of Clinton County.
Mr. Dougan had been chairman of Bank of Boston-Florida since June 1992.
Mr. Dougan succeeds William L. Bitner III, who at 61 retired as president and chief executive in September 1993.
Mr. Bitner was succeeded as chairman in June by Edward F. Allard.
