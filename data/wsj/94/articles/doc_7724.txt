Switzerland's Nestle SA, the world's largest food group, said it has reached an agreement to acquire Foremost Foods Taiwan Ltd.
The purchase price wasn't disclosed.
Nestle said Foremost Foods Taiwan is the country's leading producer of ice cream and has major market positions in the liquid milk and yogurt market.
Foremost Foods employs 400 people and had 1993 sales of $45 million, Nestle said.
