Canadian Pacific Ltd. said it will issue 22 million common shares at 22.75 Canadian dollars (US$16.94) each, for total proceeds of C$500.5 million.
The issue, which isn't being offered in the U.S., is expected to close March 10.
The transportation and energy conglomerate said it will use net proceeds of about C$484 million to repay debt, finance additions to plant and property, and for general corporate purposes.
RBC Dominion Securities Inc. is lead underwriter.
Canadian Pacific currently has 319.3 million shares outstanding.
In composite New York Stock Exchange trading, Canadian Pacific closed at $16.75, down $1.
