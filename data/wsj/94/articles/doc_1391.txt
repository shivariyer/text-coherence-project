SIERRA ON-LINE Inc., Bellevue, Wash., said it plans to raise $50 million through the sale of convertible subordinated notes in a private placement to institutional investors.
The software publisher noted that it expects to report an operating loss for its fiscal fourth quarter, ending March 31.
Analysts expect the company to post a loss of between 14 cents and 39 cents a share.
A year earlier, Sierra had a deficit of $6.2 million, or 86 cents a share, on sales of $11.9 million.
