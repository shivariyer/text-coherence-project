EZ Communications Inc. said it purchased radio station KNCIFM, Sacramento, Calif., from Nationwide Communications Inc. of Columbus, Ohio, for $13 million.
The purchase gives EZ a second fullpower FM station in Sacramento, where it also owns KRAK-FM and KHTK-AM.
And EZ said it sold Sacramento station KQBRFM to Progressive Media Group Inc., Sacramento, for $2.5 million.
With the pending purchases, EZ will own 14 radio stations.
