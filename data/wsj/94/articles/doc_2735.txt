Computer Sciences Corp. said it received a four-year contract to provide engineering and integration services to the Marine Corps Systems Command at the Marine Corps Base in Quantico, Va.
The company said the contract is valued at $19.2 million if all options are exercised.
Computer Sciences said it will provide program management, integrated logistics, automated data processing and development and engineering services to support the acquisition of aviation commandand-control programs for the Marine Corps.
The company said work will be performed by the recently acquired professional services group.
