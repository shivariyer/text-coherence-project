Travis J. Bowden, 55 years old, was elected president of Gulf Power Co., a Pensacola, Fla.-based subsidiary of this utility holding company.
Mr. Bowden, executive vice president of Alabama Power Co., another Southern Co. subsidiary, succeeds Douglas L. McCrary, 64.
Mr. McCrary will remain chairman and chief executive officer of Gulf Power until May 1, when he retires and Mr. Bowden assumes the additional positions.
