Floris A. Maljers, 60 years old, was elected to the board of this oil and natural gas company, effective April 26.
Mr. Maljers is chairman of Unilever NV of Rotterdam, Netherlands.
His election will increase the number of members to 14, following a decrease to 13 on April 1, when Frederick S. Addy, 62, retires.
FLORIS A. MALJERS, chairman of Unilever NV, was nominated to join Amoco Corp.'s board.
The result of Amoco shareholders' vote on the nomination will be announced at the company's April 26 annual meeting.
Tuesday's Who's News column incorrectly stated that Mr. Maljers had already been elected.
