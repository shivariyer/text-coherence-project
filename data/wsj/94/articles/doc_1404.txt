David S. Pottruck, 45 years old, was named chief operating officer and a director of the discount brokerage company, increasing the board's size to 10.
Mr. Pottruck will continue as president of the company, and as president and chief executive officer of its brokerage subsidiary, Charles Schwab & Co.
In his new title, Mr. Pottruck succeeds Lawrence J. Stupski, who continues as vice chairman.
