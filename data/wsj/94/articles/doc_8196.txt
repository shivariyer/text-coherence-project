Keith R. McKennon, a director, was named chairman of this electric utility.
He succeeds Don C. Frisbee, 70 years old, who is retiring from the post to become chairman emeritus effective February 9.
Mr. McKennon, 60, recently retired as chief executive officer of Dow Corning Corp.
As reported earlier, Frederick W. Buckman was named president and chief executive officer, succeeding A.M. Gleason, 63, who was named vice chairman.
Mr. Buckman is former president and chief executive officer of Consumers Power Co., a subsidiary of CMS Energy Corp.
