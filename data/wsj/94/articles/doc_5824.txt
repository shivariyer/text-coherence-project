Egghead Inc. said its new emphasis on price-cutting reduced margins and earnings in the fiscal third quarter but pushed up retail sales on a comparable-store basis by 18%.
Its stock price tumbled 9.4%.
The Issaquah, Wash., computer-software concern, which has undergone management upheaval and other internal problems, reported earnings for the quarter ended Jan. 8 fell 39% to $1.4 million, or eight cents a share, from $2.3 million, or 14 cents a share, the year earlier.
Sales for the quarter rose 11% to $208.6 million from $188.3 million a year before.
Egghead's earnings were below analysts' quarterly mean estimates of 14 cents a share.
In Nasdaq Stock Market trading yesterday, the company's stock closed at $9.625 a share, down $1.
