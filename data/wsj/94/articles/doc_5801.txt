Century Telephone Enterprises Inc. said it completed its acquisition of Celutel Inc. for cash and stock.
Century paid $51.4 million in cash, plus 1.9 million Century common shares.
Century said the purchase price is equivalent to $3.699 cash plus 0.149 shares of Century common stock for each common share of Celutel.
Century said it will also assume $41.7 million in Celutel debt.
