TOTAL ENERGY SERVICES Co., Houston, said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an initial public offering of 3.4 million shares of common stock, plus 510,000 shares for overallotments.
A spokesman for the oilfield equipment and services company said the per-share price would be announced later.
He said proceeds would be used to pay $27 million of its $58 million in long-term debt, to purchase a 20% minority interest in a unit, and for general corporate purposes.
CS First Boston will manage the offering.
