BDM INTERNATIONAL Inc., McClean, Va., said it intends to make an initial public offering of Class A common shares in the first half of the year.
The technological-services company reported sales of $424 million in 1992.
It has about 6,000 employees and operates in more than 60 locations world-wide.
Earlier this year, BDM said it would buy a majority stake in a German defense firm and it had formed a joint venture with Electrosource Inc. to produce high-performance lead-acid batteries to power electric vehicles.
