Spain's producer price index rose 3.2% in November 1993 from a year earlier, the National Statistics Institute said.
Prices of consumer goods in November posted the biggest year-on-year increase, surging 4.3% from November 1992 and climbing 0.2% in November from October.
Other manufacturing industries showed a jump of 4.2% in November from a year earlier and climbed 0.4% in November from October.
