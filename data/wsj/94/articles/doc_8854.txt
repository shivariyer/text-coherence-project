Syncordia, a subsidiary of British Telecommunications PLC, said it won a multimillion-pound contract to provide services to Glaxo Holdings PLC, the international pharmaceuticals group based in London.
BT said Syncordia, which is its global outsourcing subsidiary, will supply managed private network services to connect sites in the United Kingdom, the U.S. and Canada.
The network will provide voice, data and video services.
The contract is initially for a two-year period and Glaxo will send commercial and scientific information between its various sites.
