Heilig-Meyers Co. said January sales rose about 45% to an estimated $50.4 million from $34.9 million a year ago, while same-store sales rose 8.4%.
For the eleven months ended in January, sales rose about 31% to an estimated $656.8 million from $502.4 million, while same-store sales rose about 12%.
Heilig-Meyers is a furniture retailer operating 571 stores in 22 states.
