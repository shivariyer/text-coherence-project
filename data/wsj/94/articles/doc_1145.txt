Premier Bancorp Inc. said it reached a definitive agreement to buy Red River Valley Bank of Bossier, La., for at least $25.8 million.
Under terms of the deal, which is subject to shareholder and regulatory approval, Red River shareholders will receive about 5.1 shares of Premier stock for each share of Red River.
Premier said it expects the value of the deal to range from $25.8 million to $28.5 million.
As of Dec. 31, Red River had total assets of $137.9 million and total equity of $13.9 million.
Seperately, Premier said it reached a definitive agreement to buy Heritage Financial Corp., Ruston, La.
