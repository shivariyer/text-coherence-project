Chiquita Brands International Inc., Cincinnati, said its John Morrell & Co. unit sold its Specialty Meat Group to SMG Inc.
Terms weren't disclosed.
SMG, a newly formed company, is headed by Peter K. Shea, formerly president and chief executive officer of Morrell.
SMG acquired the Liguria, Mosey's and Scott Petersen brands as well as the exclusive licensing rights to the Nathan's Famous trademark for retail packaged-meat products.
Chiquita, a marketer, processor and producer of food products, said three production facilities associated with the Specialty Meat Group were included in the sale.
