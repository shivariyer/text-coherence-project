Canadian primary steel production totaled 272,181 metric tons in the week ended Saturday, a 5% increase from 259,188 tons the previous week but a 7.5% decline from 294,320 tons a year earlier, Statistics Canada, a government agency, said.
A metric ton is equal to 2,204.6 pounds.
For the year to date, primary steel output totaled 2,305,723 tons, a 7.4% decline from 2,490,212 tons a year earlier.
