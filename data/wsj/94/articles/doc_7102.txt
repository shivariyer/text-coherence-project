American Honda Motor Co. said it plans to export 75,000 North American-made cars to more than 30 countries this year.
Last year, the auto maker, a unit of Honda Motor Co. of Japan, exported 43,328 cars to 28 countries.
Exports of the Accord reached 22,489 in 1993, while Civic exports rose to 19,205 vehicles last year.
Nearly 57% of Honda's 1993 exports were right-hand drive models for Japan and the U.K.
