SOUTHERN UNION Co., Austin, Texas, declared a 3-for-2 stock split in the form of a 50% stock dividend payable March 9 to holders of record Feb. 23.
The natural-gas distributor also said it will implement a 5% annual stock dividend, with payment coinciding with its May annual shareholders' meeting.
Southern said it will announce specifics of the dividend no later than the date of the shareholders' meeting.
