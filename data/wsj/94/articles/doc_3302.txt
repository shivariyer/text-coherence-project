Frank W. Ziska Jr. was named director of U.S.//Canadian operations for the Cushman & Wakefield/Royal LePage real-estate joint initiative, a new position.
The initiative was formed in 1993 to provide Canadian clients of Royal LePage and U.S. clients of Cushman & Wakefield with cross-border real-estate information and services.
Mr. Ziska, 46 years old, was most recently a director and branch manager at Cushman & Wakefield.
