The Treasury plans to raise $225 million in new cash with the sale Monday of about $25.2 billion in short-term bills to redeem $24.98 billion in bills that are maturing.
The offering will be divided evenly between 13-week bills (CUSIP 912794K94) maturing on June 9, 1994, and 26-week bills (CUSIP 912794N26) maturing Sept. 8, 1994.
Noncompetitive tenders must be received by noon EST Monday at the Treasury or at Federal Reserve banks or branches.
Competitive tenders must be received by 1 p.m. EST.
The minimum denomination is $10,000.
