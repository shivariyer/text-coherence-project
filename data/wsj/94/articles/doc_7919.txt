Thomas J. Hoeppner, executive vice president, operations and technology, at General Foods USA, was named a senior vice president of the Kraft General Foods unit of Philip Morris Cos. and president of customer support services.
Mr. Hoeppner, 52 years old, succeeds Edward W. Smeds, 57, who is retiring.
Richard E. Bailey, 47, executive vice president of operations at Kraft USA, will add the title of senior vice president, manufacturing, at Kraft General Foods.
