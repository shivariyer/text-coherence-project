Reynolds Metals Co., Richmond, Va., said it reached an agreement to sell Reynolds Australia Metals Ltd. for $116 million, plus the delivery of 30,000 ounces of gold over seven years.
Reynolds Australia, which holds a 40% stake in the Boddington Gold Mine in Western Australia, will be sold to Poseidon Gold Ltd. of Adelaide, Australia, a unit of Normandy Poseidon Ltd.
The transaction is expected to close March 15.
Reynolds Metals plans to use the sale's proceeds to fund part of its 1994 capital-expenditure plan, expand businesses and strengthen its financial position.
