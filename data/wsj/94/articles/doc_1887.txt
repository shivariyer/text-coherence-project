In a move that reinforces its commitment to distributing smaller acts, Sony Music Entertainment Inc. has purchased for an undisclosed sum the 50% of Relativity Entertainment Distribution Inc. that it didn't already own.
Relativity, Hollis, N.Y., specializes in distributing records that typically sell from 5,000 to 400,000 copies.
Also included in the sale is Relativity Records, which features performers such as Steve Vai, Peter Frampton, Joe Satriani, COC, Lucy's Fur Coat and The Beatnuts.
For the fiscal year ending March 31, Relativity is expected to have more than $65 million in sales.
Sony Music Entertainment is a worldwide unit of Sony Corp. that records and distributes music.
Its labels include Epic and Columbia.
