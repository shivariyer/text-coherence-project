Kerr-McGee Corp. said it set up a subsidiary to market natural gas effective March 1.
The parent company currently produces nearly 300 million cubic feet of natural gas a day from offshore blocks in the Gulf of Mexico and onshore fields in the U.S. and Canada.
The unit, to be called Kerr-McGee Natural Gas Inc., will offer sales, purchasing, dispatching, transportation, storage, contract administration, accounting and risk management.
