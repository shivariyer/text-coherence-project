Newmont Mining and Newmont Gold Co. said they named Lawrence T. Kurlander to the newly created position of senior vice president, administration, at both gold-mining companies.
Mr. Kurlander, 54 years old, had been senior vice president, public affairs and government affairs, at RJR Nabisco Inc.'s Nabisco International unit.
Newmont Mining owns 89.2% of Newmont Gold.
