Jack A. Lavine and James J. Gaffney were elected directors of this restaurant chain, filling two vacancies.
Mr. Lavine, 52 years old, now a consultant, is co-founder and former chief financial officer of Chili's Inc., a restaurant chain.
Mr. Gaffney, 53, is a consultant specializing in corporate performance improvement and turnaround management.
The vacancies were created by the resignations of Marne Obernauer Jr., 74, and the company's former president Paul M. Brockman, 46.
