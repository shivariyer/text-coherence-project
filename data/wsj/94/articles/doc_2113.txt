Lehman Brothers Inc. said it plans to establish an office in Bombay this year and named M.V. Sreedhar Menon a managing director responsible for business development and senior relationship management in India.
Mr. Menon, who will be based in New York, prior to joining Lehman Brothers was deputy president, a member of the board and head of the AEB Branch Network at American Express Bank.
He also was chairman of the board of American Express Bank International.
Lehman Brothers is a unit of American Express Co.
