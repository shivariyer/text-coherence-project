Medical Innovations Inc. said it signed a letter of intent to acquire privately owned PRN Home Health Care Inc. of Nevada for $3.5 million.
In a news release, Medical Innovations said it will pay $2 million of the total in cash, $750,000 in common stock and $750,000 as a note.
PRN had 1993 revenue of $12 million and pretax earnings of about $600,000.
