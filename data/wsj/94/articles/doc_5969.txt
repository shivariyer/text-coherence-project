CABLE-TV: America's Talking, an all-talk cable network from General Electric Co.'s NBC, will make its debut July 4.
The company declined to say how many households will have access to the channel, which will produce 14 hours of live talk shows daily.
WHO'S NEWS: The Village Voice said editor in chief Jonathan Larsen resigned but will stay at the alternative weekly newspaper until a successor is named.
Mr. Larsen, 54, said he needed a break after a stressful period.
The Voice is closely held by businessman Leonard Stern.
