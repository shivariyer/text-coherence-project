Nikko Securities Co.
International, the U.S. unit of Tokyo-based Nikko Securities Co., said Masao Ebina was named chairman and chief executive officer.
Mr. Ebina, 52 years old, previously general manager of the parent company's Kyoto and Sendai branches, will also serve as chief operating officer Nikko's Americas Group.
He succeeds Ryosuke Suzuki, 53, who will be a senior adviser of Tokyo Securities Co., a securities firm in which Nikko holds a minority stake.
Separately, Masumi Yamaguchi, 58, vice chairman of Nikko International since 1993, will become deputy chief operating officer of the parent's capital markets group in Tokyo.
