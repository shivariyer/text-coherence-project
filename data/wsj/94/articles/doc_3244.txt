Mesa Airlines said that February traffic rose 2% from February 1993.
The carrier reported 71.6 million revenue passenger miles for the latest month, up from 70.2 million.
A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.
Load factor, or percentage of available seats filled, rose to 50.1% from 48.7%.
For the year, traffic rose 0.7% to 142.6 million revenue passenger miles from 141.6 million in 1993.
Load factor also increased, to 47.9% from 47.8%.
