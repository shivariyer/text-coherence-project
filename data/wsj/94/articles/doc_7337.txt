John D. Crabb, 50-year-old president and chief operating officer, was named to the additional post of chief executive officer of this maker of recreational products.
He succeeds Terence S. Malone, 63, who retired as chairman and chief executive.
Samuel C. Johnson, 65, who was chairman before the October 1992 election of Mr. Malone, reassumes the post of chairman.
The post of chief operating officer has been eliminated.
The company also said Helen P. Johnson-Leipold, vice president, consumer marketing services-world-wide, of S.C. Johnson Wax, was elected a director of the company, succeeding H. Fisk Johnson, who didn't stand for re-election.
With the new director and Mr. Malone's retirement, there are now six members on the board.
