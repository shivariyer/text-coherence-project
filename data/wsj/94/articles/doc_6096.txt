METRICOM Inc., Los Gatos, Calif., a provider of wireless data-communications networks, said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for the proposed public offering of 2.2 million of its common shares.
Of the shares being offered, 2.1 million will be offered by the company with the remaining 100,000 shares being offered by certain selling shareholders.
The stock closed at $22.25 a share, up 50 cents, in Nasdaq Stock Market trading.
Lead underwriters of the offering are Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette Securities Corp. and Cowen & Co., Metricom said.
Metricom said it plans to use proceeds to expand its wireless data technology applications.
Currently, the company has 9.8 million common shares outstanding.
