Richard A. Bertagna, 59-year-old president of Hughes-Avicom International Inc., was elected chairman of that unit.
Lenard Stuessel, 58, succeeds Mr. Bertagna as president of the Glendora, Calif., company, which is involved in interactive passenger communications and entertainment systems for the commercial airline industry.
Mr. Stuessel previously was director, operations, with Hughes Missile Systems Co.
Both companies are units of GM Hughes Electronics, itself a GM unit.
