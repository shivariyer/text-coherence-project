Money market mutual fund assets slid $4.17 billion to $585.16 billion from a revised $589.33 billion in the week ended Wednesday, the Investment Company Institute said.
Assets of 654 retail money market mutual funds rose by $208.6 million to $402.3 billion, the trade group said.
Among retail funds, assets of 406 taxable money market funds increased by $437 million to $311.79 billion.
Assets of 248 retail tax-exempt funds decreased by $228.4 million to $90.51 billion.
Assets of the 283 institutional money market funds fell by $4.38 billion to $182.86 billion.
Among institutional funds, the assets of 229 taxable money market funds dropped by $4.45 billion to $160.01 billion.
Assets of 54 tax-exempt funds increased by $70.3 million to $22.86 billion.
