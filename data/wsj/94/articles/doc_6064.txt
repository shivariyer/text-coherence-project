ROCHESTER TELEPHONE Corp., Rochester, N.Y., plans to raise about $88 million through a public offering of 2.1 million shares at $42 a share, and to use the proceeds to expand its lines of business and for corporate uses.
The telephone company also said its largest shareholder, C FON Corp., a unit of Sprint Corp., will sell its entire holdings of 2.9 million shares in Rochester for $42 a share.
Salomon Brothers Inc. is the lead underwriter of the offerings.
