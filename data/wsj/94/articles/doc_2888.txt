This distributor and manufacturer of leather and leather-craft supplies said it added its first outside directors, increasing board membership to nine from five.
The four additional directors are: Richard Chase, chairman, president and chief executive officer of Fiebing Co.; Robert Herndon, chief financial officer of Pier 1 Imports Inc.; Luther Henderson, president of Pirvest Inc. and former founder and chairman of Pier 1; and Stephen King, vice president of Bombay Co.
