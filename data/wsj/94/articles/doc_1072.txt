Two executives were named senior vice president, deputy general counsel of this entertainment and communications concern: Earl H. Doppelt, a former senior vice president and deputy general counsel at Paramount Communications Inc., and Michael D. Fricklas, former vice president and deputy general counsel/corporate for Viacom.
They will share responsibility for day-today management of the company's law department, reporting to Philippe P. Dauman, executive vice president, general counsel and chief administrative officer.
