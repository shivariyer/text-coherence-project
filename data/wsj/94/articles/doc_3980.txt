NOEL GROUP Inc., New York, said it is distributing all its Series A common stock holding in Belding Heminway Co. as a form of dividend income to Noel shareholders of record Feb. 28.
A total of 3,542,404 shares of Belding Series A common stock, with a market value of 20 cents a share, will be distributed to Noel shareholders at a ratio of 0.175434 share for each Noel share owned.
Belding is New York maker of industrial threads and home-furnishing fabrics.
As reported, the distributed shares will be stapled to related Noel shares and will trade as a single unit until unstapling, expected later this year.
