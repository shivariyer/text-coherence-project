Computer Sciences Corp. said it won a five-year contract with Ford Motor Co.'s European parts-and-services operations.
Industry officials say the contract value is about $100 million.
Under the contract, Computer Sciences will oversee information-technology functions for that part of Ford, which serves customers of 3,000 dealers in 17 countries from its headquarters near Cologne, Germany.
The company will manage two primary Ford data centers in the United Kingdom and Germany, plus others in France, Spain, Italy and Sweden.
