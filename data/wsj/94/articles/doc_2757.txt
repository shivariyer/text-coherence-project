Good Guys Inc. said it expects fiscal second-quarter net income to surpass analysts' expectations.
The mean forecast for the period ending March 31 is 11 cents a share, according to Zacks Investment Research Inc.
A year earlier, the consumer electronics retailer had net income of $352,000, or three cents a share, on revenue of $116.4 million.
Robert A. Gunst, chief executive officer, said the company plans to open eight or nine new stores this year, all in Southern California.
