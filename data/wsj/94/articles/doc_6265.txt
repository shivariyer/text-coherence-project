Alaska Air Group Inc. reported higher traffic for its units, Alaska Airlines and Horizon Air.
In January, Alaska Airlines' revenue passenger miles increased 40% to 488.1 million, from 348.7 million a year earlier.
A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.
Load factor, or the percentage of seats filled, was 56.4% compared with 47.7% a year earlier.
Horizon Air flew 44.4 million revenue passenger miles in January, up 15% from 38.5 million flown during the period last year.
Load factor rose to 56%, from 52.5% a year earlier.
