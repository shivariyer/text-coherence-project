Olympus Capital Corp., parent company of Olympus Bank, said it received a "preliminary indication of interest" to be acquired by a financial institution through an exchange of stock.
Olympus said that, as of Friday, the valuation of the proposed exchange would be below the company's recent trading prices.
In Nasdaq Stock Market trading Friday, Olympus closed at $14.50 a share, down $1.25 .
Olympus and its adviser, Goldman, Sachs & Co., are pursuing discussions with the potential suitor, but the company said there isn't any assurance a transaction will result.
Olympus, Salt Lake City, has about 3.1 million shares outstanding.
Company officials didn't immediately return phone calls seeking additional comment.
