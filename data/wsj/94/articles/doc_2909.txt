Acordia Inc., Indianapolis, agreed to acquire Pettit-Morry Co., Seattle.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Computer Sciences Corp., El Segundo, Calif., was awarded a contract by the Naval Surface Warfare Center's Dahlgren Division to provide systems engineering and software support to the Aegis combat weapons program.
The contract was valued at $97.5 million over five years.
Surgical Care Affiliates Inc., Nashville, Tenn., acquired Surgicenter of Southeast Texas in Conroe, Texas.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Hicks, Muse & Co., Dallas, changed its name to Hicks, Muse, Tate & Furst Inc.
GAF Corp., Wayne, N.J., acquired International Permalite Inc., Ontario, Calif.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Morrison Knudsen Corp., Boise, Idaho, was awarded the management of a five-year environmental cleanup contract valued at $300 million with the U.S. Navy.
Morrison Knudsen's part of the pact is valued at $150 million to $180 million.
