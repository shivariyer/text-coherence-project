Philip C. Ackerman, 50 years old, senior vice president, was elected a director of this oil and natural gas utility holding company.
He fills a vacancy on the board created by the death of Sister M. Lawreace Antoun, who had served as a director since 1980.
National Fuel Gas now has eight board members.
Mr. Ackerman, whose term expires at the 1995 annual meeting, is also president of five of the company's units-Seneca Resources Corp., Empire Exploration Inc., Utility Constructors Inc., Highland Land & Minerals Inc. and Penn-York Energy Corp.
