Robert E. Allen, the 59-year-old chairman of American Telephone & Telegraph Co., was named to the board of this auto maker, expanding board membership from to 14.
Chrysler's chairman, Robert J. Eaton, said that AT&T has "gone through the kind of cultural changes that Chrysler is now working on" and that Mr. Allen will help guide Chrysler through its continuing "process of internal change and improved competitiveness."
Mr. Allen was elected a member of the board's finance and nominating committees.
He also sits on the board of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, PepsiCo Inc. and Bristol-Myers Squibb Co.
Separately, James D. Donlon III, 47, Chrysler's controller, was elected a vice president.
He will continue to serve as controller.
