This engineering, transportation and construction concern created an office of the chairman and named five executives to share the post: William Agee, 56 years old, chairman and chief executive officer; Gunnar Sarsten, 57, who will also become chairman and chief executive officer of MK International; Thomas Zarges, 45, president of the company's construction and engineering group; Stephen Hanks, 43, executive vice president, finance and administration; and Robert Tinstman, 47, president of the company's mining group.
