Atlantic Richfield Co. said its Arco Pipe Line Co. unit agreed in principle to sell certain assets to Citgo Petroleum Corp. and Williams Cos.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Atlantic Richfield, an oil and natural gas company, said the proposal includes the sale of 950 miles of refined-products pipeline in Texas and Oklahoma to Citgo, a Tulsa, Okla., subsidiary of Petroleos de Venezuela SA.
The proposed sale also includes 775 miles of pipeline in Kansas, Iowa and Illinois to Williams, an energy and telecommunications firm based in Tulsa.
