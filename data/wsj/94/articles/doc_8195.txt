Lawrence E. Tilton, 58 years old, formerly president of the Lederle consumer health division, was named president of this chemical concern's Lederle Laboratories division, succeeding Edward V. Fritzky, 43, who was named chairman and chief executive of 53.5%-owned Immunex Corp.
Gary S. Balkema, 38, formerly general manager of the Lederle consumer health products division, was named vice president of Lederle consumer health division.
