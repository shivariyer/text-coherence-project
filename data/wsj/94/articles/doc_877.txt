Chiquita Brands International Inc. said it will redeem all $42 million of its 10 1/4% subordinated debentures due Aug. 1, 2005.
The marketer, processor and producer of food products said in Cincinnati that the debentures will be redeemed April 25 at 100% of par value plus accrued interest through the redemption date for a total payment of $1,023.31 per $1,000 principal amount of debentures redeemed.
Securities Transfer Co., Cincinnati, is the redemption agent, Chiquita said.
Chiquita is also calling for redemption $45 million principal amount of its 10 1/2% subordinated debentures due Aug. 1, 2004.
The company currently has about $111 million of these debentures outstanding.
They will be redeemed April 28 at 100% of par value plus accrued interest through April 28, for a total payment of $1,024.74 per $1,000 principal amount.
The company said the 10 1/2% debentures to be redeemed will be selected by lot by the trustee, Star Bank, of Cincinnati.
Securities Transfer is the redemption agent.
