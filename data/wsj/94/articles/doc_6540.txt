Italian industrialist Carlo De Benedetti increased his family's stake in De Benedetti holding company Compagnia Finanziaria De Benedetti SpA, known as Cofide, to 42.62% from its previous 37.91%.
The De Benedetti stake was increased through the purchase of 20 million shares at 1,430 lire (84 cents U.S.) a share, Cofide said.
