Pittston Co. said it completed the acquisition of most of the coal-mining assets and coal-sales contracts of Addington Resources Inc.
When it reached a definitive agreement in September, Pittston said the $157 million acquisition would increase its Pittston Minerals Group's total coal production 50%.
Pittston said it intends to issue convertible preferred stock to help pay for the acquisition.
Pittston has businesses in coal, gold mining, security services and air freight.
Addington Resources is based in Ashland, Ky.
