Phillips Petroleum Co. unit Provesta Corp. sold assets of its flavor ingredients, aquaculture, and animal-feed operations to Burns Philp & Co.'s Integrated Ingredients unit.
Terms weren't disclosed.
The company in Bartlesville, Okla., said Provesta will continue to operate its fermentation plant under contract from Integrated Ingredients.
Integrated Ingredients will merge the businesses with its own operations, Phillips Petroleum said.
