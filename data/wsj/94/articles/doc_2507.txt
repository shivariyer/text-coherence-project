Paramount Publishing in New York, said it reached a co-publishing agreement with the Impress Group, a Japanese computer book and magazine publisher, to translate Prentice Hall Computer Publishing titles for the Japanese market.
Under the agreement, Paramount's Prentice Hall Japan unit and Impress will share development and production costs as well as profits for the translated titles.
Impress will be responsible for the translation, sales, marketing and distribution, while Paramount Publishing International will retain all copyrights.
The books will be published under the joint imprints Impress/Prentice Hall, MdN/Prentice Hall, Rittor Music/Prentice Hall, and IA Publishing/Prentice Hall.
Paramount Publishing is a division of Paramount Communications Inc., which is being acquired by Viacom Inc.
