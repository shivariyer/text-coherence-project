Asyst Technologies Inc. said it has been awarded a $17 million contract to deliver semiconductor production equipment over three years to Chartered Semiconductor, a chip maker in Singapore.
Asyst, a Milpitas, Calif., maker of equipment used in the manufacture of semiconductors, said the contract, plus other anticipated orders, should contribute to "strong" results in the second half of the fiscal year ending March 31, 1995.
Trading in Asyst shares was halted pending dissemination of the news.
