The following were among yesterday's offerings and pricings in U.S. and non-U.S. capital markets, with terms and syndicate manager, as compiled by Dow Jones Capital Markets Report:
Bear Stearn Finance LLC -- $150 million of exchangeable preferred income cumulative shares priced at par to yield 8%, according to MCM CorporateWatch.
The shares were priced at $25 each.
The shares, which are callable after five years, is debt exchangeable at the issuer's option into the preferred stock of the parent, Bear Stearns Cos., with the same terms.
Rated single-A-3 by Moody's Investors Service Inc. and triple-B by Standard & Poor's Corp., the issue will be sold through underwriters led by Bear, Stearns & Co.
