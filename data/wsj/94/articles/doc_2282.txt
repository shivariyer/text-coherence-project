USAir said its February traffic rose 1.7% to about 2.54 billion revenue passenger miles from 2.49 billion a year earlier.
A revenue passenger mile is one paying passenger flown one mile.
Load factor, or the number of available seats filled, climbed to 58.4% from 55.2%.
For the first two months of 1994, the USAir Group Inc. subsidiary said it flew 5.06 billion revenue passenger miles, up 1.1% from 5.01 billion a year earlier.
Load factor was 55.5%, up from 52.8%.
