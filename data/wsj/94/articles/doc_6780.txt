A partnership led by Jones Intercable Inc. said it bought four United Kingdom cable companies from PacTel Cable.
The terms weren't disclosed.
The companies are Peterborough Cablevision Ltd., Norwich Cablevision Holding Co., Broadland Cablevision Holding Co. and Fenland Cablevision Holding Co.
Together they represent about 225,000 franchise-licensed homes in the U.K.
PacTel Cable is a unit of Pacific Telesis Group.
