Triconex Corp. acquired Premier Engineering Inc., a Baton Rouge, La.-based engineering company, for $1.9 million.
In a statement, the industrial safety and control-systems company said Premier specializes in implementing safety-critical systems for the petrochemical industry.
Triconex said Premier had revenue of about $2 million in 1993.
The company expects Premier's revenue to grow "in excess of 40% in 1994."
