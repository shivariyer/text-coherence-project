Germany's Volkswagen AG said its 1993 sales in Brazil rose 40% from a year earlier to 389,723 vehicles, the highest level since 1980.
The car maker said it achieved a market share of 35.9% in Brazil.
Sales in Argentina climbed 28% to 46,495 vehicles in 1993, VW said.
Its market share rose to 11.7% from the previous year's 10.7%.
