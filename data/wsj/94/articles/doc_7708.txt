The cellular arm of telecommunications company U S West Inc. said it agreed to purchase equipment made by Motorola Inc. to replace and upgrade its cellular network in Arizona.
Terms were not disclosed.
The agreement lends momentum to Motorola's approach to all-digital cellular networks, called Code Division Multiple Access.
U S West has already used Motorola cellular equipment in revamping cellular systems in Denver, Seattle and Minneapolis.
Motorola is based in Schaumburg, Ill.
