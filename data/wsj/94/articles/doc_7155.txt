Richard F. Hawkins, 41 years old, president and chief operating officer was named to the additional post of chief executive officer, effective today.
As chief executive, he succeeds Robert F. Radican, 57, who continues as chairman.
The post of chief operating officer will be relinquished and won't be filled, Mr. Hawkins said.
The computer consulting services concern also said it will change its name to Network Six Inc., effective today.
The company also named James J. Trainor, 61, a director, expanding the board to six.
Mr. Trainor is a consultant working out of Washington, the company said.
