Input/Output Inc. said it discovered oil in Mississippi.
The seismic instrument company said its exploration and exploitation unit, Output Exploration Co., reported the King Ranch Oil & Gas Inc.
No. 1 Atchley 13-4 well flowed 1,150 barrels of oil a day from the Norphelt formation below 14,200 feet and 721 barrels of oil a day from the Smackover formation below 13,926 feet.
Input/Output said the well, three miles south of DeSoto, Miss., will be completed from both formations, and production will begin in March.
The company said its unit owns a 50% working interest in the well; the operator, King Ranch Oil & Gas Inc., owns a 40% working interest, and a private investor owns 10%.
