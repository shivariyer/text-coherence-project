Standard & Poor's Corp. said it will establish two new industry groups within its S&P 500 Index: photography/imaging and specialty papers, effective after the close of trading today.
Eastman Kodak Co. and Polaroid Corp. joined the photography/imaging group and Deluxe Corp., R.R. Donnelley & Sons Co. and John H. Harland Co. joined the specialty papers group.
The S&P 500 contains 89 industry groups.
All five companies previously had been classified as miscellaneous.
