Gupta Corp. in Menlo Park, Calif., said it earned $2.99 million, or 23 cents a share, in the fourth quarter, more than triple the year-ago level, as sales rose sharply.
Net income for the year-earlier period was $961,000, or nine cents a share.
Quarterly sales rose 64% to $18 million, from $11 million a year earlier.
The results exceeded analysts' expectations for earnings of about 19 cents a share and the fast-growing software firm's stock rose 11.5%, or $2.125, to $20.625 in trading on the Nasdaq Stock Market.
