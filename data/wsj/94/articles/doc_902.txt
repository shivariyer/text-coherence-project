Adia Services Inc., Menlo Park, Calif., said its largest shareholder, Adia SA, proposed to acquire all of Adia Services' common shares outstanding that it doesn't already own for stock and cash valued at a total of about $80 million.
Adia SA owns 10.2 million, or about 81%, of Adia Services' 12.6 million shares.
Both companies are personnel services companies.
Under proposed terms, eight shares of Adia Services would be exchanged for one share of Adia SA stock plus $112 in cash.
Adia Services said it appointed a special board committee to evaluate the proposal.
The proposal will be submitted to Adia Services shareholders for approval at the annual meeting, scheduled for July 14.
