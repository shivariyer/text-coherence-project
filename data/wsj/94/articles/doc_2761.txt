Integrated Labs Inc. reached an agreement to acquire Inland Plastics Inc. of Hicksville, N.Y., for a combination of cash, a note and the assumption of debt.
The value of the transaction was not disclosed.
The companies said Inland Plastics distributes thermoplastic materials, processes recycled materials and sells thermoplastic resins.
Integrated Labs is a plastics, manufacturing and environmental-technologies concern.
The companies said Inland Plastics will continue to operate independently as a majority-owned unit while doing business with Integrated Labs' other plastic-related businesses.
