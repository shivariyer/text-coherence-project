Crestar Financial Corp. authorized the repurchase of as many as 750,000 shares in the next 12 months.
The bank holding company completed a previous 500,000 share buyback authorization in 1993.
Crester's board also authorized in November a separate 450,000 share repurchase in conjunction with the pending acquisition of Annapolis Bancorp.
At the end of 1993, Crestar had 37.5 million shares outstanding.
