ICF Kaiser International Inc. said it sold substantially all the operating businesses of its Cygna Energy Services unit to General Physics Corp. of Columbia, Md.
Terms weren't disclosed.
Cygna in Oakland, Calif., and General Physics provide engineering and other services to the commercial nuclear-power industry.
ICF Kaiser, an engineering, construction and consulting services company, said it will keep the subsidiary's nonnuclear seismic-engineering business.
