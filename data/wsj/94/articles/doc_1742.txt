BELLWETHER EXPLORATION Co., Houston, said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission to sell 3,250,000 common shares.
Of that total, 2,250,000 would be sold by the company and 1,000,000 by certain shareholders.
A Bellwether official said the company would have about six million common shares outstanding after a pending 1-for-8 reverse stock split and the offering.
The energy company said it would use proceeds from the offering to repay debt and to fund possible acquisitions.
Managing underwriter for the offering is Principal/Eppler, Guerin & Turner Inc.
