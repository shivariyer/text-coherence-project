Global Natural Resources Inc. said it successfully completed a natural-gas well in the Gulf of Mexico offshore Texas.
Global said its Well No. 2 on High Island Block 23-L tested at five million cubic feet of gas and 173 barrels of condensate a day from depths of 10,086 to 10,173 feet.
The oil and gas company said the well will begin production in the second quarter.
Global has a 50% working interest in the well, which it operates.
Closely held HallHouston Oil Co. holds a 20% working interest, Apache Corp. holds 20% and Edisto Exploration & Production Co., a unit of Edisto Resources Corp., holds 10%.
