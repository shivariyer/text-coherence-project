VARIAN ASSOCIATES Inc., Santa Clara, Calif., said its board approved a 2-for-1 stock split and a 20% increase in the company's cash dividend.
Varian said the board also voted to double the company's current stock buy-back program to four million shares.
The stock is being purchased on the open market.
The maker of high-tech parts for the medical, communications and other industries said it will issue its new shares March 17 to stock of record March 3.
Varian currently has about 17.2 million shares outstanding.
Varian said it will also increase its quarterly cash dividend to six cents from five cents, payable on a post-split basis May 2 to stock of record April 22.
