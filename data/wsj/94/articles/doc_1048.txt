Officials Swedish pharmaceutical group AB Astra said its best-selling product, antiulcer agent Losec, had been approved by authorities in the United Kingdom for use with the antibiotic amoxycillin for treatment of duodenal ulcers associated with the bacteria Helicobater pylori.
Astra also said it has filed an application for registration of its antiasthma dry powder Pulmicort Turbuhaler with the U.S. Food and Drug Administration.
