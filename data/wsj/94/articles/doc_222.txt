Comerica Inc., a bank holding company, completed the acquisition of Pacific Western Bancshares, San Jose, Calif., for about $124 million in stock.
Procter & Gamble Co. acquired Argentinian detergent and bar soap maker, Alejandro Llauro e Hijos SAIC.
