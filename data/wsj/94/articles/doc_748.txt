S. Jay Stewart was named chairman and chief executive officer, effective April 1, at this maker of specialty chemicals, airbags for autos, and salt.
Mr. Stewart, 55 years old, is currently president and chief operating officer.
He will succeed Charles S. Locke, 65, who will retire.
Mr. Stewart will retain the post of president; the post of chief operating officer won't be immediately filled, a spokeswoman said.
