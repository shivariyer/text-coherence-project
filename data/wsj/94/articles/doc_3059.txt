D. Harrison Gentry, formerly director of real estate development for Phelps Dodge Corp., was named to the new posts of president and chief operating officer of this pollution-control company.
Mr. Gentry, 53 years old, was also elected a director of Alanco.
Separately, the company said Harold S. "Bud" Carpenter was also named a director.
Mr. Carpenter, 60, was formerly chairman of George A. Rolfes Co., which is now an Alanco unit.
The election of Messrs. Gentry and Carpenter as directors brings membership of the board to six.
