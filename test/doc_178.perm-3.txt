A.O. Smith, which makes car, truck and farm equipment, said in light of its continuing earnings improvement, management plans to recommend that the board increase the quarterly dividend to 13 cents from 11 cents a share, effective with the May payment.

In the year-earlier quarter, the company earned a record $13 million, or 64 cents a share.

Smith said its appears that three of its largest operating units will report higher sales and profits.

Sales for the quarter are expected to rise as much as 15% to $335 million to $340 million, compared with $296 million a year earlier.

The Milwaukee company said it continues to benefit from the improving domestic economy.

A.O. Smith Corp. said its earnings for the first quarter are expected to be $15 million to $16 million, or 71 cents to 76 cents a share.
