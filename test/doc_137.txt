Bell Canada International Inc., Jones Intercable Inc., Jones Global Group Inc. and Cable & Wireless PLC said they signed a letter of intent to consolidate their cable television and associated telephone operations in the United Kingdom.

The transaction will combine the cable interests of the companies into BCETI Cable Ltd., currently 80% owned by Bell Canada International and 20% by Cable & Wireless.

The move will coincide with a public share offering of BCETI cable.

Financial terms weren't disclosed.

Bell Canada International is a unit of BCE Inc., Montreal.

Cable & Wireless is an international telecommunications concern.

Jones Global is a U.K. cable operator and a subsidiary of Jones Intercable.

Bell Canada International has signed a letter of intent to buy 30% of Jones Intercable, a Denver-based company.
