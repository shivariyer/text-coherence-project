Gary E. Kleinjan, 45 years old, was named president and general manager of this plastic and rubber houseware maker's Rubbermaid Office Products Inc. unit.

Mr. Kleinjan was vice president and general manager of MicroComputer Accessories Inc., a unit of Rubbermaid Office Products.

The company hasn't decided if it will name a successor.

He succeeds John L. Mariotti, who resigned last week to pursue other interests, the company said.
